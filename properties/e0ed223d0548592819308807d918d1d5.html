Stanley Bank, Comrie Road,
Crieff PH7 4BQ

Offers Over £280,000

Semi-Detached House

4 Bedrooms

We are delighted to offer for sale this stunning period semi-detached villa, located in an established and sought after residential area within the popular market town of Crieff. Boasting an elevated position with a fine southerly aspect. An enviable location, being within easy walking distance of the town centre, Morrisons Academy and Crieff Hydro 
Spa Hotel. Stanley Bank is a beautiful and substantial home presented in true move-in condition, offering considerable period charm. The spacious and versatile accommodation is set principally over 3 floors & comprises; ENTRANCE HALLWAY leading to Ground floor accommodation comprising; Large bright LIVING ROOM/Bed 4 with open fire & beautiful views to the surrounding countryside beyond, fully fitted DINING KITCHEN with gas ‘Redfyre Range Cooker’, electric oven & American style fridge/freezer. Upper Level comprising; FAMILY BATHROOM with electric shower over bath, Two DOUBLE BEDROOMS (fitted wardrobes in one). Lower Level comprising; Double BEDROOM with built in wardrobes, SHOWER ROOM with under floor heating, SITTING ROOM/FAMILY ROOM with multi-fuel stove, under-stair storage, UTILITY ROOM with door leading to rear garden area. A notable feature of the property is the private garden to the rear which has been well maintained & landscaped by the current owners. The gardens are south facing, boasting a brick patio/bbq area, leading to an area of decking which is an ideal entertaining area, with ample space for a table and chairs. Steps lead down to a stone chipped drying area with log shed. Lots of mature planting , shrubs & flower beds. The garden is fully enclosed with boundaries being formed by stone walls & timber fencing. Gated access to the rear leads to a further decked area with storage shed with steps down to a private parking area for 2 cars. Stanley Bank is warmed by gas central heating (new combi boiler 2024). A beautiful period home with spacious and versatile accommodation, located in a prime location.  Early viewing is advised and strictly by appointment only through Irving Geddes W.S.
