A very well-proportioned ground floor, maindoor four-bedroom flat offering versatile period accommodation close to an excellent range of local amenities.

Generously Proportioned Maindoor Ground Floor Flat, Attractive Period Building, Entrance Hall, Living Room, Dining Room, Kitchen, Utility Room, Four Large Double Bedrooms, Bathroom, Study, Walk-In Storage Cupboard, Yard to Rear, Private Front Garden, Double/Secondary Glazing Throughout, Gas Central Heating.

The property is conveniently situated on Gorgie Road, in the west of the city, close to the vibrant local amenities of Haymarket and Dalry Road. Many essentials can be found very close by including shops, restaurants, coffee shops, and Sainsbury's. At nearby Chesser there is an Aldi and an Asda as well as coffee shops and both entertainment and recreation facilities. Edinburgh city centre is within easy reach with the world-famous shops of Princes Street, and the financial district and West End also nearby. Fountainpark Leisure Complex is also within reach and offers a range of entertainment options including a multiplex cinema, bowling alley and various eateries. And the famous Murrayfield and Tynecastle stadia are also easily accessible for sports fans. Balgreen Tram Stop is approximately 0.5 miles away and for the motorist, the A71 offers rapid access to The City Bypass, M8, M9 and Queensferry Crossing.
