47 Allanton Avenue,
Paisley PA1 3BN

Fixed Price £355,000

Semi-Detached Bungalow

3 Bedrooms

47 Allanton Avenue is a stunning three-bedroom semi-detached bungalow situated in one of Paisley's most desirable residential areas. Immaculately maintained and thoughtfully upgraded, this charming home is truly in walk-in condition, offering a perfect blend of traditional character and modern convenience. Step into the welcoming entrance vestibule, which leads into a bright and spacious reception hallway. The beautifully restored original wooden floorboards immediately impress, continuing seamlessly into the elegant sitting and dining room. This versatile space is ideal for entertaining or relaxed family living, complete with French doors opening onto a raised deck-perfect for al fresco dining. The stylish, modern kitchen has been fitted with quality storage units, solid surface worktops, and a range of integrated appliances, including a washing machine, dishwasher, and fridge/freezer. There's also space for a range cooker and a wine fridge, and the semi open-plan layout creates a sociable flow between the kitchen and dining area. A rear door provides easy access to the landscaped garden. To the front of the home, the formal lounge features a bay window that floods the space with natural light and a gas fire that adds a cosy focal point. Also on the ground level is the generous primary bedroom and a sleek, contemporary shower room with a glass-screened walk-in shower. Upstairs, you'll find two further well-proportioned double bedrooms and a second, stylish shower room-ideal for guests or family living. Outside, the home boasts beautifully maintained gardens to the front and rear, with lawns, mature planting, and a private deck and patio area perfect for relaxing or entertaining. A private driveway to the side provides off-street parking. The detached garage gas been converted into a private gym with double glazing and aspects to the garden from the floor to ceiling windows and glass door. A Lifestyle Location - Discover Ralston and Paisley Ralston is one of Paisley's most sought-after residential areas, known for its peaceful surroundings, well-regarded local schools, and strong sense of community. Just minutes from Barshaw Park and Ralston Golf Course, it's ideal for outdoor enthusiasts and families alike. Paisley itself offers a vibrant mix of cafés, restaurants, shops, and cultural attractions, including the stunning Paisley Abbey and the town's thriving art scene. Excellent transport links make commuting easy, with regular trains to Glasgow, quick access to the M8 motorway, and Glasgow Airport just a short drive away perfect for professionals and travellers.
