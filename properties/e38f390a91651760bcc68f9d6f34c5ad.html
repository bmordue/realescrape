Offers over £320,000

Detached Bungalow

3 Bedrooms

Offering 1242sq ft of flexible accommodation, this is a fantastic opportunity to purchase this rarely available three-bedroom detached bungalow on a large plot which is presented to the market in move in condition. The bright, well-proportioned accommodation comprises:

*Entrance Vestibule leading to hallway
*Dual aspect living/ dining room with feature fireplace
*Kitchen fitted with a range of base and wall mounted units
*Family room/ sunroom with access to the rear garden
*Three bedrooms
*Family bathroom
*Large well-established gardens with single garage and driveway
*Gas central heating and double glazing

Kinghorn benefits from local amenities on the high street including a variety of shops, doctors, dentist, primary school, train station providing a direct route to Edinburgh. A short distance is Kirkcaldy which has the Fife retail park and Town centre which offer many restaurants and bars, theatre, museum, beaches and leisure centres to name a few.

The neighbouring coastal towns of Burntisland and Aberdour are a short drive off, along with fabulous beaches and leisure facilities at Pettycur Bay and the Beacon Leisure Centre. Kinghorn boasts its own 18 hole golf course and is the perfect setting for costal walks and a quieter pace of life.
