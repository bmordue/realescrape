End Terraced House

3 Bedrooms

Warners are proud to present this beautifully presented three-bedroom end-terrace home, located in a sought-after modern development Sighthill, just west of Edinburgh city centre. Upon entry, you're greeted by a bright and welcoming living room to the right, featuring a stunning floor-to-ceiling window that also serves as a door, filling the space with natural light and creating an airy, inviting atmosphere. To the rear of the home lies the heart of the property: a spacious open-plan kitchen and dining area, perfect for everyday family living and entertaining guests. The kitchen is fully fitted with a gas hob, oven, washer/dryer, dishwasher, fridge/freezer, and offers generous worktop and cupboard space. A convenient cloakroom and a storage cupboard complete the ground floor. Upstairs, the home offers two well-proportioned double bedrooms and a comfortable single bedroom. The principal bedroom features a sleek three-piece en-suite, built-in wardrobe, and an additional storage area. The second double bedroom also includes built-in wardrobe space, and all are served by a modern three-piece family bathroom. Externally, the property benefits from a private driveway with parking for two vehicles and a spacious, west-facing rear garden, ideal for relaxing or outdoor entertaining. The addition of solar panels further enhances the home's energy efficiency, making it as practical as it is stylish.
