Offers over £495,000

Detached House

4 Bedrooms

Set within one of Bridge of Weir's most desirable addresses, this impressive detached villa occupies a prime position on the ever-popular Watt Road-a charming and established street known for its blend of modern, period and mid-century homes. Perfectly placed for those who appreciate an active lifestyle, the property is just moments from the renowned Old Course Ranfurly Golf Club, while the village centre, with its welcoming cafés, shops and community feel, is only a short stroll away. Bridge of Weir itself offers the best of village living-peaceful surroundings, excellent schooling, and a strong sense of community-all within easy reach of Glasgow, making it an ideal setting for families and commuters alike. The home provides generous and highly versatile accommodation, thoughtfully designed to suit modern family life. On the ground floor, the principal living room is a wonderfully bright and airy space, enjoying dual aspects to the front and rear. A spacious dining room features beautiful exposed timber flooring, rich in character and history, having been reclaimed from traditional Glasgow tenements. To the rear, a cosy garden room offers a relaxing retreat, complete with French doors opening directly onto the garden-perfect for indoor-outdoor living. The kitchen is well-appointed and cleverly arranged across two sections, offering excellent preparation space along with integrated appliances including a cooker, dishwasher and fridge, plus space for an under-counter freezer. A separate utility room provides additional practicality, with access to both the rear garden and the garage. A convenient WC completes the ground floor. There is also a flexible office space which is suitable for home workers. Upstairs, there are four well-proportioned bedrooms and a stylish family bathroom. The principal bedroom benefits from double fitted wardrobes and a private en suite shower room, while bedroom two enjoys a Jack and Jill en suite arrangement. The family bathroom is beautifully presented, featuring a four-piece suite with both bath and separate shower. Externally, the property continues to impress. A double garage with electric door is complemented by a gated, stone-chipped driveway providing ample parking. To the rear lies a truly outstanding garden-an idyllic, private haven boasting a tranquil pond, mature planting, established trees and a beautifully maintained lawn. It's a space designed to be enjoyed year-round, whether entertaining, relaxing, or simply soaking in the peaceful surroundings. This is a rare opportunity to acquire a superb family home in one of Bridge of Weir's finest locations-offering space, charm and an exceptional lifestyle in equal measure.
