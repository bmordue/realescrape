Detached Bungalow

2 Bedrooms

The property is located in Copland Street, within easy, level walking distance of the towns amenities. Dalbeattie offers primary and secondary schooling, shops, local food stores, health centre, churches and a golf course. Walkers and mountain bikers are also well catered for in the region with the town woods just minutes away and the 7Stanes cycle tracks on hand in the Dalbeattie forest, less than a mile away. The "Granite Town" of Dalbeattie is the gateway to The Solway Coast and is 4 miles from the sailing village of Kippford, with the sandy beaches of Rockcliffe & Sandyhills just a few more minutes away.

Charming two bedroom cottage with large rear garden, off street parking and single garage. The property enjoys light filled rooms with the open plan lounge diner enjoying aspects to the front, side and rear. There is double glazing, electric storage heating and a feature wood burning stove in the lounge. The property would benefit from some modernisation, however does feature a modern family bathroom.
