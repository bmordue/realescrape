Rosemount Cottage, 3A Wellington Road,
Hawick TD9 7DX

Offers In The Region Of £200,000

Detached House

3 Bedrooms

Enjoying a central position within the town, Rosemount Cottage is a three bedroom detached dwelling set in an elevated position with a stunning open outlook. Having recently undergone an extensive programme of renovation, the property is now presented for sale in in truly immaculate condition. The property would suit a wide variety of prospective buyers and viewing is considered essential to fully appreciate this charming home. Internally, the front door opens up to the entrance hallway, with the main sitting room on the left - a welcoming space, bursting with character and warmth - the multi fuel stove being the focal point. Adjacent to the living room is the dining room, which flows directly to the beautiful kitchen which benefits from a range of integrated appliances as well as direct access to the rear garden. Moving upstairs, there are two double bedrooms, both of which have built in storage - the third bedroom is smaller in size, and would lend itself perfectly as a home office, which it is currently utilised as. The family bathroom has been tastefully finished, with the shower over bath, WC and wash hand basin set with a vanity unit. Access to the fully floored loft space is via the Ramsay ladder at first floor level. Externally, Rosemount Cottage has a lovely decked area to the front of the home, perfect for al-fresco dining & enjoying the warmer weather. To the rear, accessed directly from the kitchen, there is a terraced area complete with a timber shed, and access is available directly to Langlands Road.
