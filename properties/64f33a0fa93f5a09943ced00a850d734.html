Offers over £650,000

Terraced House

4 Bedrooms

Beautifully presented, traditional mid-terraced house in Musselburgh, offering two reception rooms, a semi open-plan breakfasting kitchen and dining room, four bedrooms, an en-suite shower room, a bathroom, and a separate WC, plus a one-bedroom studio, gardens, and access to unrestricted on-street parking. Representing a stylish, yet characterful family home, this four-bedroom, two-bathroom traditional mid-terraced house has spacious, sociable living areas and is situated in Musselburgh. It comes accompanied by several garden spaces, a one-bedroom studio (perfect for homeworking, creatives, and mutigenerational living), and unrestricted on-street parking. Musselburgh is located on the picturesque East Lothian coast, enjoying easy access to the beach and a wealth of green space, as well as offering excellent everyday amenities such as a wide range of shops, schools at primary and secondary levels, including a well-regarded independent school, and transport links, including a train station with fast and regular services to Edinburgh. A practical entrance vestibule, with a traditional tiled floor and space for coat and shoe storage, welcomes you into the home and leads through to a hall, where the first glimpse of the stylish, yet characterful interiors is given with neutral décor, rich wood-styled flooring, and decorative plasterwork. A further internal hall houses a useful WC and storage. The characterful living room is attractively presented with deep purple décor, rich wood-styled flooring, and traditional features including a panelled window surround, cornicing, and a ceiling rose. A south-facing bay window allows sunny light in throughout the day, whilst a log-burning stove nestled under a wood mantel creates a warm atmosphere and a focal point around which furniture can be arranged. The good-sized family room also has a log-burning stove and offers plenty of space for furniture, as well as giving the new owner excellent flexibility and options for use, and it has a door leading out to the courtyard garden. The kitchen is very well-appointed with a wide range of attractive, modern navy-blue cabinets, quartz worktops, a Belfast sink, and splashbacks, as well as a selection of integrated appliances. A breakfast bar caters for morning coffee, casual meals, and socialising while cooking, and there is also a snug area, perfect for a cosy lounge setup, set next to wide patio doors opening onto the courtyard. The kitchen is supplemented by a fantastic walk-in pantry and a utility room, and it is openly connected to the dining room, sure to appeal to those who love to host dinner parties and ideal for family dining. The bedrooms are found on the first and second floors, approached via staircases and landings with built-in storage. The peaceful sleeping areas are all tastefully decorated and carpeted for optimum comfort underfoot, and one can alternatively be utilised as a home office. The principal bedroom occupies an impressive footprint that spans the entire width of the property, including a spacious sleeping area, a large walk-in wardrobe/dressing room, and an en-suite shower room. The principal bedroom's en-suite comprises a shower enclosure with stylish tiling, a basin atop a storage unit, a WC, and a chrome towel radiator. Finally, an airy family bathroom on the ground-floor completes the accommodation on offer, replete with a corner bathtub, a shower cubicle, and a WC-suite. The house is accompanied by a wealth of outdoor space, including an internal courtyard between the family room and kitchen - perfect for alfresco dining and summer barbecues! There is also a main garden to the rear of the property with a patio area, a lawn, and colourful planting, and a secondary garden just outside the front door to the studio. Unrestricted on-street parking can be found to the front of the house on Millhill. The house comes with the unique feature of a one-bedroom detached studio in the garden, with an open-plan kitchen and living area, a mezzanine level bedroom, and a shower room. The studio could lend itself to a variety of uses by the new owner, including a separate space for live-in relatives or older children, a workspace for creatives and those who work or run businesses from home, or as a rental/Airbnb property (subject to the correct permissions) to generate additional income.
