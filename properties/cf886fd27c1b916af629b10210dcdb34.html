2 Flat B Meldrum Court,
Kirkcaldy KY2 5LP

Offers over £140,000

Ground Floor Flat

3 Bedrooms

This beautifully presented and generously proportioned three-bedroom ground-floor flat forms part of a popular modern development, offering an exceptional level of space, style and convenience in a sought-after residential location. Designed for contemporary living, the property enjoys a bright and airy ambience throughout, with well-planned accommodation ideally suited to first-time buyers, professionals or those seeking an easily maintained home on one level. A welcoming hallway with a large utility cupboard sets the tone for the quality within. The spacious living/dining room is a standout feature, flooded with natural light from an attractive box window with Juliette balcony, creating a wonderful setting for everyday relaxation and entertaining. A separate contemporary kitchen provides further appeal, fitted with sleek units and excellent worktop space There are three generous double bedrooms, all beautifully presented. The principal bedroom benefits from integrated wardrobes and a stylish en-suite bathroom, while a second double also features built-in storage. A modern family bathroom completes the accommodation. The property further benefits from gas central heating and double glazing, ensuring year-round comfort. Externally, the development is well maintained, with landscaped communal grounds, allocated parking and a secure entry system. Offering an impressive combination of size, modern finishes and a superb ground-floor position, this outstanding flat is ideally placed for a range of local amenities, transport links and green spaces, making it an excellent opportunity not to be missed. Factoring: Taylor & Martin: Approx. £250 deposit & £175 P/Q.
