2 Solway View, Preston Mill, Kirkbean,
Dumfries DG2 8AE

Offers over £170,000

Semi-Detached House

3 Bedrooms

2 Solway view is located in the small hamlet of Prestonmill, just outside Kirkbean along the A710 Coast Road approximately 12 miles from Dalbeattie and approximately 13 miles from Dumfries, Prestonmill is close to many amenities both towns have to offer such as primary and secondary schools, cafes, shops, restaurants and hotels, health centres and hospitals, golf courses, leisure facilities and churches. With views over the Solway firth, the property is close to the coast including Southerness, Carsethorn and RSPB Mersehead as well as the historic village of New Abbey.

Well-presented 3 bedroom semi-detached property on an elevated position with views over the countryside, Solway Firth and Cumbria on a clear day. The property enjoys well-proportioned light filled rooms with countryside views from every aspect. It is in excellent decorative order throughout with a modern fitted kitchen, bathroom and shower room. There is UPVC double glazing, oil central heating and a solid fuel stove in the dining lounge.

A bright entrance porch opens into the hallway. The hall gives access to the dining lounge on the right, the kitchen straight ahead and the shower room and stairs to the first floor on the left. Upstairs, there are three bedroom served by a family bathroom.

Outside the front and side garden is largely laid to lawn with a paved seating are. The gated rear garden is also laid to lawn with small trees and two wooden garden sheds.
