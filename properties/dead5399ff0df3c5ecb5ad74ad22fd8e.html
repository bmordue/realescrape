Offers over £130,000

Semi-Detached House

4 Bedrooms

This property is located in the small village of Kirkton on the outskirts of Heathhall, Dumfries, approximately 4 miles from the town centre. Kirkton features a Village Hall, Church and a Post Office which operates two days a week, Kirkton is on a regular bus route to both Dumfries town centre and Thornhill. A few miles distant is Dalswinton Estate and village hall offering pleasant countryside walks.

Four bedroom semi-detached property with front and rear gardens and pleasing countryside views in a peaceful village location. The property enjoys well-proportioned light filled rooms with the lounge having a dual aspect to the front and rear. There is UPVC double glazing and solid fuel central heating throughout with a feature solid fuel stove in the lounge.
The entrance hallway provides access to the spacious lounge, which opens into the kitchen, providing direct access to the rear garden. The entrance hallway also gives access to the stairs to the first floor. Upstairs there are 4 bedrooms served by a family bathroom.
Outside there are garden grounds to the front and rear, with the rear enjoying countryside views.
