34 Craigs Crescent,
Edinburgh EH12 8HT

Offers over £650,000

Detached Bungalow

4 Bedrooms

34 Craigs Crescent is an outstanding family home located in the sought after Corstorphine area of Edinburgh and occupying a generous plot. Offering exceptional accommodation over two floors, the house has been extended to the rear to create a stylish family dining kitchen room, an entertainers dream. With gas central heating and full double glazing the house has fresh neutral decoration and would make a lovely family home.

The accommodation includes an entrance vestibule which leads to the bright and spacious hallway with walk-in cupboard and further under stairs cupboard. The lounge is a lovely room with bay window to front and wall mounted electric fire. The true hub of this home is undoubtedly the dining and family kitchen which is a large room full of natural light and of excellent proportions. With two windows to the rear and bi-folding doors which fully open out to a raised decking area. The kitchen area is fitted with a range of modern base and wall units with oven, hob, hood, fridge/freezer and dishwasher. Located off the kitchen is a utility room with base and wall units, door to side and deep storage cupboard. There are two double bedrooms on the ground floor and a family bathroom with three piece white suite with shower over the bath. Upstairs there is a useful WC accessed off the landing and the master bedroom is a large room with fitted wardrobe, window to rear, eaves storage and an en suite shower room with window. There is a further double bedroom with the front with excellent eaves storage.

Externally the property enjoys gardens to the front, and rear which are mainly laid to lawn, the latter being fully enclosed. There is a large decking area to the rear leading to a lawned garden with shed. There is a driveway to the front providing off street parking.

Early viewing of this wonderful home is essential to fully appreciate the ideal location and true extent of the accommodation offered.

Corstorphine is a thriving local community, around four miles west of Edinburgh city centre. Here you'll find a diverse range of property types, from traditional villas and bungalows to smart new-build flats and family homes. The area's main road is one of the city's major arteries, providing an efficient route in and out of the city, and to and from Glasgow and Stirling. This convenient location means the city centre, the bypass and M8 are all easy reachable, either by bus or by car. There are good local amenities, with more comprehensive shopping to be found at the Gyle Shopping Centre just a short journey away. The world-famous Edinburgh Zoo is based here and Murrayfield Ice Rink is also nearby.
