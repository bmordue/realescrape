6 Station Road, Gifford, East
Lothian EH41 4QL

Offers over £290,000

Semi-Detached House

2 Bedrooms

Bursting with character and charm, 6 Station Road is a delightful semi-detached two bedroom bungalow, located in the picturesque East Lothian village of Gifford (Gold winner in the Beautiful Scotland awards). The property has been tastefully decorated and upgraded by the current owners, creating a gorgeous, warm and inviting home. There are lovely, well-tended front and rear gardens. The front door opens on to a welcoming entrance hall, which in turn leads to the beautifully presented sitting room where the log burning stove makes a pretty focal point in the room. There are working shutters and a press cupboard. The kitchen is fitted with a series of wall and base mounted units, in addition to a breakfast bar. The integrated appliances comprise: electric induction hob, oven, fridge/freezer, dishwasher and a washing machine. There is a large conservatory to the rear, providing a great view of the established rear garden in all weathers. The property has two well proportioned double bedrooms; one to the front, one to the rear. The shower room (with shower, WC and wash hand basin) completes the internal accommodation. Heating and hot water are provided by electricity. Externally there is a large front garden, mainly laid with stone chippings, benefitting from hedges which add privacy. Last but by no means least is the fantastic south west facing garden which is a wonderful mix of trees (including a bountiful apple tree), plants, bushes and shrubs, with a lawn and patio area. Location Situated within the popular village of Gifford which is a picturesque and charming East Lothian conservation village full of character, historical buildings, and wide leafy streets. Walkers can enjoy scenic routes in the Gifford Community Woodlands or explore the many walks and trails in the area. Within the village, there is a well kept play park attached to a large green where various children's sports activities are hosted, along with a bowling club, cricket club, beautiful golf course/club, horticulture society, cinema club and a popular village hall. Bars, cafes, and restaurants to enjoy include The Tweeddale Arms Hotel overlooking the village green, the Lanterne Rouge, and the Goblin Ha' Hotel. Daily shopping needs are well catered for with a local convenience store, garage, and newsagent, along with Beech and Birch, a Scandinavian Homeware shop. Further options are available in nearby Haddington. Yester Primary School is located in the village, and it is in the catchment for the well-regarded Knox Academy. Private schooling choices are available in nearby Haddington, Dunbar, and Musselburgh as well of course as those in Edinburgh. Edinburgh's City Centre is reached by car via the A1 in approx. forty-minutes and there are regular train links from Longniddry. Extras All blinds, light fittings, fitted flooring and integrated appliances are included in the sale price.
