5 Chestnut Grove,
Dunfermline KY11 8AY

Offers over £240,000

Detached House

3 Bedrooms

An beautifully presented and extended, three bedroom, three public, detached villa located within the popular Pitcorthie area of Dunfermline. 5 Chestnut offers an ideal family home, set within a residential cul-de-sac with primary schooling available at nearby Pitreavie Primary and Canmore. Transport links available via Dunfermline, Rosyth and Inverkeithing Train stations with the M90 motorway only a short distance away. An excellent home and viewing comes highly recommended. Amenities available including various retail shops with a range of leisure facilities, coffee shops and restaurants at nearby Fife Leisure Park.

Large driveway with parking for several cars leading to garage. The garage is currently utilised as additional storage.
The accommodation briefly comprises of a front porch leading through to living room. Additional storage under the stairs. Separate dining room able to accommodate most table and chair sets. Quality flooring throughout.
Contemporary, upgraded kitchen with a wide range of storage options, worktop space, cold tap and integrated appliances. The property is extended to the rear with an additional public room and downstairs shower room.
The first floor features three bedrooms with built in sliding wardrobes within the master bedroom. Modern shower room with WC and wash hand basin.
Large gardens to the rear with a mixture of lawn and decked areas. Summer house and access to the garage from the rear.
