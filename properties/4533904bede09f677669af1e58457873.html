At first glance, 8 Springfield Terrace appears to be a traditional semi-detached home, but step inside and you'll discover a truly exceptional, design-led transformation. Reimagined by its current owners, who have successfully designed a number of homes, this three-bedroom property offers a carefully curated interior where quality, creativity and craftsmanship shine throughout.

From the moment you enter the hallway, you are drawn towards the magnificent open-plan kitchen and dining space at the rear, which is unquestionably the heart of the home. The kitchen has been beautifully crafted with sleek modern cabinetry and a full range of integrated appliances, including an induction hob with a concealed extractor. Striking Diespeker resin terrazzo worktops elevate the space further, adding both texture and visual impact. At the dining end, large sliding doors perfectly frame views of the private rear garden and flood the room with natural light. To the front of the property, the sitting room continues the thoughtful design narrative, offering a space full of warmth, character and subtle quirkiness, an inviting retreat from the open-plan hub. Off the rear hallway is a highly practical utility room and a wonderfully playful WC, full of personality and bold design choices, a space guaranteed to raise a smile. Upstairs, the design-led approach continues. The principal bedroom is a particularly generous space, enhanced by ceiling skylights fitted with electric blinds, allowing light to pour in while maintaining privacy. The en-suite bathroom is finished with porcelain tiling, distinctive waterproof woven-style flooring that combines the look of seagrass with durability, elegant brass fixtures and fittings, and excellent storage within a large drawer vanity unit. Two further bedrooms are located on this floor, along with a sleek and contemporary shower room complete with terrazzo flooring, continuing the cohesive aesthetic found throughout the home.

Externally, the garden has also benefited from the owners' creative vision. A well-maintained lawn is bordered by natural screening for privacy, while a modern patio area provides the perfect setting for outdoor dining and enjoying the coastal sunshine. To the front, there is a spacious driveway providing parking for two vehicles.

8 Springfield Terrace offers a rare opportunity to acquire a beautifully executed, design-led three-bedroom family home offering exceptional finishes, standout interiors and a private garden setting, all just a short walk from the magnificent Belhaven Bay and the East Lothian coastline.

Location

West Barns is a highly desirable coastal village situated just to the west of Dunbar, enjoying a peaceful setting while remaining closely connected to the town. The village is perhaps best known for its immediate proximity to the magnificent Belhaven Bay and popular surf school attracting outdoor enthusiasts year-round. West Barns has a strong sense of community, centred around its well-used village hall and local primary school, making it particularly appealing to families. The renowned Dukes Gallery and Coffee Shop is a much-loved local destination, offering excellent coffee, art and a welcoming meeting place for residents and visitors alike. A short distance away lies the historic and vibrant town of Dunbar, which provides a comprehensive range of amenities. Its bustling High Street offers an excellent selection of independent and artisan shops, cafes and restaurants, alongside larger supermarkets on the outskirts. The town's two colourful working harbours beneath the ruins of Dunbar Castle remain a focal point of community life, while the restored Dunbar Battery now provides a unique open-air venue and coastal garden. Leisure facilities include a swimming pool, fitness centre and two golf courses, with beautiful beaches, cliff-top walks and the John Muir Country Park all close at hand. Well-regarded local schooling is available at primary and secondary level, with private options including Belhaven Hill in Dunbar, Compass in Haddington, Loretto in Musselburgh and further choices in Edinburgh. Excellent transport links make commuting straightforward, with regular train services reaching Edinburgh in approximately 25 minutes, convenient bus routes, and easy access to the A1 for travel both north to the capital and south towards Newcastle.
