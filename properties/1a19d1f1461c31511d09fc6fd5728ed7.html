Brookfield PA5 8UT

Offers over £315,000

Detached House

3 Bedrooms

The detached home is in a desirable address in the hamlet of Brookfield and provides a modern layout with open plan kitchen and living space. The house is in excellent condition enjoying modern fixtures and fittings making it ideal to move straight in. There is an entrance hallway that leads straight through to reveal superb living space at the back of the house. This comprises of an open plan kitchen and dining space that sweeps round to a living room. Floor to ceiling windows run along the back wall and have a French door opening to the garden. The kitchen has sleek modern units with granite worktops and plenty of prep space on the island for those looking to whip up some culinary delights. Integrated appliances include a full sized fridge, full sized freezer, dishwasher, extractor fan and 5 burner gas hob on the island. There is space for laundry goods in the convenient utility cupboard. The dining area allows the perfect space to entertain whilst the breakfast bar provides for more casual dining. When you are looking to unwind the living room offers a welcoming room with a window to the front of the house and an open arch to the dining area. There is a tiled three piece bathroom with a three piece suite and over the bath shower with body jets. Three good sized bedrooms are on the upper level with the main bedroom benefiting from an en suite shower room. The property sits in a good plot with a driveway at either side of the house for off street parking and also has a detached garage. The back garden feels very private and has a main lawn area bordered with mature plants, shrubs and trees. There is also a patio which makes the ideal space for alfresco dining. Brookfield is a small hamlet with excellent access to the M8 motorway so is ideal for those commuting to the city centre or surrounding areas. Within Brookfield there is a tennis club, bowling green and cycle track. There is also easy access to the surrounding villages of Bridge of Weir and Houston where you will find a wide selection of amenities. Johnstone is also only a short distance away with an Aldi, Starbucks and Morrison's superstore and excellent rail links.
