48 Nantwich Drive,
Edinburgh EH7 6RA

Offers over £395,000

Detached House

2 Bedrooms

48 Nantwich Drive is an attractive detached bungalow with generous private gardens and a long drive-in. The property benefits from a modern bathroom and a newly fitted kitchen and new French doors have been fitted in the lounge. Further work is now required to complete the refurbishment, mainly decoration and flooring. The vestibule leads to a central hallway and to the rear is a lovely large lounge, with new French doors to the garden, as previously mentioned. The kitchen/dining room is a superb open plan dual aspect space with windows to the side and rear as well as a door from the kitchen area to the rear garden. There are an excellent range of new modern white gloss units with a halogen hob with overhead extractor, an eye level oven/grill and an integral fridge freezer, washing machine and dishwasher all included in the sale. The property has two good sized double bedrooms, both located to the front and to the side is a large modern family bathroom with a bath and a separate shower cubicle, partial wet wall panelling and a fitted vanity unit. The property also has a large attic which may be suitable for future development, subject to the necessary planning permissions. Gas central heating has been installed and the property has solar panels and double glazing. There is a private garden to the front and a long drive-in to the side and the rear the garden is particularly generous and mainly laid to lawn. Early viewing is highly recommended to fully appreciate the accommodation on offer.

Location
The property is situated in Craigentinny, to the east of the city centre, within walking distance of Portobello, with its popular beach and promenade, good local shopping with Co-op and Aldi supermarkets, a selection of independent shops and a variety of bars, restaurants, coffee shops as well as a Victorian swimming pool with gym and fitness classes, a Sailing and Kayaking club and Five a Side Football Centre. Meadowbank Retail Park is also within easy reach with a Sainsburys Supermarket and there is also a Morrisons Supermarket nearby. Fort Kinnaird Retail and Leisure Park with shopping, cinema and restaurants, and Asda Hypermarket are just a short drive away and the city centre is also within easy reach. Holyrood Park, Queens Park and Arthur's Seat are all nearby and there are several golf courses in the area as well as health/sports clubs and the recently rebuilt Meadowbank Sports Stadium. A good public transport service operates throughout the city and the main road networks, including the A1 and City Bypass are all accessible and schooling is available in the area at all levels.

Extras
Halogen hob with overhead extractor, oven/grill, integral fridge freezer, washing machine and dishwasher are all included in the sale.
