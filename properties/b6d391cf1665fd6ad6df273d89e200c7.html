Offers Over £175,000

Terraced House

3 Bedrooms

Thorntons are delighted to market for sale, No 21 Glamis Road, Forfar, a spacious mid-terraced Villa situated close to Forfar's town centre.

The accommodation, over two levels comprises: Modern fitted Dining Kitchen, 2 Bedrooms with fitted storage and shower room on the ground floor, with the lounge, master bedroom and en suite located on the first floor.

The property benefits from double glazing and gas central heating.

All floor and window coverings, integrated hob, oven, extractor fan, dishwasher, washing machine and freestanding fridge / freezer are included in the sale. Furniture is available by separate negotiation.

Externally the property benefits from a gated entrance allowing access to the property, driveway with space for several vehicles, a double garage with workshop and vehicle inspection pit and a fully enclosed garden

The property is conveniently located for easy access to the town of Forfar which offers a range of shops, services, schools and amenities and provides convenient commuting distance to the Dundee/Aberdeen A90 dual carriageway which connects to major routes north and south.

This property would make an ideal 1st time buy, family home or rental investment and early viewing is highly recommended.
