49 Cairnsmore Road, Castle
Douglas DG7 1BN

Offers over £120,000

Semi-Detached House

3 Bedrooms

The property is located on the outskirts of the thriving market town of Castle Douglas, adjoining countryside at the rear. Also known as 'The Food Town', Castle Douglas offers a wide range of independent shops, restaurants and cafes, Supermarkets, both Primary and Secondary School, Golf Course, Health Centre, Swimming Pool, Churches and a theatre.

49 Cairnsmore Road is a well-presented semi-detached family home constructed originally as an 'Orlit' and has been extensively renovated, modernised and improved by the local authority. The property boasts light filled rooms enjoying stunning outlooks over the countryside to the rear. These well-proportioned rooms are in excellent decorative order with original wooden doors throughout and an original gas fire in the lounge. There is double glazing and gas central heating throughout.

The ground floor comprises of a spacious Lounge/diner, modern fitted kitchen with direct access to the rear garden and a family bathroom. Upstairs there are three double bedrooms all with built in wardrobes.

Outside there are front and rear gardens predominantly laid to lawn which also includes a small paved patio and gravelled drying area. There is a metal garden shed.
