1 Osborne Drive,
Dumfries DG2 9JY

Offers over £80,000

End Terraced House

2 Bedrooms

The property is situated in a quiet location within Lochside with open ground to the front, around three miles from Dumfries town centre. Regular amenities including schools, bus service and shops are within easy walking distance.

1 Osborne Drive offers generously proportioned accommodation throughout and benefits from gas central heating and double glazing. Externally, there are level garden grounds to the front, side and rear. On street parking spaces are available close to the property. The property would benefit from some modernisation but would make an ideal home or buy-to-let proposition.

Viewing is highly recommended to appreciate the accommodation on offer.
