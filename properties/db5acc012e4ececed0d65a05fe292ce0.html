11/1 Lee Crescent, a beautifully appointed four-bedroom apartment nestled in the heart of Portobello. This charming first-floor flat situated within a beautiful crescent offers a perfect blend of traditional character and modern convenience, set within a peaceful residential street just moments from the beach. Inside, the property boasts a spacious and light-filled sitting room with elegant period features and a warm, welcoming atmosphere. The kitchen is thoughtfully designed with contemporary fittings and ample space for cooking and dining. The principal bedroom is a standout feature, boasting a newly fitted en-suite shower room with utility space that adds a touch of luxury and privacy. The three remaining bedrooms are also generously sized, ideal for guests or a home office, while the main bathroom is stylishly finished with a modern suite. All double bedrooms are generously sized, offering comfortable retreats with plenty of natural light. The bathroom is stylishly finished, featuring a modern suite. One of the property's most appealing features is its private rear garden - a tranquil haven with a well-maintained lawn and a paved area perfect for alfresco dining and outdoor relaxation. Whether you're enjoying a morning coffee in the sunshine or hosting a summer evening gathering. This property is ideal for professionals, couples, or anyone seeking a relaxed coastal lifestyle within easy reach of the city. With its blend of charm, comfort, and location, 11/1 Lee Crescent is a rare find in one of Edinburgh's most desirable neighbourhoods. The Local Area Portobello is a lovely seaside suburb of Edinburgh, located around 4 miles east of the city centre, on the edge of the Firth of Forth. Known for its charming promenade, stunning beach and friendly community, Portobello is a popular choice for families, young professionals and retirees. The beautiful beach provides a great place for residents to walk, cycle or enjoy water sports and the promenade features a variety of cafes and restaurants. There are plenty of amenities in Portobello, including a range of local shops, supermarkets and independent cafes and restaurants, most of which can be found on the high street. The area also offers a number of recreational facilities, such as Portobello swimming pool and fitness centre, and several parks and green spaces. For families with children, Portobello boasts several good schools, including some highly-rated primary schools. The area has a strong sense of community, with various events and activities throughout the year. Overall, Portobello offers a fantastic quality of life, with a beautiful seaside location, excellent amenities and good transport links to the city centre. It's known for its friendly community, making it a popular choice for those seeking a quieter, more relaxed way of life while staying close to all that Edinburgh has to offer.
