Erskine PA8 6HN

Offers over £255,000

Detached House

3 Bedrooms

Situated in the much admired Linburn location of Erskine and set at the end of the cul de sac is this stunning detached villa comprehensively modernised and upgraded in recent times not only internally, externally too creating a fabulous landscaped garden at the rear for outside entertaining. A reception hallway with WC off gives an immediate sense of the quality that you are about to witness. The lounge is to the front of the property and doesn't overlook anything other than the front driveway. The fabulous dining kitchen is of excellent proportions and has the benefit of space for a dining table & breakfast bar for casual dining. The kitchen is of a contemporary variety with Granite work surfaces complementing. There is also an integrated dishwasher and washing machine and the look is complete with a Rangemaster Range. A Upvc door leads to a set of side steps that in turn leads to a stone chipped area and the rear garden. On the first floor there are three well proportioned bedrooms, (the principal benefitting from built-in fitted wardrobes) and the fully tiled house bathroom. Externally to the front there is a monobloc driveway providing off street parking and access to the single attached garage (with double height ceiling). There is also a substantial stone chipped area to the side for future development/larger driveway if required. The stunning rear garden is a must see. Over three distinct levels the main level having artificial grass, a lower level with stone chipped patio are for alfresco dining and a higher level timber deck which can also be used for outside entertaining and all is bordered by timber fencing. The specification also includes gas central heating, EV charging point and double glazing. Erskine offers primary and secondary schooling, good public transport facilities and local shopping, including Morrisons superstore and Aldi. The neighbouring countryside caters for a wide range of sports/leisure activities including fishing, golf and all equestrian pursuits. There is good access to the Braehead shopping centre and the M8 motorway network providing access to most major towns and cities throughout the central belt of Scotland, as well as Glasgow International Airport which provides access to destinations further afield.
