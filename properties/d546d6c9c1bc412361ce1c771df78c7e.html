Detached Bungalow

McDougall McQueen are delighted to present to the market this bright and spacious extended detached bungalow, offering flexible accommodation in much sought-after private residential area in the lovely Midlothian town of Penicuik. This rarely available style of property in this quiet location is superbly placed to take advantage of local schooling, transport links and all other amenities in the Penicuik and surrounding areas. This property is ideal for a number of potential buyers including families and those looking for accommodation all on ground floor level. The property is presented in good clean condition throughout, having been well maintained by its owners. There are private garden grounds to the front, side and rear which are ideal for outside entertaining, affording stunning countryside views to the front. A driveway provides off-street parking for several cars and access to an attached garage which has both light and power. This ideal family home, its flexible layout and location are sure to attract a lot of interest, we would therefore recommend viewing at your earliest convenience to avoid disappointment.
