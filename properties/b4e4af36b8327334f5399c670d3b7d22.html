5, Fenton Barns Dairy Cottages, Fenton Barns,
Dirleton EH39 5AQ

Offers over £370,000

Semi-Detached House

3 Bedrooms

Set within the charming rural hamlet of Fenton Barns, this attractive semi-detached house enjoys a peaceful countryside setting while remaining within easy reach of North Berwick and a range of local amenities. The property offers three bedrooms and generous, well-proportioned accommodation arranged over two levels, newly-finished in a consistent, neutral style throughout. The flexible layout will appeal to a wide range of buyers, including families, professionals, or those seeking adaptable space for home working. Externally, the home benefits from a south-facing garden, a private driveway for off-street parking, and an attached store providing excellent storage space. The front door opens to a welcoming reception hall with warm honey-coloured flooring that flows into the other rooms on the ground floor. A spacious dual-aspect living room is the social heart of the home, offering a comfortable setting for everyday living. To the rear, the dining kitchen is fitted with contemporary shaker-style units and integrated appliances, with ample space for a dining table. Beyond this, a generous ground-floor bedroom (with access to the rear garden through French doors) is complemented by an adjoining study or dressing room, a convenient WC and a substantial store, creating a highly versatile area ideal for guests, a home office suite, or multi-generational living. Upstairs, a central landing leads to a well-proportioned principal bedroom with built-in storage and an en-suite shower room, and a further double bedroom. A modern family bathroom serves this level, finished in a clean, neutral style with a shower-over-bath. Gas central heating (with a newly-installed boiler under long-term warranty) and double glazing throughout ensure that the property is warm and energy efficient. Outside, the south-facing garden provides an ideal space for relaxing, entertaining, or family use. The private driveway offers generous parking for multiple vehicles, completing this well-balanced and adaptable home. Extras: The sale includes all fitted floor and window coverings, light fittings, and kitchen appliances.
