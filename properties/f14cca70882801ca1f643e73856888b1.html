2 Edinburgh Road,
Tranent EH33 1AN

Fixed Price £365,000

End Terraced House

3 Bedrooms

Warners are delighted to present to the market this rarely available and beautifully positioned three-bedroom end-terraced Victorian villa, occupying a prime spot on the highly regarded Edinburgh Road in the ever-popular town of Tranent. This charming property seamlessly blends elegant period features with a stylish modern interior and is offered to the market in true walk-in condition, making it an ideal home for a wide range of buyers. Situated just a short stroll from Tranent's vibrant town centre, the property enjoys excellent local amenities and transport links, while also benefiting from a private driveway and a generous, south-facing rear garden, perfect for family living and entertaining. The accommodation is both spacious and thoughtfully laid out, beginning with a welcoming entrance vestibule and hallway. The elegant bay-windowed living room, with its rich oak flooring and stunning mahogany fireplace, creates a warm and inviting atmosphere. Ornate cornicing adds further character and charm. Flowing seamlessly from the living space, the dining room offers a lovely setting for formal or casual meals and features a useful storage cupboard with pleasant views over the rear garden. A convenient downstairs WC adds practicality, while the contemporary kitchen is finished to a high standard with sleek gloss white cabinetry, integrated appliances, and a generous pantry-style storage cupboard. The kitchen also provides direct access to the beautifully landscaped rear garden, enhancing the home's indoor-outdoor flow. Upstairs, the property boasts a large principal double bedroom with a striking bay window and built-in wardrobes, complemented by two further well-proportioned double bedrooms. The modern family bathroom features a luxurious four-piece suite, including a separate shower and freestanding bath-offering both comfort and style. Externally, the home sits within private front, side, and rear gardens. The enclosed, south-facing rear garden has been landscaped for low maintenance and includes a charming summer house, making it an ideal space for relaxation or al fresco dining. The property also benefits from a private rear driveway with access via a garden gate. Additional storage is available in the partially floored attic, accessed via a Ramsey ladder. This exceptional period home offers space, style, and convenience in a sought-after location and must be viewed to be fully appreciated.
