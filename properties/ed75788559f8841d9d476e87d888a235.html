1 Murray Terrace,
Perth PH1 1BT

Offers Over £370,000

Detached Bungalow

3 Bedrooms

This attractive 3 bedroom detached property occupies an exceptionally large corner plot and is located within the sought-after Viewlands area of Perth. Close to excellent travel links and the highly-desirable schools of Viewlands, Oakbank and Perth Academy.  This comfortable home is presented in good condition across two levels of light and spacious accommodation. 

On the ground floor the accommodation comprises an entrance vestibule leading into a very welcoming central hallway with mahogany open staircase to the first floor; an elegant lounge with bay window and feature fireplace; dining room which has potential as a 4th bedroom; a delightful garden room with access to the garden; a bright kitchen with useful pantry; side sun room with utility space; a generous shower room and 2 double bedrooms. On the first floor there is a spacious double bedroom with en-suite shower room. The extensive garden grounds wrap around all sides of the property and are predominantly laid to lawn with well stocked, colourful borders. A sunny and private patio is perfect for those BBQs and al fresco dining. To the front there is a gravel driveway providing parking for several vehicles and this leads to a single garage with remote control roller shutter door. There is additional storage space in the cellar which is accessed by an external door.

This charming home benefits from gas central heating and double glazing and is sure to have broad appeal. Viewing is essential to appreciate the overall space and excellent location on offer.

Area

The property is conveniently located within the highly sought-after Viewlands area of Perth and within walking distance of local shops, primary and secondary schools. There is also a regular bus service nearby as well as road links into the city centre and out to the Broxden roundabout for travel to the central belt and beyond.
