Dean Street is moments from an enviable range of independent shops, restaurants, bars and cafes in the ever desirable Stockbridge district, less than a mile away from Edinburgh's City Centre. A Sainsburys Local can be found on Raeburn Place and a Waitrose supermarket at Comely Bank. Nearby Craigleith Retail Park offers a wide range of shopping facilities including a Marks & Spencers, Lidl, Boots, TK Maxx and a Sainsburys superstore. Enjoying the outdoors could not be easier with Inverleith Park, and the Royal Botanic gardens all close at hand and a leisurely stroll or cycle along the beautiful Water of Leith walkway makes the perfect escape from the day's hustle and bustle. For the active type, Glenogle swim centre offers beautifully restored Victorian swimming baths, a state-of-the-art gym and fitness classes. The Grange club on the edge of Inverleith Park boasts tennis, hockey, squash, and cricket. For the commuter, there is a frequent public transport system operating from the main road to most parts of the City, although as mentioned it is a pleasant short walk to the City Centre.
