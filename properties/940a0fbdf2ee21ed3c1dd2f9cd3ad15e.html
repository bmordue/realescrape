Situated within commuting distance of historic St Andrews (approx 5 miles), this three-bedroom, two-bathroom will appeal to many buyers, including professionals, couples and downsizers. Completing the home is a four-piece bathroom with a towel radiator and a separate shower room. Externally, the property benefits from low-maintenance gardens, a summerhouse, a store and private driveway parking. Extras: all fitted floor and window coverings, light fittings, and kitchen appliances are included.
