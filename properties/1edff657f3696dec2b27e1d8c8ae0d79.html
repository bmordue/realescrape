The agents are proud to bring to the market this upper main door first floor conversion situated on the ever popular island of Rothesay, Isle of Bute. A picturesque island which is accessible via a 35 minute ferry journey from the mainland at Wemyss Bay.

The island offers a variety of local amenities including schooling & leisure pursuits. Commuting from Glasgow is a few hours away and there are regular public transport connections (bus & rail links) to both Glasgow area and also the west coast of Scotland.

Privately accessed from the rear of the building, the property would make an ideal home for those wishing to downsize for retirement or have a holiday/weekend retreat in a popular location.

The property has been upgraded by the current owners and is well presented in neutral decor with the accommodation comprising: hallway, bright lounge with feature fireplace and ample dining area, modern fully fitted kitchen, 2 good sized bedrooms & well laid out shower-room.

The property also benefits from double glazing and a system of gas central heating.

Communal rear garden together with private fenced off rear area of garden laid out in patio design .
