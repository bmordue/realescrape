  • Off-street private driveway and integral garage

Occupying a prime position within the highly sought-after Monarch's Rise development, this exceptional four-bedroom detached villa enjoys attractive open views to the front and offers a specification that sets it apart from many comparable homes within the development. Having been extensively upgraded by the current owners since new, the property is presented in true move-in condition and provides a superb blend of contemporary style, high-quality finishes and practical family living.

The accommodation is thoughtfully arranged over two levels and extends to a welcoming entrance hallway, providing access to the principal ground floor apartments. Positioned to the front of the property, the spacious lounge is flooded with natural light and enjoys an attractive open outlook, creating a comfortable and relaxing environment for everyday family life.

The heart of the home is undoubtedly the impressive open-plan kitchen and dining area. Beautifully appointed with a range of stylish contemporary units, quality worktops and integrated appliances, the kitchen has been further enhanced by the addition of a breakfast bar, creating an ideal space for informal dining, entertaining and family gatherings. The adjoining dining area offers ample room for a large dining table and provides a wonderful setting for both everyday meals and special occasions. Direct access to the rear garden further enhances the sense of space and connectivity between indoor and outdoor living. Completing the ground floor accommodation is a modern cloakroom/WC together with internal access to the integral garage, providing excellent storage and everyday convenience.

On the upper floor, the property continues to impress with four well-proportioned bedrooms. The principal and second bedrooms benefit from beautifully upgraded en-suite shower rooms, finished to an exceptional standard with contemporary sanitaryware, stylish tiling and quality fittings. The remaining bedrooms offer versatile accommodation suited to family living, guest accommodation or home working requirements.

Particular attention should be drawn to the bathroom facilities throughout the home. All three bathrooms, including the family bathroom, en-suite shower room and ground floor WC, have been significantly upgraded and now provide a level of finish rarely encountered within modern developments. The striking family bathroom is especially noteworthy, featuring a luxurious and contemporary design that creates a true focal point on the upper level.

Externally, the property enjoys equally impressive surroundings. The open outlook to the front creates a wonderful sense of space and enhances the property's overall appeal. To the rear, the fully enclosed garden has been thoughtfully landscaped to provide a highly private outdoor retreat. A sheltered dining and entertaining area offers the perfect environment for outdoor relaxation and social occasions, while the generous lawn provides ample space for children, pets and family enjoyment. The combination of privacy, functionality and presentation ensures the outdoor space is every bit as impressive as the accommodation within.

Further benefits include a private driveway, integral garage, gas central heating, double glazing and excellent energy efficiency associated with a modern home.

Monarch's Rise has quickly established itself as one of Arbroath's most desirable modern residential developments, appealing to families and professionals alike. The development enjoys excellent access to a range of local amenities, including highly regarded schooling, supermarkets, leisure facilities and commuter routes. Arbroath town centre, the historic harbour, renowned coastal walks and beautiful beaches are all within easy reach, whilst Dundee can be reached in approximately 20 minutes by car, making the location ideal for those seeking a balance between coastal living and city accessibility.

Offering a superior specification, exceptional presentation and generous family accommodation throughout, 10 Moonlight Gardens represents one of the finest homes to become available within Monarch's Rise and is certain to appeal to a wide range of discerning purchasers.
