Immaculately presented and in true move-in condition, 4 Brand Court is a superb four-bedroom detached family home, set within a popular modern development on the edge of Dunbar. Built by Taylor Wimpey and originally the show home, the property is enhanced by a host of thoughtful extras and quality finishes, making it an outstanding example of contemporary family living.

The welcoming hallway sets the tone with its bright and airy feel, offering a handy WC and a very large storage cupboard. To the front, the sitting room is generous and filled with natural light, while to the rear lies the heart of the home: a sleek, open-plan kitchen and dining room. The kitchen is beautifully appointed with excellent cupboard space, quality integrated appliances, and a stylish breakfast bar. French doors lead directly out to the rear garden, creating a seamless connection between indoor and outdoor living. Upstairs, the principal bedroom enjoys fitted wardrobes and a modern en-suite shower room, along with views over the garden. Bedroom three, also to the rear, benefits from fitted storage. To the front, bedrooms one and two provide further flexible accommodation, with bedroom two featuring built-in wardrobes, while bedroom four is currently used as a home office. A contemporary family shower room completes the first-floor accommodation.

Externally, the rear garden is beautifully landscaped, combining lawn and decked areas with a pergola and mature planting, creating an inviting space for relaxation and entertaining. To the side, a gated driveway leads to a spacious single garage, while the neat front garden adds further kerb appeal.

The property also benefits from solar panels, improving energy efficiency and reducing running costs. With an open outlook across greenspace, this exceptional home offers modern style, family-friendly space, and a peaceful setting within easy reach of Dunbar's amenities, schools, beaches, and train station.

Location

Historic Dunbar, which is surrounded by fantastic scenery, is a vibrant thriving town with a real sense of community. There is a bustling high street with an excellent selection of independent and artisan shops, restaurants and galleries with the convenience of a large supermarket on the outskirts. There are excellent schools in the area. Private schooling is also available at Compass in Haddington, Belhaven Hill in Dunbar, Loretto in Musselburgh with further choices available in Edinburgh. The two colourful working harbours beneath the ruined castle are the hub of the town and the Harbour Trust is an active part of local life. The Dunbar Battery has recently been spectacularly restored to create a unique open-air venue with an amphitheatre and a coastal garden. There is also a leisure centre with a swimming pool and a newly opened platinum fitness centre, two golf courses, beautiful sandy beaches, spectacular cliff top walks and the John Muir Country Park. The town is also home to Coast to Coast, Scotland's largest surfing school. The convenience of easy access to Edinburgh City Centre by train (25 minutes) or via a regular bus service and access to the A1 and City Bypass is easy and straightforward, which combined with accessibility to Newcastle and the South by train or car adds to the appeal of this sought-after town.
