Ardenlea, 4 Lawhead Road East, St.
Andrews KY16 9ND

Offers over £785,000

Detached House

4 Bedrooms

Exceptional family home offering extensive accommodation with four reception rooms, four double bedrooms, and three bathrooms, all nestled within beautifully landscaped gardens with excellent private parking, set in a sought-after, leafy part of St Andrews.
This deceptively spacious double-storey house enjoys a prime corner plot position with mature garden grounds and a generous driveway leading to a double garage. With over 2,300 sq. ft. of versatile living space, this elegant property offers perfect family accommodation, all within easy reach of the town's prestigious university, golf courses, and coastal charm.
A gated pathway leads through manicured front gardens to a triple-aspect entrance porch. Solid wood flooring continues throughout much of the ground floor. A fine staircase with white painted balustrade and runner provides an elegant focal point, and glazed double doors allow natural light to filter between rooms. The principal living room is a particularly impressive space, with a bright dual outlook through a large picture window and further glazed panels. Hardwood flooring and neutral walls create a timeless canvas, while double sets of glazed French doors connect seamlessly to the adjoining conservatory and family room.
The conservatory has dual access to both the garden and internal living spaces. The lovely garden views from here enhance the feeling of retreat. Adjoining the main living room via French doors, the family room offers yet another generously sized and inviting space, complete with a feature fireplace set into a timber surround, and a charming bow window overlooking the front gardens.
The formal dining room, again finished in solid wood flooring, is located to the front of the property and benefits from a large picture window overlooking structured garden planting, with elegant proportions and traditional furnishings.
The dining kitchen is a beautifully appointed space. Contemporary shaker-style cabinetry in a soft mocha colour is topped with dark composite stone worktops. Complementary metro-tiled splashbacks, soft under-cabinet lighting, and stylish brushed metal handles complete the crisp and modern aesthetic. Appliances are thoughtfully integrated including a large stainless steel Rangemaster cooker with a five-burner gas hob, dual ovens and grill, and an extractor canopy above, as well as an integrated dishwasher, and space for a full-height fridge-freezer. A south-facing window floods the space with natural light and offers a charming outlook across the rear garden. The kitchen is amply sized to accommodate a family dining table and four to six chairs. Adjoining the kitchen is a utility room: tiled throughout, and offering practical work surfaces, a sink, undercounter appliance space, and generous built-in storage, as well as direct garden access.
The family home offers four well-appointed double bedrooms, thoughtfully distributed across both floors, and three bathrooms, ensuring excellent flexibility. Located off the central hallway, the ground-floor bedroom is a bright and spacious double with generous built-in wardrobes. With bespoke cabinetry, open shelving, dressing area, and a large window overlooking the front garden, this room is ideal as a guest suite, home office or ground-level principal bedroom. It is quietly positioned away from the main reception areas, offering peace and privacy. Adjacent to this bedroom lies a traditional family bathroom. A full-length bathtub is paired with a handheld brass shower fitting, while a washbasin and WC are set into a solid timber vanity unit, providing both storage and timeless appeal.
A striking feature of the property is the handsome staircase that rises from the reception hallway. As you ascend, natural light pours down through a central skylight, illuminating the stairwell and creating a bright and welcoming upper landing. The principal bedroom is illuminated by three windows, with an attractive en-suite shower room, fully tiled and fitted with a corner shower enclosure, vanity unit with integrated basin, and WC. Bedrooms two and three both come with similar double proportions, excellent fitted storage, and enough space for freestanding bedroom furniture. These rooms are served by a dedicated family shower room, located just off the landing, offering a large enclosed shower, WC, and vanity basin within a compact, modern layout. The property enjoys beautifully maintained wraparound garden grounds and also boasts off-street parking, with a wide paved driveway, a double garage, fitted with an up-and-over door, which offers additional secure parking and excellent storage potential.
The property benefits from gas central heating and double glazing ensuring a warm yet cost-effective living environment all year round.
Extras: Included in the sale are all the integrated kitchen appliances, fitted floorcoverings and light fittings.
