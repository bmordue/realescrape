8 Wester Suttieslea Loan, Newtongrange,
Midlothian EH22 4FH

Offers over £250,000

End Terraced House

3 Bedrooms

Warners are pleased to present this exceptional three-bedroom, end-terraced family home, offered in excellent decorative order and finished to high standards. The property boasts stylish décor and quality fixtures throughout. Situated in a sought-after modern development in the popular area of Newtongrange, it is a perfect choice for contemporary living. The home also benefits from a fully enclosed garden, set in a peaceful, leafy environment, yet within easy reach of local amenities, schools, and transport links. Upon entering, you are welcomed into a spacious hallway that provides a preview of the charming interiors to follow. The hallway offers a convenient WC and under-stair storage, before leading into the generous living room. This bright, well-proportioned space is ideal for both relaxing and entertaining, bathed in afternoon sunlight and featuring a tasteful wood-textured floor. Adjacent to the living room, the dining kitchen is expansive and opens out into the rear garden. With ample space for a large dining table and chairs, the kitchen features a sleek, modern design enhanced by integrated appliances for a seamless, contemporary look. Additionally, a utility/larder room offers extra storage and practicality. A staircase from the hallway leads to the first-floor landing, which is naturally lit and provides access to the three inviting bedrooms. Each bedroom is spacious, finished in neutral tones, and carpeted for comfort. The principal bedroom includes a built-in wardrobe and a stylish en-suite shower room. The remaining bedrooms share a family bathroom, which is equipped with a modern three-piece suite. A large attic space offers ample storage options. Double-glazed windows and gas central heating provide comfort and energy efficiency year-round. Externally, the property benefits from beautifully presented, low-maintenance front and rear gardens. The fully enclosed rear garden is ideal for families and summer entertaining, featuring a summer house and a veranda complete with a barbecue and food preparation area-perfect for hosting guests.
