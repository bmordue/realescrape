Lindsays are delighted to present this lovely one-bedroom semi-detached bungalow located in a popular residential area of Carnoustie. The property has recently undergone a programme of refurbishment including new windows, doors, heating system, modern kitchen and decorative improvements throughout. While a significant amount of work has already been completed, it now offers the perfect opportunity for a new owner to put their own finishing touches to the home.

The accommodation comprises a useful porch at the front leading into a hall, a bright and welcoming lounge, and a newly installed modern kitchen providing excellent storage space with direct access to the rear garden. The double bedroom is generously proportioned and benefits from mirrored wardrobes, while the bathroom is bright and features a shower over the bath.

Externally, the property is set back from the street with an enclosed front garden and parking available directly outside. To the rear is a surprisingly large, fully enclosed garden with lawn, drying area and a summer house included in the sale. The property is sold as seen with all light fittings, blinds and newly purchased integrated white goods included.

This well-located bungalow is an attractive option for first-time buyers, downsizers or those seeking a project with the scope to complete and personalise to their own taste.

Carnoustie is situated on Scotland’s East Coast, approximately 10 miles north of Dundee. Renowned originally as a holiday resort the town has many lively businesses along its main street including coffee shops and hostelries. Famous for its golf association the town is proud of its landmark Championship links course drawing many visitors and golfers to the area. The town caters for this with several hotels, guesthouses and bed and breakfast establishments and the impressive Carnoustie Golf Hotel.  The town boasts 3 primary schools and a high school and allows easy commuting both north and south by road and railway.
