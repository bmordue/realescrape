Closing date set for Tuesday 29th April 2025 at 12 noon. The property offers flexible living space and comprises: On the ground floor - welcoming entrance hallway with cloakroom/WC, a spacious open plan lounge and dining room, a stunning kitchen and spacious double bedroom or family room/home office. Travelling up the lovely staircase with feature wall the first-floor accommodation comprises an impressive master bedroom with stylish en-suite and a double fitted-wardrobe with mirrored doors, a further double bedroom also includes a double fitted-wardrobe with mirrored doors and the three-piece family bathroom completes the accommodation. A feature of the property is the generously proportioned accommodation throughout, with stylish interior design and immaculate finishes throughout. The bright and spacious lounge includes a bay window to the front with a dual aspect to the dining area with patio doors to the rear garden, creating an abundance of natural light and a generous living space. The lovely kitchen offers an excellent range of base and wall cabinets with grey gloss doors and cabinet lighting, modern laminate work surfaces and integrated appliances include a five-ring gas hob with extractor canopy, electric double oven, microwave with space for free standing appliances. The family bathroom and en-suite both offer high specification finishes with ''Velux'' windows creating natural light. The bathroom comprising a bath incorporating a thermostatic rain shower with glazed screen and concrete style wall panels, a WC, wash hand basin set in a vanity storage cabinet, a wall mounted mirror and a heated towel rail. The en-suite includes designer tiling, a shower compartment with thermostatic rain shower combination, WC and a wash hand basin set in a vanity cabinet. Externally there is a generously sized private driveway providing parking for several cars and a large private front garden area laid to lawn. The enclosed, child friendly and secluded rear garden offers a paved patio, a large area laid to lawn with borders, a barked area and a large garden storage shed.
