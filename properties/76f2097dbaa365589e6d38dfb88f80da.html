Located in the desirable village of Monikie, this impressive four-bedroom detached villa offers generous family accommodation in a peaceful, established residential setting. Finished to a high standard throughout, the property benefits from gas central heating, double glazing, underfloor heating, and a built-in surround sound system, combining modern comfort with functional living.

All fitted floor coverings, blinds, and light fittings are included in the sale. Additional furnishings may be available by separate negotiation.

The home opens with a welcoming hallway featuring convenient storage and access to a well-appointed cloakroom/WC. A large formal lounge with a bay window provides a bright and relaxing space, perfect for both family life and entertaining. The heart of the home is the open-plan kitchen and dining/family area, designed with both space and style in mind. The kitchen is fully fitted, flowing seamlessly into the expansive family space, where a charming log-burning stove creates a cosy focal point. Patio doors open directly onto the rear garden, allowing indoor-outdoor living during warmer months. A separate utility room offers additional practicality and includes access to both the garden and the integrated double garage. Upstairs, the accommodation continues to impress. The primary bedroom boasts a bay window, built-in storage, and a private ensuite shower room. A spacious guest bedroom also benefits from built-in storage and its own ensuite. Two further double bedrooms, both with fitted wardrobes, provide flexibility for growing families or working from home. A family bathroom completes the upper floor, fitted with a three-piece suite and chrome towel rail.

Externally, the property is set back behind a neat front lawn and driveway leading to the double garage. The rear garden is fully enclosed, private and well maintained a perfect space for children, pets, and outdoor entertaining.

This is a rare opportunity to acquire a high-quality family home in one of Angus's most sought-after villages, offering generous space, modern features, and a peaceful, community-oriented location just a short drive from Dundee and surrounding areas.
