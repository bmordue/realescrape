Andrews KY16 9LS

Offers over £1,100,000

Semi-Detached House

5 Bedrooms

Welcome to a stunning five-bedroom semi-detached house with an approximately 1-acre plot and a wealth of accommodation over three floors, boasting sumptuous contemporary interiors that have undergone extensive development and upgrading, whilst still retaining lovely original features, to provide a truly unique and luxurious home for families.

With ample private parking and a roughly 1-acre mature garden, this five-bedroom semi-detached house is an exclusive family home of grand proportions. The property covers over 2951 square feet and it has undergone significant renovations between 2015 and 2017. Major works include: a reconfigured back of house with the formation of a stylish open-plan reception area (combining two rooms into one); a noteworthy attic conversion to create a self-contained bedroom with a study and sitting space; and a new bespoke kitchen with an Aga cooker and integrated appliances - all newly installed. In addition, new windows, central heating system, plumbing, wiring, and cavity wall insulation was added at the same time. The end result is simply astonishing, creating an incomparable family home with outstanding features.

Part of the Hepburn Gardens conservation area, this southeast-facing property also has a highly sought-after location in St Andrews. It is in easy reach of the beaches and world-famous golf courses, as well as schools, amenities, the botanic garden, and the picturesque Lade Braes Walk.

Entrance - Welcome to a substantial family home of magnificent proportions

The home has an imposing fa�ade which hints at the substantial accommodation therein. Upon entering, you are warmly greeted by a welcoming vestibule with built-in storage. A central hall extends from here, the neutral styling and oak floor (laid throughout the ground level) indicative of the home's exceptional standards. For convenience, the hall also has a traditional-inspired WC cloakroom.

Reception rooms - A cleverly designed reception area with a considerable footprint

Glazed doors make a grand entrance into the open-plan living/formal dining room. Here, a considerable footprint of 680 square feet allows for limitless configurations of furniture, including clearly zoned lounge and dining areas. It further benefits from dual-aspect glazing, which includes a full wall of oversized windows and patio doors gazing out to the scenic garden beyond. A neutral finish and an ornate original feature fireplace (inset with a log-burning stove) complete the allure of this cleverly designed space. In addition, a large sitting room (in light blue) provides a second reception area. Complete with a cosy original open fireplace and a southeast-facing bay window, it offers a more intimate setting for unwinding.

Kitchen - A superior dining kitchen to bring family and friends together

Offering superior space and storage, the dual-aspect dining kitchen is an exquisite example of an ergonomic design that combines style and practicality. It features an eye-catching arrangement of Shaker-inspired cabinetry in two different tones alongside on-trend splashback tiles and a continuous worktop. It is a highly sophisticated aesthetic. Adding further appeal for food lovers, it comes with an efficient Aga range cooker with four electric ovens and a six-burner gas hob. An integrated dishwasher, washing machine, and tumble dryer are also included, along with a freestanding American-style fridge/freezer.

Bedrooms - Large bedrooms with standout features

The dual-aspect principal suite is a standout feature, covering an expansive 364 square feet with generous built-in wardrobe storage. It further boasts a contemporary en-suite shower room with digital rainfall shower, and the most impressive garden views, capturing the mature plants and trees in all their glory.

The five double bedrooms are all wonderfully bright and airy, further enhanced by neutral decor and soft carpets for maximum comfort. Occupying the entire second floor, along with an attic store and eaves storage, the second bedroom is another highlight. Spanning 410 square feet, it has plenty of room for comfortable seating and a study area, proving ideal for independent family members who seek a private setting akin to a self-contained suite. The three remaining bedrooms (one with a wardrobe and another with storage) offer further space and versatility for family life.

Bathrooms - Luxurious bathroom facilities with high-end finishings

In addition to the WC and en-suite, there is a luxurious four-piece family bathroom enveloped in premium tiles. It features modern fixtures and fittings by the Albion Bath Company, and includes a toilet, a double-drawer washbasin, a digital rainfall shower enclosure, and a claw-footed freestanding (double-ended) bath. For year-round comfort, the property has gas central heating and double-glazed windows.
