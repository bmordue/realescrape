Gilson Gray are delighted to present to the market an exceptional detached luxury family home set within one of the most exclusive residential developments in the East of Scotland.  The charming village of Auchterhouse is nestled at the base of the Sidlaw Hills, offering all the advantages of a rural lifestyle while being perfectly located for commuting to Dundee, Angus, Aberdeen and Perthshire. Known for its collection of some of the finest homes in Tayside, the village provides a peaceful yet accessible setting. Just a short distance away, Dundee, Scotland’s fourth-largest city, boasts a range of amenities you'd expect from a vibrant and revitalized urban area, including retail parks, the V&A Design Museum, Ninewells Teaching Hospital, the future Eden Project, as well as a mainline railway station and a domestic airport offering daily connections to the South of England and beyond.

Spanning approximately 440SQM, this incredible family home offers spacious and adaptable accommodation. The ground floor accommodation includes an entrance vestibule, bathroom with four piece suite, adaptable family room / office, two double bedroom and access to the integral double garage. The 1st floor accommodation features a bright lounge with feature turret, contemporary kitchen with dining area, sun lounge, utility room, formal dining room, W/C and two well-proportioned double bedrooms both with en-suite facilities. The 2nd floor accommodation includes a large adaptable space/reading room and the primary bedroom with dressing area, terrace and en-suite. Practical attributes include double glazing and an LPG heating system.

Externally the property is set within spectacular landscaped garden grounds which enjoy stunning panoramic views of the Sidlaws. Vehicular needs are catered for with a horseshoe driveway leading to an integral double garage.

Internal viewing is essential to enable potential buyers to fully appreciate the extent and quality of accommodation on offer within this magnificent detached family home.

There is a factoring arrangement in place for the upkeep of common areas including the maintenance of any common buildings, outdoor areas, stair cleaning and lift maintenance (where applicable). The current factor is Palm's Property Management with an annual payment of approximately £1,200.
