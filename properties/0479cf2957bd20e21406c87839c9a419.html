Location
3 James Ewart Avenue sits in a quiet cul de sac within a popular residential development on the outskirts of Dalbeattie. Dalbeattie offers primary and secondary schooling, shops, local food stores and a health centre. Walkers and mountain bikers are also well catered for in the region with the town woods just minutes away and the 7Stanes cycle tracks on hand in the Dalbeattie Forest, less than a mile away. The "Granite Town" of Dalbeattie is the gateway to The Solway Coast, and is 4 miles from the sailing village of Kippford, with the sandy beaches of Rockcliffe & Sandyhills just a few more minutes away.

Description
Beautifully presented two bedroom, detached bungalow with level garden grounds to the front and rear. The property benefits from gas central heating and newly installed double glazing throughout. The current owner has made a number of improvements in recent years including the installation of a new boiler, solar panels, new external and internal doors, new fencing and patio and full redecoration including newly fitted floor coverings throughout. The property is currently configured to provide one bedroom and two reception rooms, with the master bedroom now located where the lounge used to be but this configuration could be easily changed. Off street parking for several vehicles is available. The property would ideally suit a couple, small family or a retired buyer.

Viewing is highly recommended to appreciate the accommodation on offer.
