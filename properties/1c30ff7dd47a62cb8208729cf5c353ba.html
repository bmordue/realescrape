12 Cleeve Drive,
Perth PH1 1HH

Offers Over £235,000

Detached House

3 Bedrooms

*CLOSING DATE SET 12 NOON FRIDAY 16/01/26.  Lindsays are delighted to offer for sale this 3/4 bedroom semi-detached villa occupying a fantastic, elevated position within the popular Oakbank area of Perth. The property does require a scheme of modernising but offers plenty of potential in a great location.

Access is via the main door at the side of the property with accommodation on the ground floor comprising a hallway with large understairs storage; a good sized front facing lounge with feature fireplace; dining room which could be used as a fourth bedroom if required; family bathroom comprising vanity wash hand basin, WC and bath with shower over and fitted kitchen with ample storage, space to dine, door to the rear garden and integrated appliances to include a fridge/freezer, ceramic hob, extractor and double oven. On the upper level there are three double bedrooms, all with built in storage.  Externally a tarmac driveway offers plenty of parking and leads to a single garage with remote control door. The front garden is laid to lawn with planted borders and the rear garden is enclosed and low maintenance with paving, gravel and further planting.  There is a handy garden store which also houses the central heating boiler under the house, and it is accessed from the rear.

12 Cleeve Drive is a most welcome addition to the market and its location and flexible accommodation makes viewing essential to appreciate all it has to offer.

Area

Situated in the desirable Oakbank area of the city of Perth, the property is well placed for schools and local amenities. The property is also on the bus route to and from the city centre. Perth also offers a wide range of commuting services including bus, road and rail links.
