64 Princess Crescent,
Paisley PA1 3SL

Offers over £125,000

Upper Flat

2 Bedrooms

This lovely two bedroom upper cottage flat offers a superb opportunity for a variety of buyers and enjoys a quiet residential setting within the Whitehaugh district of Paisley just a short walk from a host of local amenities.

The building has recently been refurbished with new insulated cladding, there is a shared driveway and this property further benefits from a timber garage and enclosed private garden.

Inside the property boasts spacious rooms, fresh decor and a beautifully appointed kitchen and shower room. Furthermore there are modern double glazed windows and a gas fired central heating system with combination boiler.

A small entrance hall leads to the stairway to the upper landing from where all the rooms are accessed. A deep cupboard off this landing affords handy storage space. The lounge is a very comfortable reception room with bay window projection adding additional depth and allowing plenty of natural light to flood in. The focal point of this room is the feature fire place with gas fire. Further useful storage space is found within a deep walk in cupboard.

Both bedrooms are of excellent proportion, bedroom one to the front and bedroom two to the rear. Both have built in storage space and enjoy pleasant aspects. The kitchen is one of the highlights of this property having been tastefully refurbished. There is an integrated hob, oven and microwave and a number of wall and base storage units. Two windows lend light and frame the pleasant aspects to the rear. The shower room has also been re-modelled in recent times complete with broad shower cubicle, wc and wash hand basin within a vanity unit. The grey toned wet wall compliments the vinyl floor covering and the stylish fixtures & fittings. A ceiling hatch provides access to the attic space.

Worth particular mention are the delightful private gardens which are enclosed by timber fencing. The timber garage has doors to the front and a courtesy door to the rear leading into the private gardens. There is also access to a shared drying green.

This property forms part of an established residential pocket just a short walk from a host of amenities on Glasgow Road. Paisley Town Centre and Renfrew Road Retail Park are within easy reach and offer a further wide selection of shopping and recreational facilities. There are regular local bus services and Paisley Gilmour Street Station is around 1 mile away. There are also excellent road links to the M8 motorway and Glasgow Airport. Other nearby landmarks include Barshaw Park, Ralston Golf Club and David Lloyd Renfrew.
