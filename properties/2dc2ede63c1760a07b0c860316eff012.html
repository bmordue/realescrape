The Farmhouse, Essenside
Place TD1 3RT

Offers Over £320,000

Detached House

4 Bedrooms

Tucked away enjoying a very peaceful setting in the popular village of Tweedbank. this substantial detached property is perfectly suited to those searching for a spacious family home and is ready to move into. It sits within walking distance of the railway station, making it ideal for those looking for a transport connection, and is also well placed for the nearby primary school and other local amenities. The property itself is deceptively spacious, offering very flexible accommodation, with the living areas particularly generously proportioned especially the lounge/dining room and the kitchen which is amply large enough for dining. Upstairs, all four bedrooms are generous doubles whilst there is the added benefit of both a bathroom and separate shower room. Externally, the gardens extend to the front, side and rear and enjoy an excellent degree of privacy whilst there are various outbuildings which could be adapted for a variety of different uses. Parking is provided by means of a garage and there is ample additional parking in the adjacent car park.
