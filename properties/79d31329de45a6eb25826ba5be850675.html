5 Park Avenue,
Paisley PA2 6HL

Offers over £335,000

Detached House

4 Bedrooms

This individual detached home occupies a good plot and is located on Park Avenue in Paisley, a popular and desirable address. The house has a flexible layout with three bedrooms on the upper level and one on the ground floor, although this could be used as another public room if required. As you enter the house the reception hallway brings you in where there is access to the lower apartments and your first encounter with the central stairwell which is surly one of the houses unique features. The stair has a gallery level on the half landing and also leads to a second gallery from the upper landing. Both look down in the main living and dining area and show the high ceilings and spacious accommodation. The lounge is open plan to the dining area and is a great space with impressive high ceilings and lots of room to relax, socialise and entertain. Double doors from the dining area open to the garden room on the rear of the house which provides another public room which is flooded with light from the ceiling and windows. The impressive dining kitchen has plenty of fitted storage units and a breakfast bar for casual dining. There are appliances including an electric hob, double oven and a dishwasher plus space for an American style fridge freezer. Further laundry goods and an additional fridge can be housed in the useful utility room which is just off the kitchen. As previously mentioned, the property has four bedrooms with one on the ground level and three on the upper floor. The family bathroom can also be found on the upper level,and it is a great size and includes a four piece suite comprising a wash hand basin, WC, bath and shower cubicle. The property further benefits from gas central heating and double glazing. There are grounds surrounding the property which including parking at the front for a number of vehicles. The back garden has a lawn area and patios to enjoy the weather. The apple tree provides a good harvest and there are further mature plants, shrubs and trees in the garden.
