Gilson Gray are delighted to present to the market a spectacular detached luxury villa offering breath-taking panoramic views of the River Tay. 

The subject property is located in a highly desirable area on the outskirts of Dundee, providing convenient access to a range of amenities, including shops, schools, scenic nature walks, Dundee Technology Park, and Ninewells Teaching Hospital. The nearby A90 ensures easy commuting to major cities: Edinburgh (57 miles), Glasgow (75 miles), and Aberdeen (74 miles). Nearby Dundee, Scotland's fourth-largest city, offers all the amenities of a vibrant, regenerated urban centre, such as retail parks, the V&A Design Museum, the upcoming Eden Project, and excellent transport links via the mainline railway station and domestic airport, with daily connections to southern England and beyond. For golf enthusiasts, the world-renowned St Andrews, known as the "Home of Golf," is just 19 miles away, offering an array of prestigious courses and historical sites.

Designed by renowned architect, Jon Frullani, this exceptional property has been finished to an impeccable standard throughout. The ground floor boasts a contemporary open-plan kitchen/ dining room/ family room, utility room, bathroom with a three-piece suite, guest bedroom with an en-suite, and bedroom three that could also serve as a further reception room. On the upper level, you’ll find an impressive lounge offering unparalleled views, feature frameless glass balcony off the landing, study, bedroom four and a spacious master suite complete with an en-suite bathroom, walk-in wardrobe, and a Finnish sauna. The property is equipped with high-end features such as gas-fired central heating, double glazing, security alarm, 20 solar panels and a CCTV system. The impressive German-designed kitchen with marble worktops includes a suite of Bosch integrated appliances, including an induction hob, oven, fridge, freezer, warming drawer, combination oven/microwave/grill and dishwasher. 

The exterior is equally impressive, with beautifully landscaped gardens to the front and rear of the property. The rear garden features a formal lawn and two timber decked areas, perfect for outdoor relaxation, entertaining and al fresco dining. An automatic gated driveway leads to a two car garage and ample parking for multiple vehicles. 

Luxury homes of this calibre rarely come to market and early viewing is highly recommended to fully appreciate the stunning view, superior quality and generous accommodation on offer.
