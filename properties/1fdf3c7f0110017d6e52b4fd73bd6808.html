This inviting, move-in-ready two-bedroom flat is quietly located in desirable St Andrews, just off the main A-road and within walking distance of attractions including the town's central shops, the prestigious university, a primary school, and the beach. A selection of fitted units and ample worktop space is paired with an integrated dishwasher, oven, hob and extractor, alongside a freestanding washing dryer and fridge.

Two bedrooms are positioned just off the hall and are fitted with cosy carpeting. The double bedroom benefits from a built-in wardrobe, while the second room offers flexibility as a single bedroom, office, or dressing room. Completing the accommodation is a stylish aqua-panelled shower room, featuring a WC and basin set into vanity storage, as well as a deluxe walk-in rainfall shower. The home is kept warm and efficient by an electric heating system and benefits from modern UPVC double-glazed windows throughout. St Andrews, Fife

World-renowned for its ancient university, golfing heritage and stunning beach, St Andrews attracts more than half a million visitors each year, and is regarded as one of the finest towns and best places to live in Scotland. Characterised by its narrow cobbled streets and beautiful architecture, the bustling town centre hosts a charming blend of independent shops and high-street retailers, plus diverse cafes, coffee shops, delis, greengrocers, butchers and bakeries. There are also several large supermarkets on the outskirts of the town. St Andrews enjoys international fame as 'The Home of Golf' - the game has been played at St Andrews Links for over 600 years, and the iconic Old Course draws thousands of professionals, amateurs and spectators from across the globe. Other tourist attractions include the 12th and 13th- century ruins of St Andrews Cathedral and St Andrews Castle, as well as St Andrews Botanic Garden. Excellent state schools can be found nearby; private schooling options include a Montessori nursery and St Leonards School. Situated in the East Neuk of Fife, St Andrews is approximately 30 minutes' drive from Dundee and 90 minutes' drive from Glasgow and Edinburgh. Excellent local and intercity bus links serve the town; daily direct flights to London are available from Dundee airport.
