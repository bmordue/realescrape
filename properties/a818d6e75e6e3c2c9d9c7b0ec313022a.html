Detached House

4 Bedrooms

Warners are delighted to present this exceptional four-bedroom detached villa with a private driveway and integral garage, located in a quiet and modern residential development in the charming rural village of Ormiston, East Lothian. Finished to an impeccable standard, this immaculate home boasts contemporary interiors and stylish décor, offering comfortable and elegant living accommodation ideal for modern family life. The ground floor opens with a welcoming entrance vestibule, leading to the bright and spacious lounge positioned at the front of the property. To the rear, a stunning dining kitchen serves as the heart of the home, featuring sleek modern units, integrated appliances, plinth lighting, and French doors that open onto the private rear garden - perfect for seamless indoor-outdoor living. A utility room and a convenient WC complete the ground-floor layout. Upstairs, the property offers a generously sized master bedroom with built-in wardrobes and a luxurious en-suite shower room. Three further well-proportioned bedrooms provide flexibility for family living, guests, or a home office. The accommodation is completed by a beautifully appointed main bathroom, featuring a contemporary white three-piece suite. Externally, the landscaped rear garden is fully enclosed and mainly laid to lawn, complemented by separate patio areas that are perfect for al fresco dining and outdoor entertaining. To the front, the driveway provides parking for two cars and leads to the integral single garage. Additional features include gas central heating, double glazing, and solar panels for improved energy efficiency.
