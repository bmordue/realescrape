20 Kedslie Place, Liberton,
Edinburgh EH16 6NU

Offers over £400,000

Detached House

2 Bedrooms

Located in Liberton, a few miles south of Edinburgh's city centre, this 81sqm two bedroom detached bungalow offers plenty of space and potential in a prime location. The front door opens to a welcoming vestibule and hallway leading into the home. On the right are the living room and dining room, which are both incredibly spacious and bright, and are connected by sliding doors. The kitchen, located across the hall, comes equipped with a door onto the driveway, which has enough room for several cars and access to a single garage. The larger of the two bedrooms is located to the rear of the property, and overlooks the south-west facing rear garden. The smaller bedroom is to the front of the property and is flooded with light thanks to a large bay window. A family bathroom completes the layout. Although the property now requires modernisation, this is an exceptional opportunity to create a bespoke family home. Further benefits include double glazing, gas central heating, private front and rear gardens, long driveway, single garage, and potential to convert the loft and extend into the garden, subject to necessary consents and permits. The Location Liberton lies to the south of the city centre where there is a good range of local shopping and banking facilities at nearby Cameron Toll and there are additional shopping facilities at Straiton Retail Park. The city centre is easily accessed by car or bus, approximately twenty minutes away, and the Royal Infirmary and the University of Edinburgh's King's Buildings are also nearby. Excellent road links are provided by the city bypass giving access to Edinburgh International Airport, the Queensferry Crossing, the Scottish Borders and East Lothian. Extras The items included in the sale of the property are the carpets, fitted floor coverings, integrated and free standing appliances, light fittings and fixtures. The seller will not warrant the working order of any appliances, systems or services. The property is being sold as seen. Council tax band E
