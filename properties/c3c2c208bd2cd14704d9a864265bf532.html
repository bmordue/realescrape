2 Comrie Avenue, Dunbar is a substantial and well-presented four-bedroom family home by Avant Homes, ideally positioned to enjoy an open outlook to the front towards the sea and a highly desirable south-facing rear garden. Presented in true move-in condition, the property offers generous, flexible accommodation perfectly suited to modern family living.

Entrance is via a bright and welcoming hallway that leads through the ground floor accommodation. To the front of the property is a comfortable sitting room with surround-sound speakers integrated into the ceiling, making it an excellent cinema room. To the rear lies the heart of the home: a spacious open-plan kitchen, dining, and family room. The kitchen is sleek and modern, featuring excellent storage alongside a combination of integrated and freestanding appliances. The adjoining family area is a versatile space with bi-folding doors opening directly onto the private rear garden, creating a seamless connection between indoor and outdoor living. Off the kitchen is a highly practical utility and laundry room, along with a conveniently located downstairs WC, adding to the everyday functionality of the home.

Upstairs, a spacious landing provides additional built-in storage. Two bedrooms to the front enjoy the attractive outlook towards what will be a new small golf course and the sea, including the principal bedroom which benefits from sliding fitted wardrobes and a contemporary en-suite shower room. Two further well-proportioned bedrooms are positioned to the rear, overlooking the garden and offering flexible accommodation for family life or home working. Completing the internal accommodation is a family bathroom complete with rainfall shower over bath.

Externally, the property is equally impressive. To the front there is a driveway, ample additional on-street parking, and an integral garage which is currently used as a games room. To the rear, the fully enclosed south-facing garden is ideal for enjoying the Dunbar sunshine, with a mix of lawn, raised beds with mature planting, and decked areas perfect for outdoor dining and entertaining
A generous and thoughtfully laid-out family home offering spacious living, excellent natural light, sea views to the front and a sunny south-facing garden to the rear, all within a popular Dunbar development.

Location

Historic Dunbar, which is surrounded by fantastic scenery, is a vibrant thriving town with a real sense of community. There is a bustling high street with an excellent selection of independent and artisan shops, restaurants and galleries with the convenience of a large supermarket on the outskirts. There are excellent schools in the area. Private schooling is also available at Compass in Haddington, Belhaven Hill in Dunbar, Loretto in Musselburgh with further choices available in Edinburgh. The two colourful working harbours beneath the ruined castle are the hub of the town and the Harbour Trust is an active part of local life. The Dunbar Battery has recently been spectacularly restored to create a unique open-air venue with an amphitheatre and a coastal garden. There is also a leisure centre with a swimming pool and a newly opened platinum fitness centre, two golf courses, beautiful sandy beaches, spectacular cliff top walks and the John Muir Country Park. The town is also home to Coast to Coast, Scotland's largest surfing school. The convenience of easy access to Edinburgh City Centre by train (25 minutes) or via a regular bus service and access to the A1 and City Bypass is easy and straightforward, which combined with accessibility to Newcastle and the South by train or car adds to the appeal of this sought-after town.
