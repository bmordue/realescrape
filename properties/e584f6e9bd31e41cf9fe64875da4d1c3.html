25-27 Port Street,
Dalbeattie DG5 4BE

Offers over £110,000

End Terraced House

3 Bedrooms

25-27 Port Street is located close to edge of Dalbeattie, only a short walk away from Dalbeattie Forest. Dalbeattie offers primary and secondary schooling, shops, local food stores and a health centre. Walkers and mountain bikers are also well catered for in the region with the town woods just minutes away and the 7Stanes cycle tracks on hand in the Dalbeattie Forest, less than a mile away. The "Granite Town" of Dalbeattie is the gateway to The Solway Coast and is 4 miles from the sailing village of Kippford, with the sandy beaches of Rockcliffe & Sandyhills just a few more minutes away.

This traditional granite, end terraced property formerly comprised two separate homes which are currently linked via one internal door. The layout of the property is such that the purchaser could use the property as two separate residences or develop the property to form one sizeable home. 25-27 Port Street benefits from gas central heating and partial double glazing. The rear garden is very generous in size and currently houses two large outbuildings which are in a dilapidated condition. Off street parking is also available. 25-27 Port Street will require a full renovation but once works have been completed, the property would suit a range of purchasers.

Viewing is recommended to appreciate the accommodation on offer.
