First Floor Flat

This modern two-bedroom flat, on the first floor of the popular Waterfront Gait development, enjoys an appealing suburban setting just a short stroll from Edinburgh's seafront. Shops, schools, and excellent transport and road links are all nearby, ensuring swift commuting into the city and beyond. The bright, well-presented interiors, featuring open-plan living, present a blank canvas for the new owner to personalise, and the flat is ready for immediate occupation. With the added appeal of secure entry access, stairs, landscaped communal gardens, and allocated parking, it represents a fantastic prospect for first-time buyers, professionals, or those seeking a rewarding rental opportunity in a sought-after area.
Inside, the welcoming hall (with useful storage) flows into the heart of the home - a spacious reception room illuminated by Juliet-balcony-fronted French windows. This bright and versatile area offers flexible space for both lounge and dining zones, openly connected to the kitchen - ideal for everyday living and relaxed entertaining. The kitchen features stylish wood-toned cabinets with matching worktops and comes equipped with integrated appliances, including a fridge freezer and washer/dryer (under maintenance contract until Jan 2026), plus a brand new oven, gas hob, chimney hood and fan, against a stainless steel splashback.

I would also confirm that there is no dish washer at the property, however, the integrated oven, gas hob, cooker hood and fan are all brand new and not been used, the fridge freezer and washer/dryer have a maintenance contract in place until January 2026, the new central heating boiler was installed in July 2023 with annual maintenance carried out, all maintenance records and user manuals will also be left at the property.
Both double bedrooms come with mirrored fitted wardrobes, while throughout the home, there is scope to introduce your own choice of flooring for an added personal touch. A modern bathroom with a bath and overhead shower completes the accommodation, and gas central heating together with double glazing ensure year-round warmth and energy efficiency.
Outside, the development is arranged around shared courtyard gardens landscaped with greenery, providing a pleasant outdoor space for residents to enjoy. There is also an underground car park, with an allocated space included in the sale.

Area
Granton, Edinburgh

Once a major industrial hub driven by a busy port, Granton, like much of Edinburgh's shoreline, has seen significant redevelopment in recent years. The area, which lies under four miles north of the city centre, is fast becoming one of the most sought-after postcodes in the capital owing to luxurious residential developments and stunning views of the Firth of Forth. Although much of old Granton has now disappeared, its quaint harbour and listed industrial landmarks are charming reminders of its rich and vibrant heritage. Granton is served by a superb range of local services and amenities including a large Morrisons supermarket and petrol station, a 24-hour ASDA in neighbouring Newhaven and Ocean Terminal shopping centre, which is home to an array of high-street stores, a multi-screen cinema and a selection of family restaurants. With a wide variety of leisure activities right on its doorstep, Granton has something for everyone: from tranquil strolls along the waterfront promenade towards picturesque Cramond to exhilarating sailing and water sports at the marina. Granton is within the catchment area for well-regarded state schools and is well placed for some of the finest independent schools in the country. Thanks to its northerly location, the area enjoys swift and easy access to Edinburgh City Bypass, Edinburgh Airport and Queensferry Crossing. Comprehensive public transport services and a vast cycle path network also provide fantastic links into the city centre and beyond.
