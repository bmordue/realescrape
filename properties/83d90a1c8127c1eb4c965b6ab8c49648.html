Offers over £255,000

Terraced House

3 Bedrooms

Welcome to 6 Old Dalmore Gardens. This lovely property could be just what you're looking for. McDougall McQueen are delighted to present to the market this three-bedroom mid-terraced family home, forming part of an exclusive modern development built on the site of a former paper mill. Designed to take full advantage of its leafy riverbank setting, the property offers a spacious, contemporary interior and has been finished to a high specification throughout. The home benefits from gas central heating, double glazing, a sunny south-facing rear garden, an allocated parking space, and additional visitor parking. Ideal for first-time buyers, professional couples, and families alike. Early viewing is highly recommended. Sought-after modern residential development ideally located for access to the Old Railway Line, great for walking, running, and cycling between Penicuik and Bonnyrigg/Dalkeith. Welcoming hallway with staircase to the upper level. Spacious living room with built-in under-stair storage, acoustic panelling feature wall and a peaceful front-facing view. Modern fitted dining kitchen with French doors to the rear garden, gas hob, extractor hood, oven and integrated dishwasher. Free-standing white goods available by negotiation. Ground floor WC. Upper hallway with loft access and airing cupboard. Principal double bedroom with built-in wardrobe and front-facing window. En-suite shower room, recently resealed and maintained to a high standard. Second double bedroom with rear-facing window. Third bedroom with front-facing window. Family bathroom with three-piece white suite, including bath with shower attachment, WC and sink. The bathroom is also well-suited for internal drying, with a pulley system and rods available by agreement. Double glazing and gas central heating with Nest thermostat. Private rear garden, perfect for outdoor entertaining and ready for planting, with strawberries, herbs, and other plants left for your enjoyment. Allocated parking space and visitor parking available.
