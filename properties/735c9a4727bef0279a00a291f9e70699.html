5 Tannery Brae, Gatehouse of Fleet, CASTLE
DOUGLAS DG7 2LQ

Offers over £85,000

Ground Floor Flat

1 Bedroom

5 Tannery Brae is a well-appointed one bedroom ground floor flat in the centre of the picturesque town of Gatehouse of Fleet. In excellent walk-in condition it is ideally situated for all local amenities. With good sized accommodation, private parking space to the front and low maintenance, secluded shared garden to the rear of the property 5 Tannery Brae would make a comfortable home for the first time buyer or a fantastic holiday home or buy-to-let property.

Currently run as a popular holiday let (gatehouseingalloway.co.uk) it offers an excellent investment opportunity having 39 weeks occupancy in 2023. Furnishings and appliances may be available under separate negotiation.

Gatehouse of Fleet is a quiet town with a strong sense of community. There are several high quality hotels and local shops. The town has its own doctor's surgery and primary school and many leisure activities including golf course, tennis court, cricket pitch and bowling green. Central to Gatehouse is the leafy Garries Park, where there are numerous walks and a children's play area. Right in the heart of Dumfries & Galloway, Gatehouse is situated to make the most of all the region has to offer. In addition, easy access is available to the A75 trunk road, which runs from the East with Dumfries some 35 miles East, the M6 motorway 55 miles and Edinburgh and Glasgow approximately 100 miles distant, to Stranraer in the West.
