Flat 9, George House, Hardgate,
HADDINGTON EH41 3JN

Offers over £125,000

First Floor Flat

1 Bedroom

Nestled in a prime central location in Haddington, this delightful and sunlit one bedroom first floor flat is conveniently positioned just off the High Street. Set back from the thoroughfare, it offers a unique retreat close to the town centre. Housed in the iconic former George Hotel, the building was thoughtfully transformed into modern flats in 2018, adhering to the latest fire safety regulations and equipped with a sprinkler system for added peace of mind. Accessed via a spacious and elegantly curved staircase, this flat is one of only three units in the stairwell, ensuring a sense of community and privacy. The interior has been meticulously modernised with tasteful neutral tones and boasts energy-efficient LED lighting and plantation shutters throughout. With full gas central heating, the flat remains cozy and economical to heat. Upon entering, you are greeted by a welcoming hallway featuring a secure entry-phone system and a central heating control. A generously sized storage cupboard conveniently houses the gas and electric meters, gas boiler, fuse board and Openreach phone/internet connection. At the heart of the home, the bright living room showcases a distinctive curved wall and a large window that offers a picturesque open outlook adorned with working plantation shutters. This inviting space seamlessly flows into the well-equipped modern kitchen. The fully fitted kitchen is designed for both style and functionality, featuring ample storage with sleek base and wall units. It comes complete with a large integrated fridge freezer, an electric oven and hob with an extractor fan, and an integrated washer-dryer. A window with plantation shutters and a deep sill adds to the charm, creating a perfect spot for a window seat and providing a wonderful view. The contemporary shower room is finished to a high standard and includes a walk-in shower, a chrome heated towel rail, a stylish wash-hand basin with a vanity unit, and a soft-touch illuminated vanity mirror. Offering a tranquil and private outlook to the rear, the double bedroom features a spacious built-in wardrobe and inviting opening plantation shutters.
