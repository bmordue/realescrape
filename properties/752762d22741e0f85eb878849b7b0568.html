The Rhodes, Rhodes Street,
Dunfermline KY11 4TA

Offers over £600,000

Detached House

6 Bedrooms

A truly historic home, The Rhodes is believed to be the oldest inhabited property in Dunfermline, built approximately 1695 and extended around 1900

A rare opportunity to acquire a substantial and beautifully presented detached residence in the heart of the town, with six bedrooms, versatile living space and an exceptional blend of period charm and modern comfort, this Grade 2 listed property is perfectly suited to contemporary family living.

The ground floor opens with an impressive Edwardian sun room with garden views, the internal hallway has an under-stair storage cupboard, a boot room with hand basin and fitted shelving and access into the bespoke kitchen including a four oven Aga, an integrated Smeg dishwasher, pulley system and Belfast sink as well as a dedicated utility area. Within the stunning sitting/dining room you will find a feature fireplace with wood-burning stove, cast-iron radiators, under-window storage, press cupboard, walk-in storage cupboard and double door access to the rear garden. The three-piece, partially tiled bathroom has a roll top bath with hand-held shower.

On the first floor, there is an east and a west wing. The east wing includes two double bedrooms, one with a feature fireplace and built-in wardrobe, and the other with a walk-in wardrobe, roll top bath and a three-piece, partially tiled en-suite shower-room. As well as a single bedroom, there are a further two double bedrooms in the west wing, one with a feature and built-in storage, the other with two walk in wardrobes and press cupboard. Finally, there is a three-piece, partially tiled shower-room.

On the second floor you will find a spacious double bedroom with access into the property's eaves and leads into an optional home office or dressing room, which is currently used as a music room.

There are several attic spaces within the property, all offering more than adequate additional storage space.

The property is fitted with a mix of both single glazed and double glazed windows and gas central heating, with the boiler having been upgraded approx. 2022 and includes under-floor heating in all bathrooms. Externally, the home is surrounded by mature private gardens, including cherry, apple, plum and pear trees and summer house. A detached garage and driveway provide excellent off-street parking.

Location

The Rhodes is located in a central and sought-after area of Dunfermline. The property is ideally positioned to benefit from the excellent amenities Dunfermline has to offer, including a wide range of shops, restaurants, cafes and cultural attractions. There are reputable primary and secondary schools within easy reach, and the area benefits from superb transport links, including a mainline railway station and convenient road access to Edinburgh, the M90 motorway and beyond.

Fixtures and Fittings

Four oven Aga, fridge/freezer, integrated dishwasher and all fitted floor coverings, blinds, curtains and light fittings are included in the sale. Please note that other items may be available through separate negotiation.
