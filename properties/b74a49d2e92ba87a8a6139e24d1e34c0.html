The Old School House, Auchenlochan,
Tighnabruaich PA21 2BE

Offers over £160,000

Detached House

4 Bedrooms

Closing Date - Tuesday 9th September 2025 at 12.00.

Corrigall Black presents to the market 'The Old School House', a rare opportunity to acquire a spacious home in one of Argyll's most desirable coastal areas, the village of Tighnabruaich. This project property is brimming with character and potential and combining that with the beautiful setting makes it a standout option either as a permanent residence or a special holiday retreat.

'The Old School House' is positioned on an elevated plot with substantial garden grounds and enjoys breathtaking views across the stunning Kyles of Bute. Stairs rise to the entrance porch with attractive colourful feature glazing providing an ideal space to store outdoor clothing, perfectly suited for those wanting to make the most of the area's outstanding coastal walks and rural pursuits. From the main entrance the interior unfolds into a traditional, multi-level layout comprising three double bedrooms located on the upper floor and a further single bedroom and bathroom on the mid-level, although there is ample potential to add further bathroom facilities in this substantial family sized home, subject to planning and renovation. The principle living space is a bright, spacious lounge that enjoys wonderful views across the water, whilst a second sitting room provides an additional space for a more intimate retreat. The kitchen retains a traditional feel and is supported by a walk-in pantry, offering excellent storage space and scope to reconfigure. A separate utility room to the rear provides a further substantial space ideal for laundry and household tasks when upgraded. A door located next to the utility room leads out to the rear garden grounds. Whilst the property requires substantial upgrading throughout its scale, setting and historic character make it a truly special prospect. This wonderful home combines heritage, location and potential in equal measure and we highly recommend early viewing to avoid disappointment.


Location

Nestled on the eastern shores of the Cowal Peninsula Tighnabruaich is a coastal village steeped in nautical history and rugged beauty, offering sweeping sea views, wooded hills and a tranquil ambience. Tighnabruaich retains its Victorian-era character while functioning as a sought-after and peaceful retreat on what's known as "Argyll's Secret Coast". The centre of the village is a short walk from the shore, offering local shops, gallery, eateries, primary school, shinty field and a modern G.P surgery. The Victorian pier still welcomes the P.S. Waverley in the summer, a truly fantastic sight in such an impressive setting. A thriving sailing community, supported by a sailing school and a nine-hole golf course, are also available.
