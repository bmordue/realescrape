55 Pitairlie Road, Newbigging, Dundee DD5 3RH is a delightful one-bedroom end-terraced cottage that perfectly combines rural charm with modern convenience. Situated in the heart of the sought-after village of Newbigging, just a short drive from the coastal town of Monifieth and approximately two miles from Dundee, this recently upgraded home offers the perfect opportunity for first-time buyers, downsizers, or those looking for a peaceful retreat within easy reach of the city.

The accommodation is thoughtfully arranged over one level and comprises a welcoming entrance hallway that leads to a bright and inviting lounge. This cosy living space is enhanced by a charming log burner, creating the perfect spot to relax on cooler evenings. The well-appointed kitchen is offered with floor and wall mounted units required for modern living, while the shower room with WC has been tastefully renovated to a high standard. The generous bedroom completes the internal accommodation and offers a private retreat with a triple wardrobe for added convience.  An oil-fired central heating system and full double glazing ensure warmth and efficiency throughout the home.

Outside, the property boasts a well-maintained private rear garden, an enviable feature that offers a tranquil setting to entertain family/friends or simply taking in the peaceful surroundings. The home’s position at the end of the terrace enhances privacy and provides a sense of space rarely found in properties of this size.

Newbigging itself is a small yet thriving village surrounded by rolling Angus countryside, offering excellent walking routes and a friendly community atmosphere. The nearby Sidlaw Hills provide endless opportunities for outdoor pursuits, while Monifieth’s sandy beaches and Dundee’s cultural attractions, including the V&A Museum and the revitalised waterfront, are all within easy reach. Excellent road links and public transport connections ensure commuting and travel are straightforward, making this property as practical as it is charming.

This move-in-ready cottage is a rare find, offering a perfect blend of traditional character, modern upgrades, and a superb location. Early viewing is highly recommended to appreciate all it has to offer.
