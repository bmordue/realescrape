Dalbeattie DG5 4LQ

Offers over £265,000

Detached Bungalow

3 Bedrooms

75 Maxwell Park sits in desirable residential area on the edge of Dalbeattie and enjoys a partial view of the surrounding countryside. Dalbeattie offers primary and secondary schooling, shops, local food stores and a health centre. Walkers and mountain bikers are also well catered for in the region with the town woods just minutes away and the 7Stanes cycle tracks on hand in the Dalbeattie Forest, less than a mile away. The "Granite Town" of Dalbeattie is the gateway to The Solway Coast, and is 4 miles from the sailing village of Kippford, with the sandy beaches of Rockcliffe & Sandyhills just a few more minutes away.

This three bedroom, detached bungalow offers spacious and well-presented accommodation throughout. The property has garden grounds to the front and rear and benefits from multiple reception rooms, double glazing and gas central heating, with a new boiler having been installed earlier in 2025. A single garage and driveway complete the accommodation on offer. The property would make an ideal family home.
