Corrigall Black are delighted to present to the market, in true walk-in condition, this wonderful upper flat positioned in a sought after locale on the shore road in the popular seaside town of Dunoon. Boasting panoramic views over the River Clyde and beyond, this property is a true hidden gem. Having been thoughtfully maintained, upgraded and tastefully decorated throughout this home has the ideal combination of modern living and a tranquil setting. Comprising lounge, breakfasting kitchen, shower room, and two double bedrooms, the property also benefits from great storage options inside and out, DG and GCH with a new boiler fitted in 2025. This lovely home has a warm, welcoming, modern feel and will undoubtedly appeal to a wide variety of purchasers and investors. The property has the bonus of substantial outdoor space to the rear, where a fully renovated outbuilding and much sought-after off-road parking are also located. We would highly recommend early viewing to avoid disappointment

The Location
The property is situated on the beautiful East Bay of Dunoon, within walking distance of the passenger ferry to Gourock where train links can be accessed. The main shopping street is also a short walk away, with its unique array of shops, cafes, pubs, and restaurants. You can enjoy a pleasant stroll along the promenade and take in the stunning views and Dunoon also offers further amenities including supermarkets, cinema, schools, hospital, swimming pool and a wonderful arts venue at the Burgh Hall. The Queens Hall is host to a library, gym, soft play area and is popular for live music events. A regular vehicle ferry is approximately 1.5 miles from the property crossing to Gourock. The local area in steeped in history and, surrounded by hills and the sea, there is access to an array of outdoor pursuits on your doorstep. The area is popular for wild swimming, kayaking, paddle boarding and sailing. The popular Bishop's Glen has fantastic forestry trails enjoyed by hill walkers and runners, mountain bikers, families, and dog walkers.

Bedroom 1
