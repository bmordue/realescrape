Jonny Clifford with McEwan Fraser Legal is delighted to present this exceptional two-bedroom mid-terraced villa, which has been lovingly upgraded and meticulously maintained, offering a rare opportunity to acquire a truly turnkey home. With a brand-new roof, windows, and heating system all installed within the last two years, this property represents the perfect blend of timeless charm and modern practicality. Step inside and immediately feel the warmth and care poured into every corner of this home. The generously proportioned lounge is a standout space, elegantly styled in neutral tones and flooded with natural light from the expansive front window. It's an ideal setting for relaxing in comfort or entertaining guests. To the rear, the newly upgraded kitchen has been fitted with contemporary units, quality worktops, and stylish splashbacks. There is ample room for a dining table, and thoughtful design ensures every inch is functional and aesthetically pleasing. Whether you're preparing weekday meals or hosting dinner, this kitchen balances practicality with clean, modern finishes. Ascending the stairs, the upper level boasts two spacious double bedrooms, both of which have been tastefully decorated and provide excellent storage options. The rooms are bright, calm, and inviting, ideal for restful nights or peaceful home working. The family bathroom is equally impressive, fitted with a modern three-piece suite and finished with quality tiling and fixtures to create a sleek, polished look. Externally, the property continues to impress. The private rear patio enjoys a sunny aspect and provides a delightful space for outdoor dining, quiet mornings with a coffee, or summer evening barbecues. It's a low-maintenance haven, perfect for unwinding. In addition, the property benefits from a separate garage, ideal for additional storage, secure parking, or as a hobbyist's workshop, a rare and valuable feature for homes of this type. Whether you're a first-time buyer, a growing family, or an investor looking for a high-quality rental opportunity, this home offers space, substance, and style in equal measure. Early viewing is strongly recommended to fully appreciate the exceptional standard of accommodation on offer.
