End Terraced House

4 Bedrooms

This bright and spacious, four-bedroom, end-terrace house has a fantastic location in a quiet and leafy residential area, in the sought after Cammo district of Edinburgh, close to excellent transport links, schools, shops in nearby Barnton and the Gyle Shopping Centre a short drive away, as well as Cammo Estate and Cramond beach. The property would now benefit from some modernising and is being sold as seen. There is potential to reconfigure the layout and/or extend the property, subject to the usual permissions. The accommodation consists of a spacious entrance hall, with storage, a lovely, light filled lounge, with double aspect windows overlooking the leafy front garden, a fitted kitchen, with appliances and stunning views across the rear garden. There is also a dining room on this level, with patio doors opening into the garden and a cloakroom with w.c. On the upper floor there is a generous master bedroom, with a covered balcony, three further double bedrooms, two of which have built-in storage, and a shower room. There is a lovely garden to the front, with mature planting, a driveway and a garage. To the rear, there is an enclosed garden, with an area of lawn, a patio edged with raised beds and access to the attractive communal grounds to the rear of the houses. Cammo is an established, prime residential area situated to the west edge of Edinburgh with easy access to the city centre, commuting links and Edinburgh Airport. It is well served by a good selection of local amenities such as a local Co-op, Scotmid and Post Office and further amenities can be found at nearby Davidson's Mains, which has a Tesco Metro store. Excellent facilities are available at the nearby Gyle Shopping Centre where there are a number of large retailers including Marks and Spencer's and Morrison's. There are good public schools in the local vicinity which include Cramond Primary and the Royal High School in the state sector as well as the highly regarded Cargilfield Preparatory School which is situated close-by on Gamekeepers Road, Edinburgh Academy, St George's and Stewart's Melville.
