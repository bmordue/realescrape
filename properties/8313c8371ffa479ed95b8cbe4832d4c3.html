3 Braemar Avenue,
Dumfries DG1 3BQ

Offers over £138,000

Semi-Detached Bungalow

2 Bedrooms

3 Braemar Avenue is a traditional red sandstone built semi-detached bungalow located in a popular residential area of Dumfries. The property is within walking distance to the centre of town, a bus route and local amenities such as supermarkets, shops, primary and secondary schools, train station and medical centre. An Italian restaurant, takeaway and pub and located around the corner from the property.

Well presented, spacious two bedroom bungalow set in a corner plot benefiting from both front and rear gardens and off street parking for several cars. The property enjoys well-proportioned, high ceilinged rooms in excellent decorative order with a modern fitted kitchen and shower room with oak doors and skirting boards throughout. There is double glazing, gas central heating and a coal effect wall mounted electric heater in the lounge.

Viewing is recommended to appreciate the property in full.
