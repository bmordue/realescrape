Detached House

This beautiful deluxe 4/5 bedroom detached villa with double garage and home office is the perfect home for the growing family. Ideally located and featuring spacious and versatile accommodation, the ‘Azalea’ offers top of the range fixtures and fittings and this particular property also benefits from a multitude of additional features as added by the current owners.

On entering the house, a fabulous L shaped hallway gives access to the spacious lounge which features a boxed bay window and double doors that open to the dining area. A separate family room is a flexible space and could be used as 5th bedroom or playroom if required. The beautiful large open plan kitchen comes fully fitted with quality integrated appliances and a stylish island unit and has double doors leading out to the garden. Completing the accommodation on the ground floor is a utility room and a stylish shower room comprising quadrant enclosure, WC and wash hand basin.  Up the stairs are 4 large double bedrooms The magnificent principal bedroom has an en-suite with large shower enclosure, fitted wardrobes and opens out to a large sitting area with doors to the Juliette balcony. The three further bedrooms are bright, spacious and airy and a convenient Jack & Jill shower room is located between bedrooms 2 & 3. An impressive family bathroom, with both a bath and shower enclosure offers a touch of indulgence. Externally to the front, a monobloc driveway leads to a double garage and there is an area of lawn. The rear garden is enclosed with a further lawned area and a deck for al fresco entertaining. Finally, a fully insulated home office is situated in the rear garden.

This stunning family home must be viewed to appreciate the location and accommodation as well as all the extras that are available here.

Area

Crieff is a traditional Scottish market town set amidst Perthshire's stunning scenery. The attractive and bustling town centre supports a wide range of businesses offering the best of food and drink, clothing, gifts, crafts and arts. There is a great choice of cafes and restaurants for fine or casual dining. A popular destination for tourists, Crieff's attractions include the Visitor Centre and Glenturret Distillery. Local schooling is available at primary and secondary level and independent schooling is available at Morrison's Academy and Ardvreck.
