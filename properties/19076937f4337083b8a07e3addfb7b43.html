"Well presented, newly refurbished and decorated one bedroomed terraced house close to the town centre"

2 Burn Street is located in Dalbeattie town centre. Dalbeattie offers primary and secondary schooling, shops, local food stores and a health centre. Walkers and mountain bikers are also well catered for in the region with the town woods just minutes away and the 7Stanes cycle tracks on hand in the Dalbeattie Forest, less than a mile away. The "Granite Town" of Dalbeattie is the gateway to The Solway Coast, and is 4 miles from the sailing village of Kippford, with the sandy beaches of Rockcliffe & Sandyhills just a few more minutes away.

Well-presented traditional one bedroomed mid terraced house. The property benefits from electric heating and double glazing and has been recently redecorated and refurbished. The property would ideally suit a first time buyer or buy to let investor.
