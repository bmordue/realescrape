Quite possibly the best presented home in the area, 15 Dryburgh Crescent offers a superb opportunity to acquire a beautifully refurbished family home offered in true ‘turn key’ condition throughout. Extensively renovated both inside and out - with all necessary planning consents in place - this impressive property offers stylish family living across three levels.

Upon entering from the hallway you are welcomed into a bright and spacious lounge with a large window which allows masses of natural light. To the rear is the modern fitted kitchen, which features sleek cabinetry, quality appliances, and ample workspace for any keen home cook. A conveniently located ground floor WC completes the lower level.

Upstairs, the first floor comprises two generous double bedrooms, each offering excellent storage potential and comfortable proportions. The stylish family bathroom is finished to a high standard, with contemporary fittings and a fresh, elegant design that adds a touch of luxury to daily routines.

A standout feature of this home is the converted attic, which now serves as a spacious third bedroom. This versatile space is ideal as a master suite, guest bedroom, or even a home office, providing flexibility to suit a variety of lifestyles.

Externally, the quality continues. The private gardens have been meticulously landscaped and designed to offer a peaceful, low-maintenance retreat. Boasting raised planters, well maintained lawn,  charming fish pond, and a delightful timber summer house, the outdoor space is perfect for enjoying the warmer months, hosting garden gatherings, or simply unwinding in a tranquil setting. The front of the house provides a small garden area and private off street parking via the homes paved driveway.

15 Dryburgh Crescent in Dundee is situated within the popular residential district of Dryburgh, in the West of the city. The location offers convenient access to various local amenities. For shopping needs, residents can reach Tesco Dundee South Rd Extra and Asda Dundee West, both within approximately a mile. Healthcare facilities are also nearby, with Kingsway Care Centre and Ninewells Hospital located closeby. Families will find several primary schools in close proximity, including Camperdown Primary School and St Clement's RC Primary School, each less than a mile away. Secondary education is accessible as well, with St John's RC High School and Harris Academy within easy reach. The city centre of Dundee is about 2.5 miles away, providing additional shopping, dining, and cultural experiences. Public transportation options, such as nearby railway stations Invergowrie and Dundee, enhance connectivity to the broader region
