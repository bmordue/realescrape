48 Preston Crescent,
Prestonpans EH32 9RA

Offers over £225,000

Semi-Detached House

3 Bedrooms

Warners are delighted to present this charming semi-detached family home, perfectly positioned in the highly sought-after coastal town of Prestonpans. Boasting an ideal location within a popular and friendly community, this property offers an excellent opportunity for families, professionals, or anyone seeking a serene yet convenient lifestyle. Step inside to discover a spacious and welcoming living room, thoughtfully designed for both relaxation and entertaining guests. The well-appointed kitchen features modern base and wall units complemented by stylish contrasting wood work surfaces and a large range cooker. Ample space is provided for freestanding appliances as well as a cozy dining area. Flowing seamlessly from the kitchen, a bright and airy conservatory creates a versatile space perfect for dining, reading, or simply enjoying picturesque views of the garden. Completing the ground floor is a conveniently located bathroom with a full-sized bathtub, ideal for unwinding after a long day. Upstairs, the first floor offers three comfortable bedrooms. The generous master bedroom provides ample space for a luxurious suite, while bedrooms two and three benefit from built-in storage, making them ideal for children, guests, or a home office. Additional highlights include a long driveway accommodating multiple vehicles and a low-maintenance front garden featuring an Astroturf lawn. The rear garden is a superb sociable space, complete with a patio area, decking ideal for outdoor furniture, and easy-care chipstone landscaping, perfect for families and entertaining. There may also be potential to acquire additional garden space at the rear, subject to further investigation with East Lothian Council, as neighbouring properties have successfully done. Charming semi-detached home in popular costal town of Prestonpans. Spacious living room and modern kitchen with large range cooker. Bright conservatory offering versatile indoor-outdoor living space. Three comfortable bedrooms, two with built in storage. Long driveway for multiple vehicles and low-maintenance front and rear gardens. Potential to purchase additional garden space subject to council approval. EPC: D, Council Tax: C Extras include: fittings, fixtures and kitchen appliances (fridge freezer, washing machine, cooker). Please note: the electric fire will be removed prior to sale, and a gas fire reinstated.
