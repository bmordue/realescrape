Semi-Detached House

3 Bedrooms

Located in a popular residential area close to the edge of town, convenient for Dalbeattie Town Wood and the bypass. A public pathway situated next to the property provides access on to the High Street. The "Granite Town" of Dalbeattie has Dalbeattie Learning Campus which comprises high school, primary school and nursery, many independent shops, local food stores, places to eat, health centre, churches, Colliston Park with pond and a popular golf course. Walkers and mountain bikers are also well catered for in the region with the town woods and the internationally famous 7Stanes Mountain Bike Trail on hand. Dalbeattie is the gateway to the Solway Coast and is approximately 4 miles from the sailing village of Kippford, with the sandy beaches of Rockcliffe and Sandyhills just a few more minutes away. Dumfries is approximately 14 miles distant.

Well-presented, semi-detached family home with sizeable rear garden and off street parking. The property enjoys well-proportioned, light-filled rooms arranged over two floors and benefits from double glazing and gas central heating. The sellers have been the property's sole owners since it was built in 2005 and they are chain-free.

The welcoming hall with WC off and staircase to the first floor, gives access to the attractive, spacious, open-plan lounge and dining room with French doors to the rear garden. Adjoining this room is a modern well-appointed kitchen with integrated appliances and direct access to the side garden. Upstairs, the light-filled landing leads to two double bedrooms, each with built-in wardrobes, a single bedroom currently used as a study, all served by a modern fitted family shower room. Outside, the level gardens are laid largely to lawn with two paved seating areas.
