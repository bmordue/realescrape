Semi-Detached House

3 Bedrooms

Immaculately presented and beautifully maintained, this impressive three-bedroom semi-detached villa enjoys a highly desirable position within a peaceful cul-de-sac in one of Paisley's most sought-after modern developments. Perfectly positioned beside Gleniffer Braes Country Park, this home offers a rare blend of modern comfort, convenience, and outdoor lifestyle - ideal for families or professionals alike. Accommodation The bright and welcoming entrance hallway features a convenient ground floor WC and leads through to the stylish living spaces. The spacious lounge offers a calm and inviting setting, perfect for family relaxation or entertaining guests, complemented by elegant décor and a large window that fills the room with natural light. To the rear, the modern dining kitchen is fitted with contemporary units, a breakfast bar, integrated oven and hob, and ample space for appliances including a washing machine and fridge freezer. There's plenty of room for family dining, making it the heart of the home. A standout feature is the fantastic conservatory/garden room, offering an additional living space flooded with light. French doors open directly to the enclosed rear garden, creating a seamless flow between indoor and outdoor living. Upstairs, there are three well-proportioned bedrooms, each with fitted wardrobes for excellent storage. The luxury shower room has been upgraded to a high standard with a walk-in rainfall shower and stylish contemporary tiling. Outside, the property benefits from a multi-car driveway to the front, while the enclosed rear garden provides a private haven with a patio area, stone-chipped section, and low-maintenance artificial lawn - perfect for relaxing or family playtime. Location - Living in Paisley Located on the south side of Paisley, this property offers a peaceful suburban lifestyle while remaining close to all amenities. Residents can enjoy Gleniffer Braes Country Park just moments away - perfect for walks, cycling, and scenic views across Renfrewshire and beyond. Paisley itself is a vibrant town with excellent shopping, dining, and leisure facilities, as well as highly regarded primary and secondary schools. The town benefits from excellent transport links, including nearby Paisley Gilmour Street Station, frequent bus routes, and easy access to the M8 motorway, making travel to Glasgow Airport or Glasgow city centre quick and convenient.
