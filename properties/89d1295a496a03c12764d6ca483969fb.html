Offering arguably the best positioned plot in one of Dundee’s most desirable developments, this simply exceptional fourth-floor penthouse apartment presents a truly rare purchase opportunity. Built by the highly regarded developers H&H Properties, Riverside Apartments is a 202-unit flagship development that contributes to Dundee waterfront’s impressive £1bn regeneration project. The exclusive development provides residents with private parking, video-controlled security entry, and modern lift access to each floor.

Upon entering the apartment, your eyes are immediately drawn to the impressive open-plan lounge/kitchen/dining area. Floor-to-ceiling windows flood the room with natural light and provide breathtaking panoramic river views. An oak and glass balustrade leads up to a raised mezzanine, offering a highly adaptable space perfect for a home office or additional sitting area. The chic, contemporary kitchen is of the highest quality, featuring integrated Neff appliances including a dishwasher, fridge freezer, washer dryer, microwave, quadruple oven, and induction hob. Under-cabinet and shelving lighting add to the luxurious feel.

Currently, the home is configured as a one-bedroom apartment, but there is potential for it to be easily converted into a spacious two-bedroom layout. The primary bedroom is exceptionally well-sized, resembling a suite from a luxury hotel. It provides stunning river views, a further mezzanine level with fitted wardrobes, and a tiled ensuite shower room with high-quality white sanitaryware and chrome fittings. A family bathroom, featuring a bathtub with a shower overhead, matches the same top-of-the-range finishings as the ensuite, completing the accommodation.

Externally, a sheltered balcony runs the entire length of the apartment on the south side of the building and can be accessed from both the bedroom and the living/dining area. These larger balcony terraces are exclusive to the penthouse-style apartments and offer some of the best vistas in the city. Warmth is provided via an electric underfloor heating system with mechanical ventilation and heat recovery. Parking is included in the apartment’s private garage, along with an additional covered parking space.

From the beautiful tiled floors to the electrically operated window coverings, it is evident that attention to detail was paramount for both the current owners and developers in creating this truly wonderful property.

Ideally positioned, the property offers easy access to Dundee’s vibrant West End and bustling City Centre, as well as both Dundee and Abertay Universities. Dundee, located on the east coast on the Firth of Tay estuary, was recently voted by the Sunday Times as one of the top twelve places to live in Britain. A historic port and now a UNESCO City of Design, Dundee is brimming with culture. It boasts a regenerated waterfront with two nautical museums—RRS Discovery, Captain Scott’s Antarctic expedition ship, and the 19th-century warship, HM Frigate Unicorn—and the architectural triumph of the newly opened V&A Dundee, Scotland’s first design museum. Further museums, arts centres, and theatres can be found across the city, along with a vibrant array of bars and restaurants. The city also offers a wildlife centre, cinemas, and excellent sports and leisure facilities. Largely walkable, Dundee provides a delightful and convenient shopping experience, hosting a wide selection of high street retail outlets, shopping centres—one with a large public library—and supermarkets. Education is provided at all levels, with both private and state institutions, and one of the UK’s leading universities. Major road and rail links connect Dundee to the rest of Scotland and beyond, while Dundee Airport offers flights to London City Airport.

This exceptional penthouse offers luxury living with stunning views, high-quality finishes, and convenient access to all that Dundee has to offer.
