This beautifully renovated detached bungalow enjoys a peaceful setting within a quiet cul-de-sac in the popular town of Forfar. The property has undergone extensive modernisation and upgrading to create a superb family home, presented in true walk-in condition throughout.

Forfar itself provides an excellent range of local amenities including shops, supermarkets, cafés, leisure facilities and schooling at both primary and secondary levels. The town also offers good transport links to the surrounding area, with the nearby A90 dual carriageway giving swift access to Dundee and Aberdeen.

Internally, the accommodation has been thoughtfully redesigned to offer contemporary family living. An inviting entrance vestibule opens into a bright hallway, leading to a generously proportioned lounge with a west-facing aspect, allowing natural light to flood the space. The newly fitted dining kitchen, accessed from the lounge, is the heart of the home and is equipped with a high-quality range of modern units and integrated appliances. With ample space for a family dining table and direct access to the rear garden, this room provides a perfect setting for both everyday living and entertaining.

There are three well-presented bedrooms, all finished to an excellent standard, with the master bedroom further enhanced by a stylish en suite shower room. A luxurious family bathroom, fitted with contemporary sanitaryware and premium wet-wall, completes the internal layout. Excellent storage is available throughout.

Externally, the property continues to impress. The front garden offers extensive private parking options with a selection of mature plants and shrubs. To the rear, the south-east facing garden offers a private outdoor retreat, laid mainly to lawn with a newly laid patio area to the side of the house the surrounding gardens offer a private sheltered retreat. A large garage to the side of the property provides additional storage and parking options.

This outstanding home represents a rare opportunity to acquire a fully modernised bungalow in a sought-after residential setting and is ideally suited to family buyers seeking style, comfort and convenience in equal measure.
