Semi-Detached Bungalow

1 Bedroom

Well presented, semi-detached bungalow with off street parking and easily managed gardens at the front and rear. The property enjoys well proportioned, light filled rooms with the lounge and bedroom each enjoying a double aspect. The fitted dining kitchen has a walk-in storage cupboard and direct access to the rear garden. It has a modern fitted shower room and excellent storage throughout. The property also benefits from double glazing, gas fired central heating with an electric fire in the lounge.

Gordon Drive is an established, residential area in the northeast of the town of Castle Douglas convenient for supermarkets and all of the town's amenities. Castle Douglas is a popular market town with a good range of independent shops, primary and secondary schools, park with loch, churches, theatre, swimming pool and all other facilities commensurate with a town of its size.
