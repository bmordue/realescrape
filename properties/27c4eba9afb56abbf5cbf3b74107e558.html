Touchwood, Failford,
Mauchline KA5 5TF

Offers over £285,000

Detached Bungalow

3 Bedrooms

"Touchwood" - a most appealing Modern Detached Bungalow enjoying a wonderful countryside setting, discreetly positioned on country lane, a short distance from the centre of Failford by Mauchline, a small "hamlet" of mixed style homes.

A comfortable retreat for its owners over many years who have enjoyed its semi-rural location/wonderful views, yet convenient for access to surrounding Ayrshire towns - approx' 9 miles from Ayr/8.5 miles from Prestwick & 2.5 miles from Mauchline.

Touchwood is neatly presented, featuring 6 on-the-level Main Apartments, with the owners having consistently maintained the property, most recently fully re-roofed. Focal points are the delightful elevated woodland landscape views to the front, countryside backdrop to the rear whilst the balcony doors/patio from the lounge enable one to "step into" the landscape.

The excellent accommodation offers flexibility of use, reception hall, lounge to the front with semi-open plan dining room to the rear - access from here into the charming larger size conservatory which is a wonderful additional living space in which to relax, kitchen to the rear whilst 3 double bedrooms are provided (Nos 1 & 2 to the front & No 3 to rear). A bathroom and adjacent "Guests WC" are located off the hallway.

The specification includes both gas (LPG) central heating and double glazing. In addition there is a wood-burning stove fire in the lounge. There is a septic tank private to this property (within its boundaries). EPC - E. Attic storage is available. A sloping driveway provides private off-lane parking with a further level gated driveway beyond. A much-valued detached private garage provides secure parking/storage adjacent to the driveway. Mature private gardens to the front & rear provide outdoor space in which to relax or potter.

South Ayrshire remains ever popular with its thriving variety of town centres whilst Ayr, Prestwick & Troon boast their own sweeping promenade/seafront together with a variety of famous golf courses. Touchwood will be of appeal to both local buyers and Glasgow based buyers seeking a semi-rural location which still allows easy commuting ...noting that Glasgow is accessible (approx' 30 miles) either by car or train/bus service.

In our opinion... a wonderful opportunity to acquire a charming semi-rural home, featuring a desirable 6 Main Apartment layout ...all framed by the picturesque countryside views. Quite simply ...a joy to come home to.
