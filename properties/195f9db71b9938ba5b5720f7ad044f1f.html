5 Middleton Park,
Kelty KY4 0GZ

Offers over £525,000

Detached House

4 Bedrooms

An outstanding executive detached villa located within an exclusive development of nineteen prestigious homes. The bespoke builders (Thomson Homes) built these properties to the highest specification to create the ideal family home with oak finishes throughout and high quality fixtures and fittings. The property is a credit to the present owners who have upgraded the property with show home flair, offering contemporary living with stylish decoration. The external and internal finishes are of the highest standard with unique features throughout including underfloor heating on the ground floor and in all bathrooms. The property further benefits additional features with "smart home" hardwire complete with full house audio system, broadband and television points. There is a full security system and further features include integral vacuum system. The landscaped gardens are south facing with woodland backdrop which sets this property aside as offers a high degree of privacy, an idyllic haven offering a child and pet safe environment. It is also adjacent to open land and is therefore not overlooked. The accommodation briefly comprises, reception hall, w.c, lounge, dining room, fitted kitchen/family room and separate utility room. On the upper level a gallery landing leads to four double bedrooms (all with built in wardrobes) two en-suite facilities and family bathroom. The double garage has electric doors and driveway for several vehicles, power and light within. The property is double glazed with gas central heating. Internal viewing is highly recommended to fully appreciate this stunning family home architecturally designed offering distinction in the current market.
