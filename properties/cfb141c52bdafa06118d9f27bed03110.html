1 Nether Kirkton House,
Glasgow G78 3QQ

Offers over £259,999

Ground Floor Flat

3 Bedrooms

This garden apartment occupies the entire ground floor of Nether Kirkton House and is a fabulously unique property with great sized rooms and an impressive specification. The property is entered from ground level via a private front door and also has a back door leading to the grounds as well. The living room occupies a superb corner position at the front of the building with dual aspects and is a lovely room to relax and receive guests. The dining kitchen is of excellent quality and ideal for creating some culinary delights. The appliances include twin Neff ovens with slide and hide doors, Neff combination microwave and oven, warming drawer, Neff gas hob, boiling water tap, wine cooler, dishwasher and an American style fridge freezer. The separate utility room is a great room to have and has a back door to the gardens. There is a second sink in this room as well as room for laundry appliances and a second freezer. The three bedrooms are all great sizes and bedroom one has an en suite shower room. Completing the accommodation is the luxurious shower room which has an illuminated mirror and three piece suite including a double sized shower cubicle and beautiful Porcelanosa tiling. The building sits in well maintained communal grounds and also benefits from residents parking. One of the other notable aspect of the property is that it has a private area of the garden which could be developed into a private garden space.
