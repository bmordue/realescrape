Detached House

3 Bedrooms

Tucked away in an elevated position within a peaceful cul-de-sac in the ever-popular Thornly Park area of Paisley, this beautifully maintained detached villa offers the perfect setting for growing families ready to put down roots. Bright, welcoming and ready to move straight into, this is a home designed for easy everyday living with plenty of space to grow. Step inside to a welcoming reception hallway with convenient ground floor WC - ideal for busy family mornings. The generous lounge is filled with natural light from the front-facing window and features a charming dining nook, perfect for cosy family meals, weekend brunches or homework time while dinner is on the go. A second flexible public room on the ground floor offers wonderful versatility - whether you need a playroom, formal dining room, snug, or a dedicated home office, this adaptable space evolves with your family's needs. The kitchen provides a range of fitted units, space for everyday appliances and a practical serving hatch through to the dining nook, making entertaining and family life effortless. Upstairs, there are three well-proportioned bedrooms and a stylish family shower room. Bedrooms one and two benefit from bespoke built-in storage solutions with automatic illumination - a thoughtful touch that keeps everything organised and clutter-free. The luxurious shower room features a walk-in shower cubicle and a useful drying cupboard, ideal for busy households. Outside, the home continues to impress. A garage and stone-chipped driveway provide off-street parking, while the south-facing rear garden is mainly laid to lawn - a suntrap in the warmer months and the perfect space for children to play. The charming 'sit-oot-erie' means you can enjoy the garden whatever the Scottish weather decides to do, making spring and summer evenings something to look forward to. Living in Paisley Located just minutes from the heart of Paisley, Thornly Park is a well-established and highly regarded residential area popular with families. Paisley offers an excellent selection of primary and secondary schools, local parks, family-friendly amenities and a vibrant town centre with shops, cafés and leisure facilities. For commuters, there are excellent transport links with regular rail services to Glasgow in under 15 minutes, and easy access to the M8 motorway network. University of the West of Scotland also adds to the town's energy and investment appeal. With green spaces, a strong community feel and superb connectivity, Paisley continues to be a smart choice for families looking for both lifestyle and convenience.
