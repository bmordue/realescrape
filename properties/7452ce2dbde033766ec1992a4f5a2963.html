5 Carron Place, Broughty
Ferry DD5 3HR

Offers Over £350,000

Detached House

4 Bedrooms

Nestled on a generous corner plot at the end of a tranquil cul-de-sac in the heart of Broughty Ferry, this exquisite four-bedroom detached villa has been impeccably upgraded and modernized throughout. Offered in true move in condition the property is equipped with full double glazing, gas central heating and fresh contemporary décor.

The spacious and versatile accommodation is spread over two levels. On the ground floor, you will find a bright and well-appointed lounge featuring a wood-burning stove. The open-plan dining room and kitchen are stunning, with dark grey shaker-style cabinets, contrasting worktops and fully integrated appliances.  Bi-fold doors flood the room with a mass of natural light and open onto the spacious and private rear garden grounds. The ground level also includes two double bedrooms, each with fitted wardrobes, and a family bathroom with a shower over the bath.

The upper landing houses a wonderfully convenient utility cupboard and provides access to the delightful master bedroom itself, offering excellent eaves storage and a stylish en-suite shower room.

A fourth spacious double bedroom with eaves storage completes the upper floor accommodation.

Externally, this beautiful home occupies a secluded and spacious corner site at the end of the cul-de-sac. The driveway accommodates multiple vehicles and leads to a well-maintained timber garage.

The beautifully presented rear garden, offers a spacious lawn, large decked area and an attractive patio. Tree lined and incredibly private this stunning space enjoys sun throughout the day making it the ideal place for entertaining friends and family during the summer months.

Broughty Ferry gracefully rests at the mouth of the River Tay, offering a unique charm that harmonizes historical heritage with contemporary conveniences. Its picturesque waterfront, adorned with charming shops, cafes, and restaurants, makes it a delightful spot for both locals and visitors. The iconic Broughty Castle stands proudly, overlooking the estuary and providing insight into the area's rich maritime history. On sunny days, the sandy beach is perfect for relaxation, while nearby nature reserves offer opportunities for exploration and wildlife observation. Broughty Ferry effortlessly blends the tranquillity of coastal living with the convenience of urban amenities, making it a desirable destination for those seeking a peaceful yet vibrant lifestyle.

Nearby Dundee, Scotland's fourth largest city, boasts all the amenities of a vibrant, regenerated urban centre. Highlights include retail parks, the V&A Design Museum, and the upcoming Eden Project. The city also features a mainline railway station and a domestic airport, both offering daily connections to southern England and beyond.
