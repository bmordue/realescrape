Presented in immaculate condition throughout, this stylish ground floor apartment offers modern, low-maintenance living in a sought-after setting, making it an ideal choice for downsizers seeking comfort and convenience or young professionals looking for a well-connected home with contemporary appeal. Built by Kier Homes, the property forms part of the popular Hawkhead Village development, positioned at the entrance to the development on Hawkhead Road. With Hawkhead Train Station just a short walk away, the apartment is perfectly placed for commuters, offering quick and easy connections to Glasgow city centre and beyond, while also benefiting from a peaceful residential setting. A broad and welcoming reception hallway creates an excellent first impression, complete with useful storage cupboards that enhance the practical layout. The main living space is bright and spacious, designed with modern lifestyles in mind, offering ample room to relax or entertain. Patio doors lead directly to a private balcony, creating a lovely spot to enjoy a morning coffee or unwind at the end of the day. The contemporary open-plan kitchen flows seamlessly from the lounge and is fitted with a range of stylish units and integrated appliances, providing both functionality and a sociable space for everyday living. There are two double bedrooms, both benefiting from fitted wardrobes, while the principal bedroom further enjoys the luxury of its own en suite shower room. A well-appointed main bathroom, finished with a modern three-piece suite, completes the accommodation. Residents also benefit from private parking, adding further convenience to this attractive home. There are some contents/furniture that may be available which would be under separate negotiations. About the Area Paisley has become an increasingly popular place to call home, offering a vibrant mix of historic character, excellent amenities, and superb transport links. From its striking architecture and renowned cultural attractions to a fantastic selection of cafés, restaurants, shopping facilities and green spaces, Paisley provides an excellent quality of life. Its close proximity to Glasgow Airport, easy access to the motorway network, and regular rail services make it particularly appealing for professionals, while its strong sense of community and abundance of local amenities continue to attract those looking to downsize without compromise.
