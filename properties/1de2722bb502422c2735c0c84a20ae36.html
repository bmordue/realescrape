67 Rose Hip Crescent, Larbert,
Falkirk FK5 4ZP

Offers over £170,000

Terraced House

2 Bedrooms

McEwan Fraser Legal welcomes 67 Rose Hip Crescent to the market! This delightful 2-bed terraced house is perfect for first-time buyers, small families, or anyone seeking a comfortable and stylish home in a friendly community. As you enter the property, you are greeted by a welcoming hallway with stairs leading to the next level. The ground floor features a beautifully decorated living room that is bright, fresh, and clean, offering a snug and inviting atmosphere.

The modern kitchen is a standout, boasting stunning tiling, a crisp and bright ambience, and ample space for a dining table and chairs. The kitchen is equipped with an integrated oven and provides access to a private, well-maintained garden, ideal for outdoor dining and relaxation. For added convenience, there is a freshly painted downstairs WC, complete with a toilet and basin on this level.

Moving upstairs, you will find two bedrooms and a family bathroom. One of the bedrooms benefits from fitted wardrobes, offering plenty of storage space. The bathroom is clean, fresh, and features modern tiling. It comprises a three-piece suite, including a toilet, wash hand basin, and a bath with a shower overhead.

The property boasts stunning external views, overlooking the surrounding green with rolling hills in the distance, providing a serene and picturesque setting. Situated in a community-spirited area, this home offers a warm and welcoming environment for families and individuals alike.

This property is in walk-in condition and the perfect starter home for young couples or families. Being just a 5-minute drive away from Larbert Train Station it is perfect for the commuter too.

Early viewing is highly recommended.
