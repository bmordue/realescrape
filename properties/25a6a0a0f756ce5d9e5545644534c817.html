4 Maryfield Drive, Bo'Ness, West
Lothian EH51 9DG

Offers over £395,000

Detached House

4 Bedrooms

This light-filled, detached contemporary villa (190 sq m), offers spacious and versatile accommodation with wonderful views to the River Forth to the north and a mature, south facing garden and country views to the rear. The properties have been thoughtfully designed for privacy outside and flexible space inside with sliding and folding doors to give the choice of open plan living if desired. The interior offers many striking features on both floors.

The unique and award winning "Maryfield", forms part of The Drum, a modern development on the fringes of the coastal town of Bo'ness and just over a 5 minute drive to Linlithgow with its mainline station. There is easy access to the M9 on ramp to the airport, a 15 minute drive away and onwards to Edinburgh and the 909 bus offers a direct service to Stirling, the airport and Edinburgh.

The area is rich in history with Kinneil House, the House of the Binns and Linlithgow Palace. Blackness Castle is a short drive away but can also be accessed via the John Muir Way, within short walking distance of the property.

The spacious hall leads to the 3 public rooms: a large living room with a feature granite fireplace and living flame gas fire and a spacious dining room, both south facing and with French doors to the large deck. A sliding cherry wood door from the living room leads to the family room with folding double doors to the fitted kitchen and utility room. The cloaks/WC completes the ground floor accommodation.

A carpeted timber staircase leads to the upper floor with open aspects at the half landing, a vaulted ceiling and onwards to the open library with wonderful views to the River Forth.

There are 4 double bedrooms.

The south-facing main bedroom has a vaulted ceiling, a dressing area with twin bi-fold mirrored door wardrobes, twin glazed shelf display areas and a further wardrobe. The en-suite bathroom leads off with a recessed wash hand basin with vanity storage, WC, double tray shower cubicle and bath.

The guest bedroom offers views to the countryside to the south and has an en-suite shower room.

The 2 further double bedrooms both have fitted wardrobes and are also south facing.

There is a 4-piece family bathroom which also has a cupboard housing the tank.

In addition, there is a vast attic which is partly floored.
