14 New Station Road is located in a popular, quiet residential area on the edge of Dalbeattie but is within easy walking distance of a range of amenities. Dalbeattie offers primary and secondary schooling, shops, local food stores and a health centre. Walkers and mountain bikers are also well catered for in the region with the town woods just minutes away and the 7Stanes cycle tracks on hand in the Dalbeattie Forest, less than a mile away. The "Granite Town" of Dalbeattie is the gateway to The Solway Coast, and is 4 miles from the sailing village of Kippford, with the sandy beaches of Rockcliffe & Sandyhills just a few more minutes away.

This two bedroom, mid terraced house offers well-presented accommodation throughout. It benefits from double glazing, two reception rooms and gas central heating, with a new boiler having just been installed. There are garden grounds to the front and rear and off street residents' parking is available. The property would ideally suit a first time buyer, a small family or a buy to let investor.

Viewing is highly recommended to appreciate the accommodation on offer.
