2 Pedwarden Road,
Perth PH2 7JW

Offers Over £390,000

Semi-Detached House

4 Bedrooms

This traditional semi-detached property has many redeeming features and is a fine example of a Victorian Villa built circa 1897 with architectural design of that period with lots of original character and charm.   The rooms are well proportioned with high ceilings, ornate cornicing, deep skirtings and panelled doors. It offers accommodation over two levels and comprises vestibule, entrance hall, lounge, dining room, kitchen, utility room, shower room, four bedrooms and a family bathroom. Externally it offers off street parking, a double garage and mature gardens to front and rear. There is also a summerhouse, coal shed and boiler room.  Benefitting from oil central heating and sash and case windows, this delightful property is oozing with potential. It would benefit from a full scheme of upgrading, allowing the new owner to infuse their personal style and create a wonderful family home. Properties like 2 Pedwarden Road are a very welcome addition to the market and early viewing is advised to appreciate the potential and location on offer here.

Area

The property is within walking distance of all amenities Perth has to offer including: shops, restaurants, leisure facilities and the North Inch Parklands. Close by, Kinnoull Hill offers fantastic woodland walks, and beautiful views down the Perthshire valley. The area is well connected, with main routes to Edinburgh, Glasgow and Dundee all easily accessible as well as excellent bus and rail routes nearby.
