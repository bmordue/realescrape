Queens Grove is a peaceful cul-de-sac on the outskirts of Dalbeattie, within level walking distance of the town centre and its amenities. Dalbeattie offers primary and secondary schooling, shops, local food stores, health centre and golf course. Walkers and mountain bikers are also well catered for in the region with the town woods just minutes away and the 7Stanes cycle tracks on hand in the Dalbeattie Forest, less than a mile away. The "Granite Town" of Dalbeattie is the gateway to The Solway Coast and is approximately 4 miles from the sailing village of Kippford, with the sandy beaches of Rockcliffe and Sandyhills just a few more minutes away.

Well presented, semi-detached bungalow set within easily maintained gardens with off street parking for two cars. The property enjoys spaciously laid out, light filled rooms in good decorative order with a newly fitted kitchen and shower room as well as newly installed oak internal doors. There is UPVC double glazing and gas central heating throughout with a living flame gas fire in the lounge.

The property was completely renovated in 2000 and taken back to the bare brick. More recently in 2018, the roof tiles were renewed. The accommodation consists of a spacious, light lounge with fire-place and adjoining kitchen which provides access to the sizeable rear garden. There are two double bedrooms each with built in wardrobes, served by a modern Shower Room.
This is an excellent opportunity to purchase a comfortable, affordable bungalow.
