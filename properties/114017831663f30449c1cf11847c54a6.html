3 Little Wood Grove,
Bonnyrigg EH19 3DH

Offers over £285,000

End Terraced House

4 Bedrooms

Immaculately presented, this spacious four-bedroom end-terrace townhouse offers stylish family living with gardens, set in a modern, maintained, family-oriented development in Bonnyrigg, Midlothian. Featuring a bespoke rear extension, flexible living spaces, and off-street positioning adjacent to open parkland, this property is an ideal family home.

Comprises: halls for each level, a living room, a dining/kitchen, four bedrooms, an en-suite shower room, a family bathroom, and a ground-floor WC.

Highlights include a quality integrated kitchen, stylish bathroom suites, contemporary flooring and lighting, solid oak doors for the ground floor, gas central heating, and double glazing. Ready-to-move-in, it also has multiple TV points, a separate utility cupboard, and good storage provision, including a loft.

Externally, the property boasts a rear garden featuring a patio and a store shed, with a path and lawn to the front. This popular development has ample additional unrestricted residential parking, together with well-maintained communal areas and a primary school.

A welcoming entrance hall that sets the tone for this beautifully presented home. From here, there is access to a convenient WC, a staircase to the upper floors, and an impressive open-plan dining kitchen. Finished with attractive wood-effect flooring that flows seamlessly into the living room, the space is ideal for modern family living. The stylish kitchen is fitted with modern units and Minerva worktops, complemented by a breakfast bar, tiled splashbacks, and a full range of integrated appliances, including a double oven, five-ring gas hob, microwave, dishwasher, and fridge/freezer. The generous dining area also benefits from a useful understair storage cupboard and a separate utility room with space for freestanding appliances. Twin doors open into a rear-facing living room, stylishly decorated and enhanced by feature acoustic wall panels, a wall-mounted TV point, and sliding patio doors that lead directly out to the garden.

The first floor hosts two well-proportioned double bedrooms, positioned to opposite aspects and finished with carpeting, offering ample space for freestanding furniture. Bedroom two further benefits from a large built-in wardrobe. A further bedroom provides a versatile space, perfect for use as a nursery, home office, or study. Completing this level is a contemporary family bathroom, fitted with a modern three-piece suite including a shower over the bath, metro-style tiled splash walls, and a ladder-style radiator. Occupying the entire second floor, the impressive master bedroom offers a superb sense of space and privacy. This elegant retreat includes a dedicated dressing area and a stylish en-suite shower room, featuring a rainfall shower, partially tiled walls, and a ladder-style radiator - creating a luxurious sanctuary within the home.

Factor Fees: James Gibb, approx £180 per year, Scottish Woodlands, approx £160 per year
