Internally the property is in excellent decorative order throughout and benefits from gas central heating, double glazing and good storage facilities. The integrated kitchen appliances are included in the sale together with the washing machine and fridge/freezer.

External
A monobloc driveway provides off-street parking for several cars and leads to a detached single garage with up and over door, power and light.

The side and rear gardens are fully enclosed and feature patios and decked areas with space for garden furniture, lawns and an attractive array of established plants and shrubs. The garden sheds are included in the sale.

Area
Local shops and amenities providing for all everyday needs are available in nearby Colinton village. Additional shopping facilities are to be found at Colinton Mains, which has a large Tesco store and at Hunters Tryst which has a Morrisons supermarket.

Well regarded schooling is available from nursery to senior level, within the private and public sectors. Regular bus services are available on nearby Redford Road and Colinton Road to and from the City Centre and many surrounding areas. For commuters, ready access southwards is available to the city bypass, which links with the A1, M8, M9, M90, The Forth Road Bridge and Edinburgh International Airport.

The open spaces of Colinton Dell and the Pentland Hills Country Park are nearby and Craiglockhart sports and tennis centre is just a short drive away.
