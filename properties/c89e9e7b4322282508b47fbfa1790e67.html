42 Gowanbrae Drive,
Dunfermline KY12 7RL

Offers over £250,000

Detached Bungalow

3 Bedrooms

Situated within a highly sought-after and well-established residential area of Dunfermline, this attractive three-bedroom bungalow offers spacious, flexible accommodation ideally suited to a wide range of buyers including families, downsizers, and first-time purchasers. Gowanbrae Drive forms part of a popular residential estate within Dunfermline, known for its convenient access to a wide range of amenities. Local shops, supermarkets, leisure facilities, and healthcare services are all within easy reach, while Dunfermline town centre offers an excellent selection of retail, restaurants, and cultural attractions. The location is ideal for commuters, with swift access to the M90 motorway network providing links to Edinburgh, Perth, and beyond. Public transport options, including bus routes and nearby railway stations, further enhance accessibility.

The property is entered via a welcoming central hallway, providing access to all main rooms. To the front, a bright and generously proportioned living room offers an inviting space for relaxation, enhanced by excellent natural light. The home benefits from a flexible layout, with a separate room currently utilised as a dining room which could easily serve as a third bedroom, home office, or additional living space depending on individual requirements. The kitchen is well positioned and provides a practical layout, with direct access leading through to a charming conservatory. This additional living area enjoys views over the garden and is ideal for informal dining, relaxing, or entertaining guests throughout the year. There are two further well-proportioned double bedrooms, both offering comfortable accommodation. A centrally located family bathroom completes the internal layout. Externally, the property is complemented by a detached garage, providing secure parking and excellent storage. The garden grounds are generous in size and have been well maintained, providing an excellent outdoor space for relaxation and entertaining

Early viewing is highly recommended to fully appreciate the versatile accommodation and sought-after location this appealing home has to offer.
