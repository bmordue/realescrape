Nestled within a peaceful rural setting, this exceptional detached country property in the heart of the Angus countryside offers a perfect blend of character, comfort and space. Brothock meadows is surrounded by woodland and countryside views and proudly sits on beautifully landscaped garden grounds extending to approximately 1/3 acre, has been finished to the highest standards with a classic kitchen, triple glazed windows, alarm system and total control electric heating system, together with 4kw of solar panels.

This rural retreat gives you the space to enjoy countryside living yet is conveniently located with easy commuting links to surrounding Angus towns providing the best of both worlds – seclusion and accessibility.  The property is ideally located with schools, shopping, leisure facilities and railway and bus stations only approximately 4 miles away in Arbroath. 

This impressive home offers generously proportioned living spaces throughout, designed to accommodate modern living with comfort and style. Upon entering, you're greeted by a bright and welcoming entrance hallway, complete with built-in mirrored storage cupboards for added convenience. From here, you are led into the spacious lounge, which boasts panoramic views across the beautifully maintained garden grounds — an ideal setting for both relaxing and entertaining. The lounge opens seamlessly into the light-filled dining room, featuring dual-aspect windows and twin sets of French doors that flood the space with natural light. These doors lead directly into a large conservatory, perfectly positioned to enjoy uninterrupted views of the garden year-round. Adjacent to the dining room is a well-appointed kitchen, which benefits from a separate utility room providing internal access to the adjoining garage. The lower level is completed by a versatile double bedroom, ideal for guests or use as a home office, and a convenient downstairs W.C.  

Upstairs, the accommodation continues with a generous master bedroom featuring an en-suite bathroom and access to a private enclosed balcony. A further well-sized bedroom on this level shares a Jack and Jill-style family bathroom, ideal for flexible living.  There is also a large walk-in linen cupboard.
