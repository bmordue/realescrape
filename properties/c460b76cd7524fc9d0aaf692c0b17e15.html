Detached House

3 Bedrooms

Generously proportioned throughout, this impressive DETACHED VILLA offers excellent family accommodation within this sought after and exclusive district with excellent amenities nearby including the Broom Shops, only minutes from Clarkston Toll, The Avenue shopping centre and Greenlaw retail park, within the catchment of popular East Renfrewshire schooling including Kirkhill Primary and Mearns Castle High School both nearby.

The property benefits from gas central heating and double glazing.

Opaque double glazed front door onto broad reception hall, extensive large storage cupboard below stairs, lounge with aspects to front and focal point marble fireplace, separate living/dining room with aspects over enclosed fully stocked rear garden, fully fitted kitchen with window and double glazed door to rear garden. Comprising floor and wall mounted polished white veneer fronted units with complimentary work tops and ceramic tiling full height around walls, integrated double oven, hob and hood, downstairs cloakroom/toilet comprising two piece suite.

First floor: broad landing with deep storage cupboard, three bedrooms (each with built-in fitted mirror wardrobes), shower room comprising three piece suite to include a large shower cubicle with full height glass screens and wet wall panelling with ceramic tiling full height to remaining walls.

The property is set amidst private gardens to front and rear. Driveway with access to single car garage.
