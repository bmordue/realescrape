A beautifully presented and exceptionally well-finished 3-4-bedroom detached villa, occupying a sought-after position within the desirable semi-rural hamlet of Inveraldie. Finished to an exacting standard throughout, 1 Dalziel Road offers a superb blend of contemporary style and flexible family living, with high-quality fixtures and luxurious finishes evident across every room. Set against a stunning backdrop of open countryside, this impressive home enjoys generous private garden grounds, outstanding outdoor entertaining space and a peaceful setting, whilst remaining within easy reach of Dundee and excellent local amenities.

The accommodation is bright, spacious and thoughtfully arranged over two levels, ideally suited to modern family living. The welcoming entrance hallway leads through to a generously proportioned lounge, providing an elegant principal public room with ample space for both relaxation and entertaining. Positioned adjacent is the formal dining room with feature log burner, creating an excellent setting for family gatherings and entertaining guests.

To the rear of the property, the contemporary kitchen has been finished to an exceptional standard with quality cabinetry, premium work surfaces and a practical layout ideal for day-to-day living. The kitchen flows directly into a highly versatile reception room, a wonderfully bright space enjoying direct access to the rear gardens and offering the perfect environment for informal family living, entertaining or relaxing whilst taking full advantage of the beautiful open countryside outlooks. This space could be utilised as a formal dining area, additional lounge or a spacious fourth bedroom depending on requirements. A separate utility room provides additional storage and laundry facilities, while the ground floor also benefits from a well-proportioned double bedroom and a contemporary family bathroom, offering excellent flexibility for guests, multi-generational living or home working requirements.

The upper level comprises two further exceptionally spacious double bedrooms, both benefiting from fitted storage with the primary suite boasting its own en-suite shower room. An additional shower room serves the second bedroom and completes the upper accommodation. The homes attic spaces is complete with flooring and lighting for added convince and storage options. The layout offers excellent flexibility and would suit a wide range of buyers, from growing families to those seeking adaptable living arrangements to those looking for a stunning detached home with the ability to live on one level if required.

Externally, the property enjoys substantial private garden grounds which are undoubtedly a key feature of the home. The exceptionally large rear garden has been thoughtfully designed to maximise enjoyment of the stunning open countryside views beyond. A generous patio area provides the ideal setting for outdoor dining and entertaining, while the raised decked terrace offers an elevated space to relax and enjoy the peaceful surroundings. The expansive lawn further enhances the sense of privacy and space, creating an ideal environment for families and outdoor living.

To the front, a private driveway provides ample off-street parking, while the attractive frontage and immaculate presentation create immediate kerb appeal.

Despite its peaceful semi-rural setting, the property is exceptionally well positioned for convenient access to both Dundee and Aberdeen, making it ideal for commuters and those seeking a balance between countryside living and city convenience. Dundee is only a short drive away and offers an extensive range of amenities including renowned universities, excellent shopping facilities, restaurants, leisure pursuits and mainline rail connections.

This is a rare opportunity to acquire a truly exceptional detached villa offering luxurious, versatile accommodation within a picturesque semi-rural setting. Combining high-quality finishes, generous living space and stunning countryside views, 1 Dalziel Road represents an outstanding family home of significant appeal.
