1 Blackford Hill Grove, Blackford,
Edinburgh EH9 3HA

Offers over £695,000

Detached House

4 Bedrooms

Occupying a large corner plot and commanding magnificent panoramic views including Salisbury Crags, Arthur's Seat and the Castle, this substantial detached villa offers ideal family accommodation extending to around 1600 square feet over two levels. Designed to take full advantage of the unparalleled views, the house would now benefit from a degree of modernisation but offers the perfect opportunity to create a unique and stylish home in a superb location. The elegant dual aspect sitting room offers plenty of space to entertain as does the equally spacious dual aspect kitchen/dining room which comes complete with integrated appliances. Both rooms have patio doors offering direct access to the rear garden. Also, on the ground floor there is a utility room, a WC and an extremely useful study/home office. Upstairs, a large galleried landing area gives access to all four bedrooms, a contemporary shower room and a separate WC. There is ample storage space throughout including a large attic. Additional benefits include double glazing and gas central heating. A driveway leads to the single car garage and steps lead up to the front door. Mature, well stocked garden ground lies to the front, side and rear of the house. The rear garden being south facing and particularly secluded. A patio area running the length of the house is ideal for alfresco dining and the perfect spot to enjoy long summer evenings.
