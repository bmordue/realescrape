The Courtyard, Woodbush,
Dunbar EH42 1HB

Offers over £450,000

Detached House

7 Bedrooms

Now in need of upgrading this unique detached property is located within the popular coastal town of Dunbar. This property would appeal to someone wishing to take on a project and restore the property to make a fine family home. Situated to the east of Edinburgh the historic town of Dunbar offers a quieter way of life with local walking routes including the John Muir way and Golf Courses. The High Street is filled with local independent shops, cafes and eateries. The property benefits from spectacular uninterrupted views of the sea, and offers direct access to the beach for kayaking, swimming and other water related activities. This is an unfinished project with planning permission and building warrant for some work.

Set within a private courtyard sitting on the coastline, the property opens to an entrance hallway with stairs to the upper level and leads to the hallway with an under-stair cupboard, additional storage cupboard and giving access to the remaining downstairs accommodation. The large, dual aspect family room has views out to the sea via double doors, a door giving direct access to the front courtyard, and a sink unit.
There are 5 bedrooms on the lower level, the largest of which featuring an ensuite which comprises of a shower, WC, and wash hand basin. The remaining 4 bedrooms are front facing and two with sinks. There is a handy WC/cloakroom with a wash hand basin, and an additional family bathroom which comprises a 3-piece suite.

The first floor has a hallway with 2 handy built-in cupboards, the largest of which houses freestanding fridges and a chest freezer. The kitchen features a large slot in cooker and freestanding dishwasher both included. The dual aspect living/dining room features beamed ceilings, ocean views, and ample room for both living and dining furniture. There are two double bedrooms, the largest is dual aspect, with a large ensuite off featuring a bath, separate shower, WC and a wash hand basin. The remaining bedroom is front facing and features an ensuite with a WC, wash hand basin, and shower.

To the front of the property is a large private courtyard, with a garage. Additional benefits include, stunning ocean views, gas central heating and some double-glazed windows.


Note
This property will be sold as seen with no further works to be carried out by the current owner
