Livingston EH54 9HL

Offers over £325,000

Detached House

5 Bedrooms

Nestled in a charming and family-friendly neighbourhood, 20 Ossian Drive is a modern, five bedroomed villa in immaculate, walk-in condition. Viewing is highly recommended to appreciate all this beautiful home has to offer. The property has been decorated to a high standard throughout and hosts generously sized rooms, ample storage throughout, a sun room, beautiful gardens to the front, side and rear and a monoblock driveway. The property also benefits from gas fired central heating and double glazed windows throughout.

The garden grounds feature a pond, mature shrubs and plants, a large garden shed and access to the woodland lying immediately behind the property.

The sought-after Murieston neighbourhood provides an array of convenient amenities nearby, such as a local Coop, various takeaway food options, and hair salons. Additionally, the Almondvale Shopping Centre and Livingston Designer Outlet are just a short distance away, offering a wide selection of popular retailers, dining establishments, cafes, and supermarkets. Excellent schools are situated within the vicinity, while recreational opportunities are within easy access including Bellsquarry Woods and Murieston Valley's cycle paths, and woodland trails. There is also a choice of sporting and leisure pursuits including swimming pools, golf courses, libraries and sports centres with a multi-screen cinema situated within the shopping centre. The Murieston area is well placed for those commuting daily with regular rail links to Edinburgh & Glasgow from Livingston South station, with Edinburgh International Airport just a short drive away. Alternatively, major access roads including the A71 and M8 also allow for easy travel out with the area.
