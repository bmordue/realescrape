Detached Bungalow

5 Bedrooms

Detached bungalow offering versatile family sized accommodation arranged over two levels. Set back from Lanark Road at the start of Redhall Bank Road, this generous home enjoys many features including views over Colinton Dell towards the Pentlands and level mobility access. The flexible accommodation comprises; at ground floor level - entrance vestibule, hall, sitting room with an attractive fireplace housing a gas living flame fire, dining/family room with two sets of patio doors overlooking the rear garden and beyond, kitchen, study/playroom/fifth bedroom, large double bedroom with own entrance having level mobility access and an en suite wet room, second double bedroom and bathroom with a white three piece suite having an electric shower over the bath. Upstairs has two bedrooms and a well appointed shower room. The house has gas central heating, double glazing, a delightful rear garden centred around a shaped lawn, with a raised deck, 'sun trap' circular patio, well stocked borders and a superb view. To the front is an extensive driveway offering plenty of off street parking and a good sized integral single garage having a utility area. On street parking is also available. Kingsknowe is set to the south of the city centre. Within the immediate area is a large Sainsburys supermarket on Inglis Green Road and a 24 hour ASDA at nearby Chesser. There are both primary and secondary schools available locally plus a choice of bus services to and from the city centre. The greenery of Colinton Dell and the Water of Leith Walkway are both in close proximity. For commuters, easy access is available to the city by-pass providing links to the A1, central motorway system and the airport, plus the Kingsknowe railway station is within walking distance.
