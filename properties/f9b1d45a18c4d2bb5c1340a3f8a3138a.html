Detached House

3 Bedrooms

Description This is an immaculate, well proportioned (74m sq) detached house in true move in condition throughout, located on a popular, modern, private estate. The ground floor accommodation comprises entrance hall, lounge, modern fitted kitchen with integrated appliances and a handy WC. Upstairs is the master bedroom with fitted wardrobes and en suite shower room, two further bedrooms and a part tiled family bathroom with three piece white suite. The property benefits from gas central heating and double glazing throughout. Location The property is located in a modern estate which occupies an elevated position above the small town of Gorebridge. Located close to the A7 some 4 miles from the City Bypass, Gorebridge has expanded in recent years through modern development and is now a popular commuter base with nearby rail stations (Gorebridge and Newtongrange). The town has well served bus routes, local shops and leisure centre. Within easy reach is Straiton Retail Park, Tesco Superstore, several golf courses, walkways and Vogrie Country Park. Gardens and parking There is a small front garden and a larger enclosed, rear and side garden which has a wooden decked area and a lawn. The property benefits from having an integral garage and a driveway. Extras All fitted floor coverings, blinds, and a selection of integrated and freestanding appliances-including a gas hob, oven, extractor hood, fridge/freezer, washing machine, dishwasher, and shed-are included in the sale price. Please note that none of these items come with guarantees or warranties." Home Report The property has been valued by surveyors at £260,000 and the home report is available via the ESPC listing.
