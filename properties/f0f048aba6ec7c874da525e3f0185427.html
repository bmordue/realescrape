86 Laird Street, Dundee, is a well-proportioned three-bedroom mid-terraced villa positioned within a popular and established residential area of the city. The property offers generous accommodation throughout and presents an excellent opportunity for those seeking a home with great potential in a convenient and well-connected location.

The ground floor comprises a welcoming lounge to the front, a bright and spacious room that enjoys good natural light and provides ample space for both everyday living and family dining. The kitchen lies to the rear and offers a practical layout with direct access to the private garden, creating an ideal setting for those who enjoy outdoor living.

On the upper level, there are three comfortable bedrooms, each of a good size and offering flexibility to suit a variety of needs, whether as additional sleeping accommodation, guest space or a home office. The bathroom completes the accommodation and features a three-piece suite.

Externally, the property benefits from an enclosed rear garden which offers excellent outdoor space and the opportunity to landscape or develop further to individual preference. The front garden enhances the home’s frontage and provides scope for creating additional kerb appeal.

Laird Street sits within a quiet, well-established part of Dundee that is well served by a range of local amenities including shops, schools and leisure facilities. Regular public transport links connect easily to Dundee city centre, Ninewells Hospital and both universities, while nearby Clatto Country Park and Camperdown Park offer wonderful green spaces for walking and recreation.

This property represents a superb opportunity for buyers looking to create a home tailored to their own style within a sought-after residential location. With its generous layout, private gardens and excellent access to local amenities, 86 Laird Street offers the foundations for comfortable and flexible modern living.
