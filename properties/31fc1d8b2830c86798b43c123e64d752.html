Second Floor Flat

1 Bedroom

Immaculate, one-bedroom, second floor flat situated at the top of the vibrant Grassmarket area in Edinburgh's Old Town which is also in close proximity to Fountainbridge and Tollcross. Nearby you'll find fantastic shops and cafes and it's just a short walk to The Meadows and Edinburgh University. This property is in walk-in condition, is beautifully presented, with smart fittings and fixtures throughout, ideal for a first-time buyer, investment opportunity or as a pied-a-terre. Entering the communal stair the flat is on the second floor and comprises; a hall, open plan kitchen/ dining/living room, with an excellent range of modern fitted units, appliances, window seat, with storage, built-in blinds, a pleasant rooftop outlook, a cupboard and plenty of space for dining and seating. The bedroom has two, slim built-in wardrobes, rooftop views and built-in blinds. The shower room has a shower cabinet, feature wash hand basin, w.c., heated towel rail and the washing machine. The flat has been cleverly designed and the furniture can be included in the sale. There is permit parking close by. The Grassmarket is just a short walk from Edinburgh Castle and the Old Town and many of Edinburgh University's buildings as well as the business and financial districts of Fountainbridge and Tollcross. The vibrant West End and all that the city centre has to offer are also nearby. There is an abundance of on hand amenities from independent shops & cafes including Mary's Milk Bar and Hawico, a multitude of bars, pubs and restaurants as well as Tesco and Sainsbury supermarkets. Leisure facilities nearby include PureGym at Edinburgh's Quarter Mile and the Cameo and Odeon cinemas. Fountain Park entertainment park offers something for everyone and has a Nuffield Health gym, Ten Pin Bowling, multi screen cinema and a Trampoline park. Closely situated to Edinburgh Castle, The Meadows and Union Canal, there are lovely walks to be had and the property boasts good proximity to the city's transport network and Haymarket Station, along with convenient access to the City Bypass and A1.
