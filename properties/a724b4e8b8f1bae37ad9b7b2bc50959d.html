Plot 34 The Haddon, Roseberry Park, Marr Crescent,
Tranent EH33 2FB

Fixed Price £386,000

Detached House

4 Bedrooms

Are you in the market for a new home that combines style, practicality, and modern comforts? Introducing The Haddon, a stunning 4-bedroom detached home with a detached single garage. This new build home for sale is designed to cater to your every need, offering spacious living areas and a range of impressive features. Let's take a closer look at what makes The Haddon the ideal choice for your dream home. First impressions matter, and The Haddon's frontage is sure to leave a lasting one. With its stylish and clean design, featuring a striking stone base course and white render, the exterior exudes a sense of modern elegance. The use of high-quality materials ensures a durable and visually appealing finish that will stand the test of time. Step inside The Haddon and you'll be greeted by a large welcoming hallway, complete with a convenient storage cupboard to keep your belongings organized. The spacious downstairs WC provides an additional touch of practicality for you and your guests. The heart of the home lies at the rear, where you'll find open plan kitchen, and versatile dining/family activity area. This modern kitchen boasts upstands and back panels, chrome downlights, and worktop task lighting, creating a contemporary and functional space for cooking and entertaining. Equipped with a 4-burner gas hob, oven and fridge freezer, the kitchen has some great facilities to help get you started. French doors lead out to the garden, perfect for combining the indoor and outdoor areas on long summer days. Adjacent to the kitchen is a large utility room, offering ample space for a washing machine and additional storage. With its external door to the side of the property, it's perfect for use as a boot room, conveniently accessing your garden and taking the dogs out for a stroll around their new neighbourhood. The lounge, situated at the front of the property, is a cosy retreat. It features superfast broadband connection, CAT 5 network cabling, a TV point, and access to understairs storage. Large windows flood the room with natural light and offer a picturesque view of the front garden. As you ascend to the first floor, you'll find an inviting landing that wraps around, connecting all of the bedrooms and the family bathroom. The layout ensures privacy and ease of movement throughout. Bedroom one comes complete with fitted wardrobes and an en-suite, which features a shower cubicle. You have a choice of your preferred tiling, and the chrome trim creates a professional finish. Bedrooms two and three are wonderfully spacious and include fitted wardrobes. These rooms can easily accommodate family members or guests in comfort. Bedroom four, positioned at the front of the property, presents a versatile space that can be used as a nursery, playroom, home office, or guest room, adapting to your changing needs. The Haddon not only offers a beautiful interior but also provides delightful outdoor spaces. A detached single garage with lighting and power supply adds versatility to your home, serving as a home gym or workshop, catering to your hobbies and interests. The block-paved driveway ensures convenient and secure parking for your vehicles. The front and rear gardens are expertly turfed, creating a lush and inviting environment. A 1.8m fence and gate enclose the back garden, offering privacy and security for you and your loved ones. An external tap in the rear garden provides easy access to water for gardening or outdoor activities. The property's external doors feature multipoint locking while a PIR downlighter at the front door alerts you to people at your door. A light at the back door ensures convenience and safety when accessing the rear of the property during night-time.
