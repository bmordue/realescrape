Offers over £135,000

Terraced House

3 Bedrooms

Now requiring full modernisation, this generously proportioned end-terraced villa presents an excellent opportunity for anyone looking to put their own stamp on a property. Located within a popular residential estate, the property benefits from a generous front garden and a particularly large rear garden-ideal for outdoor living or potential expansion. It also features gas central heating with a modern boiler and double glazing throughout. The ground floor comprises a welcoming entrance hall with storage, a spacious dual-aspect living/dining room, a separate kitchen. Upstairs, there are three bedrooms, as well as a family bathroom with a three-piece suite.This property offers fantastic potential for those looking to create a family home tailored to their own tastes and requirements. Central Heating and Double Glazing Gas central heating is complemented by double glazing. Garden and Parking The property benefits from a private driveway and private back garden space. There is also on street parking. Location The district of Mayfield has expanded in recent years with modern development and has evolved into a popular commuter base. It is conveniently positioned between the A68 and the A7, some 8 miles approximately from Edinburgh and only a few minutes' drive away from the City Bypass and the Sheriffhall park and ride facility. There are several local amenities including woodland walks, health services a leisure centre, primary schools, the recently opened Newbattle High School and shops plus frequent bus connections. Neighbouring Dalkeith offers a wider range of facilities, alternative secondary schooling and retail outlets including a modern Tesco supermarket. Further "high street" retail units are available at Straiton and Fort Kinnaird. Extras All carpets, floor coverings, curtains and light fittings are included in the sale.
