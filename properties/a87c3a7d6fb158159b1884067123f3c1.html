38 Eagles View,
Livingston EH54 8AE

Offers over £200,000

Ground Floor Flat

3 Bedrooms

McEwan Fraser Legal is delighted to present Eagles View to the market.

Positioned on the ground floor of a well-maintained modern development, this beautifully presented apartment opens into a welcoming entrance hall finished with warm timber-style flooring and generous built-in storage. From here, the home flows into the show-stopper of the apartment, a truly stunning kitchen. Designed with both style and function in mind, it boasts sleek grey cabinetry, premium integrated appliances, and a statement tiled splashback. The layout offers ample workspace and a seamless feel, making it a joy to cook, host or simply enjoy.

Continuing through, the spacious living room enjoys a pleasant outlook through a large window and benefits from soft natural light throughout the day. The neutral tones, quality flooring, and open layout create a versatile setting for relaxing or entertaining.

There are three well-proportioned double bedrooms, each thoughtfully styled and complete with fitted wardrobes to maximise storage. The principal bedroom further benefits from its own private en-suite shower room, smartly appointed in modern tones for a sleek finish. The remaining two bedrooms are equally generous, offering flexibility for family life, guests, or working from home.

Completing the accommodation is the main bathroom, tiled in warm neutral tones and fitted with a vanity basin unit, WC, and a full-sized bath with overhead shower, ideal for unwinding at the end of the day.

Stylishly finished throughout and set in a sought-after development, this home is perfect for first-time buyers or professional couples seeking a move-in-ready property in a convenient, well-connected location.
