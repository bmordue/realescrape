The Factors House, 5 The Courtyard, Easter Broomhouse,
Dunbar EH42 1RA

Offers over £500,000

End Terraced House

4 Bedrooms

Situated just half a mile from Dunbar and surrounded by picturesque countryside and farmland, this pristine four-bedroom home in an exclusive steading development offers a highly desirable semi-rural lifestyle. The property immediately impresses with its striking red stone exterior, terracotta-tiled roof, and charming alfresco seating area. Stepping inside, a bright and airy carpeted hallway leads you to a stunning triple-aspect living room. With south, east, and west-facing windows offering beautiful views of the rear garden, the room is bathed in natural light, creating a warm and inviting atmosphere. Decorated in soft muted tones and featuring plush carpeting along with a wall-hung living flame fireplace, this space is perfect for family relaxation and social gatherings. It also enjoys seamless access to the garden through patio doors. Returning to the hallway, the stylish dining kitchen and family room impresses with its sleek wall and floor cabinetry in soft grey, paired with white countertops and high-spec integrated appliances, including an extractor hood, induction hob, eye-level grill, microwave, and oven. Sliding patio doors open to the alfresco dining area, while a tasteful décor and adjoining utility room make it ideal for modern living. A well-appointed shower room and a comfortable double bedroom complete the ground floor. Upstairs, two double bedrooms, and a third bedroom, are accessible from the spacious landing. The luxurious principal bedroom, with dual-aspect windows, boasts built-in cabinetry, a soothing colour palette, and a lavish en-suite featuring a bath, a washbasin set in a wall-hung vanity, and a hidden cistern WC, all in natural tones. The two additional double bedrooms provide cosy retreats, with one currently used as a second living room. Both share access to a stylish shower room with a countertop washbasin, chrome towel radiator, and hidden cistern WC. Outside, the beautifully presented south and west-facing rear garden, with tranquil countryside views, features an expansive alfresco dining area, lush lawn, decorative stones, and established shrubbery, creating a serene escape. The property also offers parking with an integral garage and a gravel driveway. There is also a car charging point at the garage door.
