Gilson Gray are delighted to present to the market this beautifully presented two-bedroom bungalow, occupying a highly sought-after residential position within Dundee. Offering spacious, well-maintained accommodation all on one level, this superb home has been thoughtfully upgraded by the current owners and provides a fantastic opportunity for a wide range of purchasers including downsizers, professionals and those seeking accessible single-storey living.

Internally, the property offers a welcoming and practical layout centred around a spacious reception hall which provides access to all principal apartments. To the front of the home, the bright and generous lounge enjoys excellent levels of natural light from an attractive bay window, creating a warm and inviting space ideal for both everyday living and entertaining.

The accommodation is complemented by two well-proportioned double bedrooms, both offering ample space for freestanding furniture and enjoying a peaceful setting within the property.

A particular highlight of the home is the impressive kitchen/dining room which has been recently upgraded to an exceptional standard. Designed with both style and practicality in mind, this contemporary space offers an excellent range of modern cabinetry, quality work surfaces and integrated storage, creating the perfect environment for cooking, dining and entertaining family and friends. The kitchen is further enhanced by a matching utility room, also recently modernised, providing valuable additional workspace and storage while maintaining a seamless finish throughout.

The bathroom has likewise been upgraded and now boasts a stylish modern suite featuring a large glass-enclosed walk-in shower, contemporary fittings and attractive finishes, delivering a luxurious and highly functional space. Completing the internal accommodation is a delightful sun room overlooking the rear garden, providing an additional reception space that can be enjoyed throughout the seasons and offering the perfect place to relax while enjoying an abundance of natural light.

Externally, the property continues to impress with a beautifully maintained plot. To the front, there is an attractive and easily maintained stone-chipped garden which enhances the property's kerb appeal while requiring minimal upkeep. A private driveway runs along the side of the home, providing ample off-street parking.

To the rear, the property enjoys a superb private garden which provides the perfect outdoor retreat. Predominantly laid to lawn, the garden offers generous space for relaxing, entertaining and family enjoyment whilst benefiting from a high degree of privacy. This wonderful outdoor space perfectly complements the internal accommodation and further enhances the appeal of this exceptional bungalow.

Muirfield Crescent enjoys a particularly convenient and desirable location within Dundee, offering easy access to a wide range of local amenities including supermarkets, independent retailers, healthcare facilities and leisure amenities. Dundee City Centre is within easy reach, while excellent public transport links and nearby commuter routes provide convenient connections throughout the city and beyond. The area is also well served by reputable schooling and a variety of recreational opportunities, making it a highly desirable place to call home.

Presented in true move-in condition with a recently upgraded kitchen, utility room and bathroom, this outstanding bungalow combines modern living with a sought-after location and is certain to appeal to a broad range of buyers. Early viewing is highly recommended to fully appreciate the quality, space and lifestyle on offer.
