28 Sydney Terrace, Craigentinny,
Edinburgh EH7 6SL

Offers over £480,000

End Terraced House

4 Bedrooms

28 Sydney Terrace is a beautifully-presented three-bedroom end-terraced house that perfectly blends comfort and modern living in the heart of Craigentinny. This charming property features both private front and rear gardens, as well as a private driveway. With its elegant interiors and inviting outdoor spaces, this home promises to provide a fantastic setting for a family home. As you step inside, you are greeted by a welcoming vestibule that leads into a versatile sitting room that can easily double as a fourth bedroom. The room boasts a gas-burning fire and a delightful bay window to the front, allowing natural light to flood the space. The built-in Edinburgh Press-style unit adds character and practical storage, making this area perfect for relaxation or entertaining guests. The heart of this home lies in its open plan kitchen, dining, and family room, designed for modern living. The stylish kitchen features shaker-style floor and wall-mounted cabinets, complete with a farmhouse sink and a prestigious Belling range cooker. Integrated appliances, including an extractor hood and fridge freezer, provide convenience, while the bright sitting area is enhanced by large bi-folding doors that seamlessly connect to the rear garden. A utility room off the sitting area offers additional versatility and leads to a modern three-piece shower room. Venturing to the first floor, you will find two spacious double bedrooms-one positioned to the front and the other to the rear-along with a further versatile third bedroom. The modern three-piece bathroom, equipped with a chrome heated towel rail completes the accommodation. The first-floor landing also boasts fantastic storage, adding to the practicality of this delightful home. Step outside to discover a generous rear garden that spans over two levels. The first level features a lovely paved area, perfect for alfresco dining, while the second level offers a lawn surrounded by an array of colourful plants and mature trees. Additional benefits of this property include gas central heating and double glazing throughout, ensuring comfort and efficiency all year round. Location Craigentinny is a charming residential area located to the east of Edinburgh's City Centre. Local shopping can be found on Craigentinny Road including a pharmacy and convenience store. Portobello, which is situated nearby, has larger shopping facilities including an Aldi supermarket. Portobello's Promenade and Beach are also within easy reach as are the swimming pool and Tumbles Gym. Edinburgh Fort, which has a wide variety of high street retailer including Marks & Spencer, Boots and Next, is within a short drive. There is also a large Asda at The Jewel. Craigentinny has good bus links into the City Centre. Extras All blinds, light fittings, fitted flooring and integrated appliances are included in the sale price.
