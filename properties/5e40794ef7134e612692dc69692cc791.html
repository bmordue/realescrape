Offers over £700,000

Detached House

3 Bedrooms

Tastefully extended, stone-built late Victorian detached house, which historically was the Railway Master's Cottage and with extensive mature gardens and significant potential for further development. Rich in original period detail, the property retains a wealth of charming architectural features synonymous with its era. Occupying a prime position on one of Colinton's most prestigious and sought-after streets, the house is ideally situated within easy walking distance of the village centre and an excellent range of local amenities. Offering exceptional scope to create a substantial family home, the property presents a rare opportunity for future enhancement and extension, subject to the necessary consents. The property further benefits from gas central heating.
