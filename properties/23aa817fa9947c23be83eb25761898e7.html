End Terraced House

3 Bedrooms

Closing Date Set for Tuesday 1st April at 2:00pm This versatile three-bedroom end-terraced house features a sizeable, enclosed garden and ample private parking, including a secure garage. It enjoys a quiet residential setting - well-connected to the rest of the city and its coastline by local bus links, day and night. Situated to the east of the city centre, it also provides swift access to the bypass for travel further afield. Now in need of some modernisation, the property provides plenty of scope for a purchaser to put their own stamp on the interiors. Upon entering, a hallway leads into a living room where a wide southwest-facing bay window fills the area with sunlight during the afternoons and early evenings. It boasts the practicality of oak-style flooring and direct access to the kitchen. The bright kitchen is functional, featuring a selection of classic wood-toned cabinets and workspace, designed to accommodate a range of freestanding appliances. Additionally, there is built-in pantry storage and access to the garden, which is convenient for barbecuing and alfresco dining on warmer days. Also accessible from the living room, and completing the ground floor, is a spacious garden-facing bedroom that could easily be used as a formal dining room or home study. Two double bedrooms, one of which features ample integrated storage, are on the first floor, accessible from the landing. A bright shower room equipped with a WC, vanity storage, and a shower enclosure is also on this level. The property is electrically heated and fully double-glazed. Outside, the extensive rear garden is fully enclosed, providing a safe environment for children and pets. It is predominantly laid to lawn, presenting attractive opportunities for landscaping improvements or even offering sufficient space for a home extension, subject to the necessary consents. At the front of the property, a gated tandem driveway is flanked by a low-maintenance gravelled garden and leads to an attached single garage. Northfield, Edinburgh Roughly two miles from Edinburgh city centre, Northfield is a popular residential area with excellent local amenities that are further supplemented by the wealth of local shops, cafes, pubs, and takeaways in neighbouring Portobello. The area is served by a Morrisons supermarket with more extensive shopping facilities at nearby Meadowbank Shopping Park, where there is a selection of popular high-street stores, eateries, and supermarkets. Northfield is well placed for enjoying some of Edinburgh's finest outdoor locations, situated within walking distance of Portobello Beach and Holyrood Park, home to the iconic Arthur's Seat. For indoor exercise, Meadowbank Sports Centre has recently reopened after significant refurbishment. The area is served by fantastic public transport links, with regular buses running day and night providing a swift connection to the heart of the capital. Furthermore, it also offers convenient access to the A1 and the city bypass, allowing quick and easy travel to Edinburgh International Airport and to the M8/M9 motorway network for journeys across the central belt and beyond. The property is in the catchment area for well-regarded primary and secondary schooling.
