Terraced House

5 Bedrooms

This four/five-bedroom mid-terraced family home in desirable Braids offers the space and elegance of a classic Victorian residence, occupying an elevated position with stunning views of Arthur's Seat, the Pentland Hills, and the coast. It promises exceptionally comfortable living with two reception rooms, a study, a bathroom and second WC, and a dining kitchen with a utility room flowing onto an enclosed garden. Parking is available on street and is conveniently unrestricted.
