Barend Farmhouse, Sandyhills,
Dalbeattie DG5 4NU

Offers over £210,000

Semi-Detached House

4 Bedrooms

McEwan Fraser Legal are delighted to offer to the market this beautiful four-bed traditional farmhouse. The property is nestled within substantial gardens and benefits from stunning sea views within the peaceful and popular Barend Holiday Village, Sandyhills, Dalbeattie, Dumfries and Galloway. This unique home is brimming with traditional charm, whilst also impeccably blended with modern convenience. The extensive refurbishment of the property is evident throughout, and it's offered in absolutely walk-in condition from top to bottom.

The accommodation comprises an entrance boot room to ensure a clutter-free environment at all times. The lounge is very comfortable and features an open fire and French doors leading out effortlessly to the stone-walled garden. The beautiful kitchen/diner is finished in a stylish range of shaker units, with an electric hob, double oven, and ample space for an American-style fridge freezer and large dining table. It's the perfect spot for entertaining and family gatherings. The kitchen leads out to the conservatory, spectacular views abound, and the feeling is deeply relaxing.

The home benefits from four bright and welcoming bedrooms. The main bathroom is beautifully presented and contains a toilet and sink with a bath and a shower. The property also benefits from a very stylish downstairs WC. Central heating and double-glazing keep the home warm and comfortable.

The outside space of this home adds fantastic lifestyle value for the new owners. The gardens are beautifully landscaped, simple and low-maintenance, with space to relax on the sun-patio.

This unique home is sure to appeal to a wide swathe of buyers. It's ideal as a family home, equally it makes an incredibly appealing holiday let or getaway haven. If you are searching for a walk-in condition holiday home set in a very popular holiday village, then this may be it. Early viewing is highly recommended.
