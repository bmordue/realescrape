12 Tanhill Road, Blackwood
Lanark ML11 0JY

Offers over £340,000

Detached Bungalow

4 Bedrooms

Set in a highly desirable position at the end of a quiet row of homes and close to a local playpark, this beautifully presented four-bedroom detached bungalow offers spacious, flexible accommodation ideal for family living.

On entering the property, you are welcomed by a bright entrance vestibule which leads into a light and inviting central hallway. To the front of the home sits a beautifully decorated living room, providing a comfortable and elegant space to relax.

The heart of the home is the generously proportioned dining kitchen, which flows seamlessly into a charming sitting room area. This space benefits from French doors opening directly onto the rear garden, creating an ideal setting for both everyday living and entertaining. A utility room located off the kitchen provides additional storage and practicality and gives direct access to the integral garage.

The property boasts four generously sized bedrooms, three of which benefit from fitted wardrobes. The principal bedroom further enjoys the convenience of a private en suite, while the remaining bedrooms are served by the main family bathroom.

Externally, the home is complemented by a large, secure driveway offering ample off-street parking. The enclosed rear garden features a well-maintained lawn and patio area, perfect for outdoor dining and family enjoyment.

This superb bungalow enjoys a peaceful yet well-connected location, combining privacy, space and excellent transport links, making it an outstanding opportunity for a wide range of buyers.
