Flat

4 Bedrooms

This exceptional duplex apartment is a rare addition to the market, offering generously proportioned and highly flexible accommodation across two levels. Set within a charming traditional building on a peaceful, historically rich street, the property has been thoughtfully upgraded by the current owner to an impressive standard-blending period character with contemporary style. Bright and spacious throughout, the home is perfectly suited to a wide range of buyers, from professionals and couples to growing families seeking adaptable living space. A well-maintained communal close leads to the first-floor entrance. The welcoming reception hallway provides access to all lower apartments and features a staircase to the upper level. A cleverly concealed storage cupboard beneath the stairs adds valuable practicality. The standout feature of the home is the stunning dining kitchen. Designed with both style and functionality in mind, it boasts modern fitted units and a generous central island incorporating an induction downdraft hob, under-counter fridge, freezer and dishwasher. Timber sash and case double glazed windows which due to the spiral balanced mechanism the windows slide elegantly. Extensively upgraded bathroom with custom mood lighting and full opening window. Additional appliances include a double oven, dishwasher, and washing machine. The space comfortably accommodates dining and relaxed seating, making it ideal for both everyday living and entertaining. The elegant front-facing lounge retains beautiful period details, including ornate cornicing and a ceiling rose, perfectly complemented by a contemporary fireplace. A versatile main bedroom is located on the entrance level and could easily serve as an additional public room if desired. Upstairs, there are three further well-proportioned bedrooms, offering flexibility for family living, home working, or guest accommodation. A stylish three-piece bathroom completes the upper level, featuring a thoughtfully designed over-bath shower. Further benefits include gas central heating, double glazing, and enhanced insulation, ensuring year-round comfort. Externally, the property enjoys access to communal rear gardens. Each of the four flats benefits from its own private garden section, along with a shared drying green. Living in Paisley Paisley is a vibrant and historic town that combines rich heritage with modern convenience. Known for its striking architecture, including the iconic Paisley Abbey, the area offers a strong sense of community alongside excellent local amenities. Residents benefit from a wide range of shops, cafes, and restaurants, as well as excellent transport links-making it an ideal location for commuters to Glasgow and beyond. With nearby parks, leisure facilities, and reputable schools, Paisley continues to be a popular choice for a diverse range of buyers seeking both lifestyle and connectivity.
