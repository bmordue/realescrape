21 Oss Quadrant,
Motherwell ML1 2NY

Offers over £265,000

Detached House

4 Bedrooms

We are delighted to introduce to the market, this stylish and very impressive four-bedroom detached family villa. This is a superb and very adaptable home, which provides substantial accommodation and living space for any growing family. Beautifully nestled in a popular family-friendly area that's well known for its neighbourly spirit, the property is offered to the market in immaculate condition and benefits from a quiet Cul-de-sac location. The accommodation consists of a welcoming hallway, off which there is a handy WC cloakroom toilet, something that's always a welcome addition to any busy family home. The bright and airy lounge is a relaxing place to sit at the end of the day, the room benefits from a feature fireplace flanked by a window on each side which ensures the room remains bright and welcoming. Leading through to the dining room it offers a perfect space for the family to get together around the table at mealtimes. Subject to planning and building approval the dining room could easily be knocked through to open up to a larger open-plan, kitchen diner if required. The kitchen/diner is well-appointed, it makes a lovely hub of the family home around mealtimes and benefits from a good range of fitted units, a gas hob, electric oven, with space for a freestanding dishwasher and American-style fridge freezer. it's the perfect place for any chef to serve up a marvellous meal. The breakfast bar is the ideal place to eat on busy work or school mornings when time is at a premium. The handy utility room helps to cope with the challenges of the family laundry requirements, with units matching the kitchen and ample space for a washing machine and tumble dryer. The family bathroom is very fresh and bright, with a white suite, consisting of a bath and a shower. There are four good-sized bedrooms within this lovely home, all have plenty of room for freestanding furniture and all contain built-in storage. The master bedroom as you would expect has a beautiful ensuite with a mains standalone shower enclosure and a stylish chrome radiator. The home is kept warm, comfortable and secure via double glazing and gas central heating. French doors lead out beautifully to the secure back garden, which is both pet and child-friendly, with a lawn, a large decked area, a drying area and a gazebo, it makes a perfect spot to relax on sunnier days. Outdoor space is complemented by off-street parking to the front aspect, with a two/three car driveway. The opportunity exists (subject to planning and building approval) to create a garage conversion which would add another dimension to the already sizable accommodation on offer. It could offer flexible space as a cinema/games room/fifth bedroom or a perfect spot for a home office/gym if required. Some properties tick all the boxes and this is certainly one of them. Early viewing is strongly advised for anyone seeking a beautiful family home, in a great location with all the hard work already done. Extras (Included in the sale): Freestanding appliances may be available by separate negotiation.
