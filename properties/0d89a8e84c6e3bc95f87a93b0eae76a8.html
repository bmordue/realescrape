20 Eskfield Grove, Eskbank,
Dalkeith EH22 3FA

Offers over £425,000

Detached Bungalow

3 Bedrooms

This detached three-bedroom bungalow, situated in the sought-after family-friendly area of Eskbank, exudes both charm and practicality, making it an ideal residence for any family. The property showcases a thoughtful layout with spacious rooms, modern conveniences, and delightful outdoor spaces, ensuring a comfortable and enjoyable living experience. The home boasts a bay-windowed living room, a space that combines elegance and warmth with its cosy fireplace and abundant natural light. This inviting area is perfect for family gatherings or quiet relaxation. The bright dining room, positioned separate to the living room, offers a seamless transition to the expansive conservatory. This light-filled extension not only enhances the living space but also connects directly to the landscaped garden, creating a harmonious indoor-outdoor flow that is perfect for entertaining guests or enjoying quiet moments with nature. The kitchen/breakfast room stands out with its wellappointed modern units, providing a stylish yet functional space for culinary adventures. The kitchen's design ensures plenty of storage and counter space, making meal preparation a pleasure. The adjoining utility room, with its practical layout, offers additional space for laundry and other household tasks, with convenient access to the garden, making outdoor chores straightforward and hassle-free. The bungalow features three generously sized double bedrooms, each designed with comfort in mind. The master bedroom, a true retreat, boasts an en-suite shower room that has been neutrally decorated to create a calm and restful atmosphere. The remaining two bedrooms are equally spacious and share a well-appointed family bathroom equipped with a shower over the bath, ensuring convenience for the whole home. Externally, the property continues to impress. The multi-car driveway provides ample parking space for residents and visitors alike, while the garage offers secure storage for vehicles or additional belongings. The wrap-around garden, meticulously maintained, surrounds the bungalow, offering various spots for relaxation, play, and gardening enthusiasts. This outdoor haven not only enhances the property's aesthetic appeal but also provides a safe and enjoyable environment for children and pets to explore. Located in Eskbank, a community known for its family-centric atmosphere and excellent amenities, this bungalow is within easy reach of schools, parks, shopping, and dining options. The neighbourhood's welcoming environment and convenient location make it an ideal place to call home. In summary, this detached three-bedroom bungalow in Eskbank combines spacious, well-designed interiors with beautiful outdoor spaces and practical features. It offers a perfect blend of comfort, style, and convenience, making it an excellent choice for families looking to settle in a vibrant and friendly community. In brief it comprises: welcoming vestibule. bay windowed living room with fireplace. bright dining room with spacious, adjacent conservatory that leads to the beautiful garden. well appointed kitchen/breakfast room with useful utility room with garden access, three spacious double bedrooms with the master bedroom boasting a neutrally decorated ensuite shower room. contemporary family bathroom with shower over bath. well maintained wrap around garden, gas central heating and double glazing. multi-car driveway and garage.
