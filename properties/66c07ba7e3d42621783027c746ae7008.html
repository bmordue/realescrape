13 Rhodes Cottages, North Berwick, East
Lothian EH39 5NL

Offers over £300,000

House

2 Bedrooms

Dating back to the 1850s, 13 Rhodes Cottages is a charming, traditionally built stone cottage enjoying a truly spectacular setting beside the Glen Golf Course in North Berwick and only a short walk to the beach and sea. With uninterrupted views over the Firth of Forth, Bass Rock, Craigleith, and the Fife coastline, this delightful two-bedroom home offers the perfect blend of period character, comfort, and coastal charm.

The ground floor accommodation begins with a welcoming hallway with useful storage. The bright sitting room features double doors opening directly onto a private decking area to the rear garden, perfect for taking in the stunning sea views. The kitchen is well designed and full of character, with a Belfast sink, wood worktops, good storage, space for appliances, and a charming breakfast bar connecting to the living space. A comfortable double bedroom with built-in storage and a modern family bathroom, with shower over bath and underfloor heating, complete the ground level. Upstairs, the converted attic provides a spacious principal bedroom with Velux windows framing the spectacular coastal panorama. There is excellent eaves storage and a stylish en-suite bathroom with a modern three-piece suite and underfloor heating.

Externally, the rear garden is mostly laid to lawn along with raised beds and is perfectly positioned to make the most of the incredible views. A decked patio area just outside the house offers a wonderful spot for relaxation, while an insulated summerhouse with power provides an ideal space for a home office or studio, and there is also a useful garden shed, also with power, providing further storage and communal pathway. The property benefits from ample free on-street parking nearby.

13 Rhodes Cottages is a rare opportunity to own a characterful cottage in a breathtaking location. With stunning sea views, a charming interior, and a private garden with a summerhouse, this delightful home offers coastal living at its most picturesque.


Location

North Berwick lies on the East Lothian coast within easy commuting distance of Edinburgh and is one of the most sought-after towns in the county. It has a busy high street, with a wide range of local shops and restaurants and there is a Tesco and Aldi supermarket. North Berwick has two excellent golf courses, a yacht club, rugby club, tennis courts, a swimming pool and sports centre, and a luxury spa club is situated at the Marine Hotel. The town provides excellent local primary schooling and the well-regarded North Berwick High school. Private schooling is available at Compass in Haddington, Belhaven Hill in Dunbar, Loretto in Musselburgh with further choices available in Edinburgh. East Lothian has many fine walks by the sea and inland. Edinburgh can be reached in about 45 minutes by car or by train in half an hour and there are extensive bus services.
