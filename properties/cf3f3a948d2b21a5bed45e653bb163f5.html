We are delighted to bring detached this family home in the popular village of Ratho to the market. Decorated to a high standard in walk in condition, this is a must view property.

The family house consists of 3 bedrooms, open plan living/dinning room separate kitchen, and family bathroom. There is a secure private rear garden plus garden and driveway at the front of the property. In additional there is a double garage/Storage area for parking.

The home comes equipped with an ultra modern central heating comprising an air source heat pump and gas boiler installed in December 2022.

The house home is located next door to the well regarded Ratho Primary School.

Living in Ratho, Edinburgh offers a charming blend of countryside serenity and city accessibility. Ratho is a historic village located to the west of Edinburgh, known for its picturesque surroundings and close-knit community. Residents enjoy the scenic beauty of the Union Canal, the lush Ratho Moors, and the quaint village centre.

Ratho may be a small village, but it's certainly not short on things to do

EICA: Close by are the leisure facilities at the EICA, including a gym, soft play and cafe in additional to its international acclaimed indoor climbing arena.

Union Canal: Take a leisurely stroll or bike ride along the Union Canal towpath.

Ratho Park: This green space offers a play area for children, open fields for picnics, and pathways for walking or jogging. It's a great place for families and those who enjoy spending time outdoors.

Bridge Inn: Visit this historic pub, which not only offers great food and drinks but also sits right along the canal, providing a beautiful view and a cosy atmosphere.

The peaceful ambiance of Ratho provides a refreshing escape from the hustle and bustle of Edinburgh. Yet, being just a short distance from the city, you have easy access to urban amenities, cultural attractions, and employment opportunities. Commuting is relatively convenient, whether by car, bus, or even bike along the canal towpath.
