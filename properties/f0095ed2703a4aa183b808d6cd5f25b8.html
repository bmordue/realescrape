Semi-Detached House

3 Bedrooms

Situated on the edge of Milngavie village, this traditional SEMI VILLA offers excellent family accommodation with a flexible layout and benefitting from gas central heating and double glazing.

Double glazed and PVC panelled front door onto entrance hall with cloaks cupboard, near 18' lounge with aspects to both front and rear, larger breakfasting kitchen overlooking the rear garden and having a double glazed and PVC door providing access, separate dining room or alternatively bedroom three with aspects to front and built-in recessed wardrobe.

First floor: large shelved storage cupboard on landing with good natural light and stairwell provided by window to rear, two double bedrooms (each with built-in storage) and partially tiled shower room comprising three piece suite.

The property is set amidst easily maintained gardens to both front and rear. Driveway to side providing access to single car timber garage.

The property is conveniently situated for schooling at both primary and secondary levels, excellent shopping available including a Waitrose, Tesco, M&S Food and Milngavie Station.
