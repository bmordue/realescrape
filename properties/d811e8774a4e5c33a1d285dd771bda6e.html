Glenhelen, Spycraig Road,
Dalbeattie DG5 4HP

Offers over £180,000

Detached House

3 Bedrooms

Glenhelen is located in a quiet position, only a short walk away from Dalbeattie town wood. Dalbeattie offers primary and secondary schooling, shops, local food stores and a health centre. Walkers and mountain bikers are also well catered for in the region with the town woods just minutes away and the 7Stanes cycle tracks on hand in the Dalbeattie Forest, less than a mile away. The "Granite Town" of Dalbeattie is the gateway to The Solway Coast and is 4 miles from the sailing village of Kippford, with the sandy beaches of Rockcliffe & Sandyhills just a few more minutes away.

Detached three bedroom granite home offering newly decorated, spacious accommodation. The property benefits from gas central heating and UPVC double glazing, except where specified. Externally, there is an enclosed, easily maintained garden to the rear with outbuilding and a parking bay to the front. Glenhelen would make an ideal family home.

Viewing is highly recommended to appreciate the accommodation on offer.
