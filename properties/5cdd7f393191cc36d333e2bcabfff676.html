9/12 Murano Place,
Edinburgh EH7 5HH

Fixed Price £178,000

Third Floor Flat

1 Bedroom

This one bedroom top/third-floor flat lies just off bustling Leith Walk, which offers a variety of shops and a wealth of great cafés, bars and restaurants. Also benefiting from swift access into the city, the flat offers an ideal home for professionals or those hoping to invest in the rental market. Welcoming you into the is an inviting hallway, which offers access to a handy cupboard. Directly ahead is a well-proportioned lounge and dining room, enjoying a tranquil aspect to the rear, conveniently positioned adjacent is the fitted kitchen. Completing the accommodation is a good-sized double bedroom (with built-in mirrored wardrobes) and a tasteful shower room comprising a quadrant shower enclosure. Leading off the shower room is a large store cupboard. The property benefits from secure residents parking area to the rear. The development is factored by Charles White for the maintenance of the communal areas and building insurance. The cost is approx. £97 per month. Extras: all fitted floor coverings, window coverings, light fittings, and all kitchen appliances will be included in the sale. Furniture also included in the sale price. A prime central location, just minutes from the city centre's east end. There is a wealth of local amenities to choose from, and the addition of the attractions offered by the city centre; great bars, restaurants and cultural venues. Within easy reach are the Omni centre, which has a cinema, a number of eateries and gym and the Playhouse Theatre. Meadowbank Retail park offers additional shopping, as well as it's sports centre and stadium, for leisure activities. Holyrood park and the Scottish Parliament are a 15 minute walk, as well as the Waverley Station and the tram network, which offers direct access to the west and Edinburgh International Airport.
