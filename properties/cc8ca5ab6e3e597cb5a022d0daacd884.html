This attractive top-floor flat enjoys a gable-end corner position within a traditional stone-built tenement in the Bonnington district, thereby offering an abundance of natural light from both front and side windows. The property blends modern upgrades, such as recently installed double glazing and the gas combi boiler, with period features like polished wooden floorboards, working shutters, and ceiling cornicing. Upgarding also includes re-plastering and painting. The decor is predominantly light and neutral in tone.

The location provides easy access to both Edinburgh city centre and Leith, making it particularly appealing to first-time buyers and investors interested in capital growth.

In today's dynamic marketplace, early viewing is strongly recommended.
