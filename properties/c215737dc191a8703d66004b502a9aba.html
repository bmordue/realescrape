Detached Bungalow

2 Bedrooms

Well-presented two-bedroom detached bungalow in move-in condition, situated within the popular new Pitcrocknie Village housing development.

Lindsays are delighted to bring to the market this well-presented two-bedroom detached bungalow, offered in true move-in condition and located within the popular Pitcrocknie Village housing development just outside Alyth. The property benefits from gas central heating, double glazing, solar panels and an EV charging point. Included in the sale are all floor coverings, light fittings and blinds where fitted, along with all integrated white goods.

The accommodation is entered via a welcoming hallway which offers excellent built-in storage. To the rear of the property is a bright and spacious open-plan kitchen, dining and living area, ideal for modern living, providing ample space for cooking, dining and relaxing. Patio doors open directly onto the rear garden, allowing plenty of natural light and an easy connection to outdoor space. There are two very generously sized double bedrooms, both benefiting from excellent storage, and a modern shower room fitted with a walk-in shower.

Externally, the property enjoys a driveway providing parking for multiple vehicles. The front garden is attractively laid out with a low-maintenance chipstone finish, while the main garden is located to the rear and is fully enclosed, offering a good level of privacy. The rear garden features a lovely decking area ideal for outdoor entertaining, with a garden shed included in the sale.

This attractive bungalow will appeal to a wide range of buyers including downsizers, professionals and those seeking a modern, low-maintenance home in a popular residential setting. Early viewing is highly recommended.
