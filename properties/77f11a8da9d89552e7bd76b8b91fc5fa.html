Located in the highly regarded Craigowl Law development on the northern edge of Dundee, 16 Newall Avenue offers a rare opportunity to acquire an exceptional example of the Tambrook house type, built by Avant Homes. This four-bedroom detached villa has been extensively upgraded by the current owners and is offered in immaculate, move-in-ready condition, showcasing high-end fixtures and fittings throughout.

On entry, the property opens into a spacious and welcoming hallway, setting the tone for the quality and generous proportions found throughout. To the rear, the expansive open-plan kitchen and dining area forms the heart of the home. Thoughtfully enhanced, the kitchen is fitted with premium integrated appliances, stylish cabinetry, and quartz work surfaces, all finished to an exacting standard. French doors lead directly out to the garden, creating an inviting space for entertaining and everyday family life.

Adjoining the kitchen is a practical utility room with additional built-in storage, as well as a large downstairs WC – both finished with the same attention to detail. The main lounge to the rear features elegant bi-fold doors which open out to a landscaped private garden, offering an effortless connection between indoor and outdoor living.

To the front of the home is an additional, versatile reception room—ideal as a second lounge, home office, or snug—providing a peaceful retreat from the main living areas. The ground floor is further enhanced by internal access to the integrated garage and multiple storage cupboards.

Upstairs, the accommodation continues to impress. The main bedroom is generously proportioned and benefits from built-in wardrobes and a luxurious en-suite shower room, complete with designer tiling and contemporary sanitaryware. Two further double bedrooms and a spacious single bedroom are all beautifully presented and serviced by a modern family bathroom with high-spec finishes.

A large landing offers further built-in storage and connects all bedrooms, maintaining a bright and open feel across the upper level.

Externally, the property enjoys a well-maintained private garden to the rear, which has been professionally landscaped for low-maintenance enjoyment and year-round appeal. To the front, a monoblocked driveway provides off-street parking and leads to the integral garage.

The Craigowl Law development is a popular and modern residential area located to the north of Dundee, with stunning open views toward the surrounding countryside and hills. The location offers excellent access to local amenities, highly regarded primary and secondary schools, and regular public transport links. Dundee city centre, Ninewells Hospital, and Dundee Technology Park are all within easy reach by car, making this an ideal base for commuting professionals and families alike.
