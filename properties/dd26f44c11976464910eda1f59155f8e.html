99 Harlawhill Gardens,
Prestonpans EH32 9JH

Offers over £265,000

Semi-Detached House

4 Bedrooms

Accompanied by private parking and a low-maintenance secure garden, this attractive four-bedroom semi-detached house is quietly nestled at the end of a cul-de-sac in the popular seaside town of Prestonpans. It is ideally situated within walking distance of local shopping, the seafront, and the train station, where services connect to central Edinburgh in just 15 minutes. The modern family home is well-presented with a neutral finish, and its interiors include a trio of connected living and dining spaces and a washroom on each level. Welcoming you inside is a naturally lit entrance hall featuring durable oak-style flooring and useful storage. From the hall, you step into a comfortably carpeted living room benefiting from a sunny, southwest-facing aspect, built-in storage, and handy kitchen access. The bright kitchen incorporates a social seated dining area that flows into a multi-purpose sunroom leading onto the garden. In addition to cabinet storage and workspace, appointed in tasteful cream and wood tones, the well-equipped kitchen comes neatly integrated with a fridge, a freezer, a dishwasher, a washing machine, an oven, and an electric hob with a feature subway-tiled splashback and chimney-style hood. Completing downstairs is a spacious bay windowed principal bedroom elegantly presented with plush carpeting and delicate accent wallpaper. It is fitted with stylish, mirrored wardrobes and conveniently located beside a chic shower room. The remaining bedrooms are upstairs, reached via a bright landing with storage. These comprise two doubles, one with fitted wardrobes, and a single bedroom (with storage) that is presently used as a study. A bright family three-piece bathroom serves the first floor. The home is kept warm and efficient by electric heating and full double glazing. Externally, the house has well-tended lawns to the front and rear. The securely enclosed rear garden also boasts a seating terrace for alfresco dining. To the front is a private driveway and a handy external store. Extras: The sale includes all fitted window coverings, blinds, light fittings, and appliances.
