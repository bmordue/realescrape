29 Glenashdale Way,
Paisley PA2 7QG

Offers over £130,000

Terraced House

2 Bedrooms

Located in the Dykebar area of Paisley, this well-maintained mid-terrace villa offers a fantastic opportunity for first-time buyers, young families, or downsizers looking to create their ideal home. Set within a peaceful residential street, the property combines comfort, potential, and location - making it a must-see. There is a bright and spacious entrance hallway giving access to all ground floor rooms and the staircase to the upper level. The generously sized living room is flooded with natural light from a large front-facing window, creating a welcoming space for relaxing or entertaining. To the rear, the well-proportioned kitchen features a range of fitted units and ample space for appliances, including an oven, hob, and washing machine. Just off the kitchen is a practical rear hallway/boot room with built-in storage and direct access to the private rear garden - ideal for families and pet owners. Upstairs, the property offers two spacious double bedrooms, both with newly laid carpets, and a family bathroom complete with a three-piece suite and over-bath shower. Additional features include: Gas central heating Double glazing Private front and rear gardens, with the rear enjoying tiered levels and elevated views - perfect for summer barbecues or a peaceful morning coffee. Paisley is fast becoming one of the most desirable places to live in the West of Scotland, offering an unbeatable combination of affordability, transport links, and amenities. With excellent road and rail connections to Glasgow city centre, and only minutes from Glasgow Airport, it's ideal for commuters. The town itself boasts a vibrant cultural scene, historic architecture, a range of local shops, parks, and excellent schooling.
