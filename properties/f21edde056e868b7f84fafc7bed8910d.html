Location
Blairhall is a small village in West Fife, situated approximately a mile west of Comrie and 7 miles west of Dunfermline. Blairhall offers a blend of historical charm and community life.

The village is served by Blairhall Primary School, which caters to children from Blairhall and the surrounding areas. The Blairhall Community Centre offers a variety of activities for all age groups, including parent and toddler groups, youth clubs, and senior citizen groups. Facilities include a main hall, meeting rooms, a cafe, and a garden. It is surrounded by scenic woodlands and nature reserves. To the south lies Balgownie Wood, offering opportunities for walking and wildlife observation.
