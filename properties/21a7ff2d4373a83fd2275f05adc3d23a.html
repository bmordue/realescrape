Coorie Lodge, 2 Trinity Lane, Castle
Douglas DG7 1BS

Offers over £295,000

Detached Bungalow

4 Bedrooms

Coorie Lodge is located at the end of a small, quiet lane, just off Abercromby Road in the centre of Castle Douglas. The property enjoys lovely open views over Castle Douglas golf course to the front. Castle Douglas has a good range of individual shops, supermarkets, primary and secondary schools, park with loch, churches, theatre, swimming pool and all other facilities commensurate with a town of its size.

Beautifully presented 4 bedroom detached bungalow in a quiet location within Castle Douglas. Coorie Lodge benefits from spacious accommodation with stunning fully renovated interior decoration, generous garden plot and beautiful views overlooking the golf course.

The current owners have fully modernised the property over the last two years, including an upgrade of the electrics and installation of a new bespoke kitchen, shower room, bathroom, floor insulation, flooring, wood burner, decking, doors and windows and landscaping of the garden grounds. The property has UPVC double glazing and gas central heating throughout.

2 Trinity Lane would make an ideal family home.

Viewing is highly recommended to appreciate the accommodation on offer.
