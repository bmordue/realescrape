4 Windsor Square,
Penicuik EH26 8ES

Offers over £190,000

Semi-Detached House

4 Bedrooms

Viewing is highly recommended of this 3/4 bedroomed semi-detached house offering flexible living within the popular commuter town of Penicuik. Penicuik is a Midlothian town in a rural setting and offers good local shopping facilities along with a choice of public transport. Further shopping can also be found at Straiton Retail Park offering a selection of High Street Stores. Quick and easy access is available to the City Bypass and motorway links with Edinburgh International Airport a short journey away. Lying between the Pentland Hills and River North Esk the adjacent countryside caters for a wide range of outdoor pursuits including fine walks, cycling, golfing and the Hillend dry-ski facility. The property opens to a welcoming entrance hall which has stairs to the upper floor, two deep storage cupboards, and much of the downstairs accommodation off. To the front is a dual aspect living room with carpeted floors and looking out onto the front and rear gardens. To the rear is the kitchen which features base and wall units, built in pantry cupboard, shelving and gives access to the rear garden. The kitchen benefits from several white goods including a slot in cooker, a washing machine, tumble dryer and a fridge freezer. The dining room, which would also make an ideal fourth bedroom, has a WC/cloakroom off which features a wash hand basin, WC, and tiled walls. The stairs take you to a landing which benefits from two built in double cupboards and hatch to the loft space. To the front is a double bedroom with two large built-in wardrobes. Two additional double bedrooms are found to the rear, both with built in cupboards Completing the accommodation is the rear facing family bathroom, with partially tiled wall, bath with overhead electric shower, WC, wash hand basin and a heated towel rail. The house has private front and rear gardens which are fully enclosed and feature a handy garden shed for further storage. Additional benefits include gas central heating, double glazing, and unrestricted on street parking.
