42 Martinton Road, Heathhall,
Dumfries DG1 3QS

Offers over £165,000

Semi-Detached House

3 Bedrooms

42 Martinton Road is located within the popular residential area of Heathhall, with easy access to primary school, nursery and shops. It is on the regular bus route to Dumfries town centre, approximately three miles away, where a wide range of amenities can be found.

Semi-detached, three bedroom house offering spacious accommodation. The property benefits from multiple reception rooms, generous garden grounds, UPVC double glazing and gas central heating throughout. A single garage and driveway with space for several vehicles, complete the accommodation on offer. 42 Martinton Road would suit couples and families alike.

Viewing is recommended to appreciate the accommodation on offer.
