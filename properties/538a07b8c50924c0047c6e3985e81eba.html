This beautifully presented, extended detached bungalow is situated in the sought after Angus village of Letham, and convenient for all amenities. Letham boasts a wide cross section of facilities, including primary school, bowling green, hotels, shops, hairdressers, and pharmacy. Nearby Forfar and Arbroath provide a full range of services, and Dundee is within comfortable driving distance.

The property offers spacious and well proportioned accommodation, all at ground floor level, and is in excellent decorative order having been freshly decorated throughout. The subjects benefit from double and triple glazing, gas fired central heating with combi boiler, multi fuel burning stove in lounge, a modern fitted kitchen with open plan dining area, a modern bathroom with electric shower, and two double bedrooms both with fitted wardrobes, and with main bedroom having En Suite shower room. An added attraction is the sun lounge to rear which enjoys a pleasant outlook over the gardens.

There is driveway parking for a number of vehicles, and single garage with power and light. The gardens to front are laid out for ease of maintenance, and the rear gardens are beautifully landscaped, all enjoying a degree of seclusion and privacy.

This is an excellent opportunity to obtain a quality bungalow of this style and location and viewing is essential to fully appreciate.
