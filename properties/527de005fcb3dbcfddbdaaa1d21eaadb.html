71 St Ninians Terrace presents an excellent opportunity to acquire a well-proportioned family home within the popular and well-established St Mary’s area of Dundee, offering adaptable accommodation formed over two levels and presented to the market in true move-in condition throughout

The property is entered at ground floor level where a welcoming reception hall provides access to the principal living areas. To the front of the home lies a generously proportioned lounge which enjoys excellent natural light, a feature woodburning stove and offers an ideal setting for both everyday living and formal entertaining. The kitchen is positioned to the rear and is well appointed with contemporary base and wall units, contrasting work surfaces and space for freestanding appliances. Also located on the ground floor is the modern family bathroom, fitted with a four-piece suite including full sized bath, separate walk in shower, wash hand basin and WC.

A staircase from the hall rises to the first-floor landing which gives access to three well-proportioned bedrooms, all of which benefit from good natural light and flexible use depending on individual requirements. The principal bedroom offers excellent space for bedroom furniture, while the remaining two bedrooms are equally well suited to guest accommodation, children’s rooms or home office use, ensuring the property adapts easily to a variety of lifestyles. The homes spacious attic space has been converted to offer a handy space for a range of potential uses including additional storage or a handy home office.

Externally, the property benefits from private outdoor space which provides a valuable extension of the internal accommodation and offers an ideal environment for relaxing, entertaining or everyday family use. A large shed in the rear garden currently offers an outdoor gym but could be repurposed to suit a wide range of needs depending on the owners requirements. To the front the property offers a private driveway for two cars with additional on street parking readily available nearby.

St Ninians Terrace is a well-regarded and conveniently positioned location, ideally placed for a wide range of amenities including local shops, supermarkets, schooling and leisure facilities. Regular public transport services operate nearby, while swift access to the Kingsway ensures ease of travel throughout the city and beyond. Dundee city centre, Ninewells Hospital and both universities are all readily accessible, further enhancing the appeal to owner occupiers and investors alike.

Offered to the market in genuine move-in condition, this attractive home provides spacious, versatile accommodation within a consistently popular residential setting. Early viewing is strongly recommended to fully appreciate the quality of accommodation and the excellent opportunity on offer.
