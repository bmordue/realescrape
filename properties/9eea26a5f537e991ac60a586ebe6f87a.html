A rare opportunity to acquire this stunning, fully refurbished former Manse set within beautifully maintained gardens in the prestigious and peaceful setting of Chanonry Wynd, Brechin.

Refurbished to an extremely high standard this exquisite family home provides a luxurious blend of timeless period elegance and a wealth of charm ensuring a move in ready home of exceptional quality.

This impressive family home offers generous and flexible accommodation throughout.  The  ground floor comprises four elegant public rooms, all thoughtfully designed to maximise natural light and comfort, ideal for both formal entertaining and everyday living, along with a fabulous kitchen, forming the heart of the home.  A cloakroom/WC, larder and integral garage, with utility area, complete the ground floor accommodation.

On the upper floor the accommodation extends to five well proportioned bedrooms, including a principal suite complete with a stylish en suite bathroom and a well appointed family bathroom serves the remaining bedrooms, all of which are presented to an impeccable standard.

Externally, the property is surrounded by beautifully landscaped gardens, offering a peaceful retreat with ample space for outdoor entertaining, relaxation, and family enjoyment. The quiet setting enhances the sense of privacy while remaining within easy reach of local amenities and transport links.

Positioned within one of Brechin’s most desirable addresses, this outstanding home combines seclusion with convenience, making it a rare and compelling offering for the discerning buyer and early viewing is essential to fully appreciate the space, elegance, and lifestyle opportunity this wonderful home presents.
