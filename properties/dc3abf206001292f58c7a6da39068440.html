Spacious 2-Bedroom Semi-detached House

Situated in a charming neighbourhood. This delightful 2-bedroom property is well decorated with a spacious layout.

As you enter, you'll find a good-sized living room that serves as the heart of the home, perfect for relaxing or entertaining guests. The space is bright and airy, with abundant natural light that creates a pleasant ambiance. The spacious well-equipped kitchen features a modern design, equipped with ample counter and storage space. The property includes two spacious bedrooms with large built-in wardrobes in both. The home also includes a modern shower room with WC . One of the standout features of this property is the, bright spacious south facing conservatory overlooking the mature, well maintained garden.

Step outside to discover more of the good-sized garden covering the front, side and rear of the property, perfect for outdoor activities and a spot of gentle gardening.

Loanhead is situated just a few miles south of Edinburgh, making it an ideal location for those who work in the city but prefer a quieter lifestyle. The town offers easy commuting options via a regular bus service and easy access to major roadways via the Edinburgh City Bypass which is only a few minutes drive away.

Loanhead offers a mix of tranquillity and accessibility. Its proximity to Edinburgh allows residents to enjoy city life while benefiting from a pleasant community atmosphere. Loanhead stands out as an excellent choice for individuals and families alike looking for a balanced lifestyle. Despite its small size, Loanhead is equipped with essential amenities, including local shops, a couple of quality restaurants, schools, and healthcare facilitieswhile the leisure centre includes a swimming pool and local library. There are two parks within a 2 minute walk of the property and plenty walks including Straiton Pond Nature Reserve and the old railway line where you can walk and cycle. Straiton Retail Park is within a 5 minute drive boasting a number of well known grocery stores, clothing and homeware outlets when you need to stock up.
