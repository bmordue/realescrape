4, Greenfield Crescent,
Dundee DD4 0FT

Offers Over £280,000

Semi-Detached Bungalow

4 Bedrooms

  • Immaculately presented home within the ever-desirable Castleview Park development
  • Impressive open-plan lounge/kitchen/dining area with French doors to garden
  • Two well-proportioned ground floor double bedrooms
  • Contemporary family bathroom with separate shower enclosure
  • Two further bedrooms upstairs, the principal benefiting from a stylish en-suite shower room and walk-in wardrobe
  • Private garden grounds to the front and rear, with summer house included in the sale
  • Off-street private driveway for multiple vehicles

Situated within the ever desirable Castleview Park development, 4 Greenfield Crescent is an immaculately presented four-bedroom semi-detached home offering spacious, contemporary accommodation perfectly suited to modern family living. Combining stylish interiors with generous private front and rear gardens, this impressive property provides a superb balance of comfort, practicality and entertaining space, making it an ideal home for growing families and those looking to enjoy a move-in-ready property in a sought-after residential setting.

The accommodation is thoughtfully arranged over two levels and has been designed to maximise both space and natural light. Upon entering the property, a welcoming entrance hall provides access to the principal living accommodation, a useful under-stair storage cupboard and the staircase leading to the first floor.

The true heart of the home is the impressive open-plan kitchen, dining and lounge area which spans almost the full length of the property. This superb family space offers clearly defined areas for cooking, dining and relaxing whilst maintaining an effortless flow throughout, making it equally suited to everyday family life and entertaining guests. The modern fitted kitchen provides an excellent range of wall and base units together with generous worktop space, while the dining area comfortably accommodates a family-sized table before flowing seamlessly into the bright and spacious lounge. French doors open directly onto the private rear garden, creating an excellent connection between the indoor and outdoor living spaces during the warmer months.

Also on the ground floor are two well-proportioned double bedrooms, both offering flexible accommodation to suit a variety of lifestyles. Whether utilised as comfortable bedrooms, guest accommodation, a home office or playroom, these versatile rooms ensure the property can easily adapt to changing family requirements. The contemporary family bathroom is conveniently positioned between the bedrooms and has been finished to a modern standard, complete with a bath and separate shower enclosure.

The first floor is dedicated to the remaining bedroom accommodation, creating an ideal level of privacy. The generous principal bedroom benefits from its own stylish en-suite shower room and an adjoining walk-in wardrobe, providing an excellent principal suite. A further spacious double bedroom with built-in wardrobe completes the upper level.

Externally, the property enjoys private gardens to both the front and rear. The enclosed rear garden provides a fantastic space for children and pets to play safely while also offering the perfect setting for outdoor dining, summer entertaining or simply relaxing in the sunshine. A well presented summer house is also included in the sale. The front garden enhances the property's kerb appeal and creates an attractive approach to the home and provides private off street parking for multiple vehicles.

Greenfield Crescent enjoys an excellent location within easy reach of a wide range of local amenities, including reputable schooling, supermarkets, leisure facilities and excellent transport connections into Dundee city centre and beyond. Combining generous living accommodation with a highly desirable layout and modern presentation throughout, this exceptional family home is certain to appeal to a wide range of purchasers.

Early viewing is highly recommended to fully appreciate the quality of accommodation, spacious layout and enviable lifestyle this superb home has to offer.
