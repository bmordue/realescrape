Detached House

This extended detached villa sits in a great corner plot in the popular Hawkhead Estate. This has always been a desirable spot for families looking to settle in Paisley and has a good proximity to local schooling. This particular house sits in an enviable position on the edge of the countryside affording the uninterrupted views over the fields to the rear. It is currently used as a four bedroom property with two public rooms but could be a three bedroom with three public rooms as one bedroom is on the ground level. The current owners even considered a remodel of the house which could perhaps inspire a new owner to really make the house their own.
