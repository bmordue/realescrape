Semi-Detached Bungalow

"Well-presented tow bedroomed semi-detached bungalow on good sized corner plot with easily maintained garden grounds and off road parking"


Dalbeattie offers primary and secondary schooling, shops, local food stores and a health centre. Walkers and mountain bikers are also well catered for in the region with the town woods just minutes away and the 7Stanes cycle track in the Dalbeattie Forest, less than a mile away. The "Granite Town" of Dalbeattie is the gateway to The Solway Coast, and is 4 miles from the sailing village of Kippford, and the sandy beaches of Rockcliffe & Sandyhills just a few more minutes away.

Well-presented semi-detached bungalow with parking, set in easily managed gardens, within a sought after residential area on the edge of Town, and a short walk from the main bus route. The property enjoys light and airy rooms is in good decorative order. It benefits from double glazed windows and electric heating. There are level garden areas to the front, side and rear, laid mainly to grass with off road parking to the side. There are views from the rear of the bungalow to Munches HiIls.
