Offers over £250,000

Semi-Detached House

3 Bedrooms

A beautifully presented three bedroom semi-detached house in the popular village of Roslin. The location allows easy access to the city by-pass and to central Edinburgh and is also within easy reach of the many amenities at nearby Straiton. The internal layout is ideal for the family buyer with a bright and welcoming hallway giving access to the sitting room to the front and a well-equipped breakfasting kitchen to the rear. The kitchen is equipped with a gas hob, double electric oven, fridge/freezer, Belfast sink and dishwasher and there is direct access from here to the rear garden. Upstairs are three bedrooms and a contemporary shower room. The principal bedroom is equipped with extensive built-in wardrobe space. From the upper landing there is access to a large, floored attic room. There is planning permission in place for an attic conversion and also to create an open plan kitchen/dining/ sitting room on the ground floor. Externally there is a private area of lawn to the front and side of the house, two areas of private rear garden (one of which houses a large garden shed) and areas of shared garden to the side and rear. The property is fully double glazed and has gas central heating.
