29 Southerton Gardens,
Kirkcaldy KY2 5NG

Offers over £379,000

Detached House

4 Bedrooms

Sumptuous executive detached family villa with large double garage, courtyard parking and fabulous landscaped gardens backing onto woodland and Raith Lake. This rarely available and highly prestigious property is located within one of the town's most sought after residential locations and will not fail to impress the most discerning viewer. Formed over 3 1/2 levels and comprising: On the first level, you will be impressed with the very bright and airy lounge flooded with natural light from the front facing window and a balcony to the rear with elevated woodland and lake views. This room leads through an archway to a dining room which overlooks the back garden and there is a spacious and stylish modern dining kitchen with appliances. This level is completed with a handy utility room and separate WC. Staircase leads down to the expansive lower level family / garden room with French door leading to and two large windows overlooking the wonderful, mature and private back garden. On the upper level, you will be impressed with the bedroom accommodation which features a very spacious master bedroom with fitted wardrobes and a large en-suite bathroom with separate shower cubicle. There are 3 further good-sized bedrooms (including 1 with a modern en-suite shower-room), and the accommodation is completed with a family bathroom. There are fitted wardrobes in each bedroom and storage space throughout.

Outside, you will be delighted with the beautifully landscaped and very private back garden which backs onto woodland and Raith lake. This beautiful garden is an ideal escape for balmy summer evenings and ideal for family entertaining and alfresco dining. You will be impressed by the layout, which includes a patio and summer house patio. To the front, you will find a generous mono-block courtyard driveway with plenty of parking for multiple cars and a large double garage with concrete floor.

This lovely property is located within a cul-de-sac in a charming, leafy estate set just a stroll from the Beveridge Park, Kirkcaldy Rugby Club, Golf Club and primary and secondary schools. Southerton is an idyllic setting and well placed for lovely country walks and with the bonus of having the town centre just a short distance away where you will find an array of retail outlets, the Adam Smith Theatre, library, leisure centre, parks, beaches and fabulous riverside esplanade. This property is ideally located for easy access to the town's own bus and train stations and the A92 for commuting to Dundee, Edinburgh and beyond.
