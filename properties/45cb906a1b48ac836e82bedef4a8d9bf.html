Detached House

3 Bedrooms

An executive, four-bedroom detached villa located within a sought after residential address of similar homes. Located on the outskirts of Dunfermline's Eastern Expansion, the home offers spacious accommodation, upgraded gardens and will suit a variety of buyers. An excellent location for both local amenities and transport links with easy access to several local train stations, including Rosyth and Inverkeithing, offering a regular service to Edinburgh Waverly. Additional transport links via Halbeath and Inverkeithing Park and Ride with bus links to Edinburgh Airport. Less than half a mile to the M90 motorway.

Amenities within Duloch Park include various supermarkets, shops and food outlets with a variety of leisure facilities on offer. Fife Leisure Park also offers various restaurants, coffee shops, health club and a ten-screen cinema. Primary and secondary schooling within a short distance of the property with bus links available.

Paved driveway leading to double garage with additional parking space added by the current owners. Internal access to garage.
The internal accommodation briefly comprises of entrance hall with WC. Formal lounge to the rear of the property with feature fireplace and separate dining room. Impressive, high quality kitchen and family area to the rear of the home. Excellent storage throughout, including within the centre island, double oven, space for an American fridge freezer and retractable downdraft extractor fan within the centre island.

Master bedroom with built in wardrobes and en suite shower room and three additional bedrooms with built in wardrobes available within bedrooms two and three.
Upgraded, easy maintenance gardens to the rear consisting of raised composite decked area and patio. Benefitting from south west aspects, gardens also come with hot tub and are perfect for alfresco dining and entertaining within the summer months.

Viewing comes highly recommended to appreciate this beautiful, executive family home in a highly sought after location.
