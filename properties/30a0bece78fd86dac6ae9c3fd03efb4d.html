47 Robb Place, CASTLE
DOUGLAS DG7 1LW

Offers over £255,000

Detached House

4 Bedrooms

Immaculately presented, detached family home with off street parking and both front and rear gardens. The property enjoys well-proportioned, light filled rooms with the original garage having been converted into a sitting room/bedroom 4. It is in excellent decorative order throughout with modern fitted kitchen and shower room. There is UPVC double glazing and gas central heating throughout with a gas fire in the lounge.

The open plan lounge and dining room is an attractive family space with a tilt and slide patio door providing access to the rear garden. The modern fitted kitchen leads through into the main hallway of the house where the utility room and sitting room/bedroom are located. The utility room also grants access to the rear garden and contains a W.C. compartment. Upstairs there are two double bedrooms and a study/bedroom all served by a family shower room.

Outside, the property has off street parking with driveway and car port and both front and rear gardens with a garden shed.
