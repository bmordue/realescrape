This exceptional detached villa forms part of the highly regarded and award-winning Balgillo Heights development in Broughty Ferry. The property is an outstanding example of the ever-popular “Marr” house type, a design celebrated for its generous proportions, thoughtful family-focused layout and high-quality specification throughout. Occupying one of the finest plots within the development, 9 Ben Attow Terrace enjoys a truly enviable position backing directly onto open fields, offering a wonderful sense of privacy and an attractive open outlook rarely available within modern developments. The current owners further enhanced the home at the point of build with over £10,000 of carefully selected upgrades and optional extras, creating one of the best specified properties of its kind within Balgillo Heights. 

Extending to approximately 191 square metres, the accommodation has been designed with modern family living firmly in mind and offers an impressive balance of formal and informal living space. A welcoming reception hallway immediately sets the tone for the quality and scale found throughout, leading to a beautifully proportioned formal lounge with feature bay window flooding the room with natural light and creating an elegant setting for relaxing or entertaining.

To the rear of the home lies the true heart of the property – a spectacular open plan kitchen, dining and family space extending across the full width of the house. This superbly designed area offers exceptional versatility for modern lifestyles, with high-specification cabinetry, premium integrated appliances, stylish upgraded finishes and an impressive breakfast bar, all flowing seamlessly into generous dining and informal family seating areas. French doors open directly onto the rear garden, perfectly framing the open field outlook beyond and allowing indoor and outdoor living to blend effortlessly. A practical utility room with excellent storage, direct garden access and internal access to the integral double garage adds further convenience, while a contemporary cloakroom/WC completes the ground floor accommodation.

Ascending to the upper level, a spacious landing gives access to four exceptionally well-proportioned double bedrooms, all benefiting from fitted wardrobes. The principal bedroom is particularly impressive, offering a luxurious retreat complete with an upgraded en-suite shower room, while bedroom two also enjoys its own stylish en-suite facilities. Bedrooms three and four are equally generous and are served by a beautifully appointed and striking family bathroom. Featuring high-quality contemporary fittings, a central bath, separate shower enclosure and stunning green tiling it is a true standout feature of this exceptional home.

Externally, the property enjoys immaculate landscaped grounds with the rear garden being a particular highlight. Carefully designed to maximise the exceptional setting, this private outdoor space provides an ideal environment for family life, entertaining or simply enjoying the uninterrupted open outlook across the surrounding fields – a level of privacy and tranquillity that is seldom found in modern developments. The monobloc driveway provides ample off-street parking and leads to the substantial integral double garage.

Offering the perfect combination of luxury specification, exceptional plot positioning and one of Broughty Ferry’s most sought-after residential addresses, this is a rare opportunity to acquire a truly standout family home presented in outstanding move-in condition throughout.

There is a factoring arrangement in place for the upkeep of common areas including the maintenance of any common buildings, outdoor areas, stair cleaning and lift maintenance (where applicable). The current factor is James Gibb with a quarterly payment of approximately £48.
