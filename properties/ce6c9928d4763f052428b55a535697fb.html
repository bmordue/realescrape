9 Castle View Court, Cousland,
Dalkeith EH22 2RE

Offers over £370,000

Detached House

4 Bedrooms

Located on a quiet residential street, 9 Castle View Court is an immaculately presented, stunning four bedroom home, bursting with character and style, whilst offering delightful views of the surrounding fields, located in the lovely rural village of Cousland. The home benefits from an air source heat pump added by the current owners to increase energy efficiency. Beautiful wood flooring has been laid throughout the ground floor which bounces light throughout the home. The ground floor of the property comprises of: a welcoming hallway, a well presented open plan sitting room with log burning stove that follows into a sleek, generous kitchen/dining room with doors leading out to the patio and rear garden, in addition to a handy WC. On the first floor, is the charming, bright, principle double bedroom complete with an en-suite shower room, three generously proportioned double bedrooms and a further bathroom. At the entrance of the property, there is a monobloc driveway with space for to two vehicles leading up to the garage and a well-maintained front garden. To the rear of the property there is an impressive, large landscaped, terraced private garden with a patio and stunning views of the fields. Lying within easy driving distance of Edinburgh and close to the historic town of Dalkeith, the picturesque village of Cousland offers a peaceful rural retreat with a strong sense of community and family friendly village hall. An extensive choice of shopping and a wide range of recreational facilities are found in nearby Dalkeith, Musselburgh and Fort Kinnaird Retail Park. Schools are well represented, the property currently in the catchment area for Tynewater Primary School in nearby Pathhead, and Dalkeith High School. Wide ranging recreational facilities include scenic cycling and walking trails and a number of prestigious golf courses in the vicinity whilst nearby Vogrie Country Park and Dalkeith Country Park provide delightful environments for enjoying nature and the outdoors. Cousland is particularly convenient for access to the city bypass linking with major motorway networks with rail links operating between nearby Wallyford, Musselburgh and Eskbank stations and Edinburgh Waverley. All blinds, light fittings, fitted flooring and integrated appliances are included within the sale price except the light fittings in the living room and principle bedroom, and the wall mounted shelves.
