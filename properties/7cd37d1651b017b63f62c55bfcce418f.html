"A charming and spacious detached 3 bedroomed bungalow in a quiet residential area of Kirkcudbright within easy walking distance of the town centre"

Situated in a quiet cul-de-sac on the outskirts of Kirkcudbright, 4 Seggies is within easy walking distance of the town centre and all the local amenities including a variety of shops, hotels and restaurants. The town also has both primary and secondary schooling and health centre. There is also a swimming pool, 18 hole golf course, tennis courts, squash courts and bowling green. More rural pursuits include hill walking, fishing, mountain biking and sailing.

A charming well-presented detached 3 bedroomed bungalow in a peaceful position on the outskirts of Kirkcudbright. 4 Seggies is a spacious, well-proportioned bungalow with lovely garden and parking with carport. The property benefits from double glazing and gas fired central heating.
