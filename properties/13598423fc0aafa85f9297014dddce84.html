Terraced House

2 Bedrooms

Lindsays are delighted to present this spacious two bedroom villa, offered to the market in genuine move in condition throughout. Situated in a popular and well established residential area, the property is ideally placed for easy access to a range of local amenities including schools, shops, public transport links and excellent road connections for commuting both north and south. Its location and layout make it an ideal choice for couples, families or those seeking a comfortable and modern home in Blairgowrie. The property benefits from gas central heating, double glazing and full fibre. Included in the sale are all white goods, floor coverings, blinds where fitted along hive heating and ring door bell.

On entering the property, the hallway provides a bright and welcoming introduction to the home, benefiting from excellent storage space on both the ground and upper levels. A convenient WC is positioned at the front of the house, offering added practicality for day to day living.

The kitchen and dining area are generously proportioned, offering ample counter and cupboard space along with room for a dining table. Patio doors lead directly out to the rear garden, allowing natural light to fill the room and creating a pleasant connection between indoor and outdoor spaces. The living room is presented in a modern finish and offers a bright, comfortable and spacious area ideal for relaxation or entertaining.

Upstairs, the property features two exceptionally spacious double bedrooms, each benefitting from built in storage and plenty of natural light. A modern shower room is also located on the upper level, completing the well designed layout of the home.

Externally, the property offers residential parking to the front with EV charger included in the sale. The rear garden is fully enclosed and provides a private outdoor space mainly laid to lawn, ideal for families, pets or leisure. A garden shed is included in the sale.

This attractive and well maintained home represents an excellent opportunity for anyone looking for a spacious villa in a desirable and convenient Blairgowrie location.


Area

Blairgowrie is a picturesque market town nestled on the banks of the River Ericht.

The bustling town boasts a good array of local specialist shops, cafes, restaurants, hotels, schools and many leisure pursuits including the fabulous Rosemount golf club, fishing, shooting and hill walking which includes the wonderful Cateran Trail. The popular ski resort of Glenshee lies just half an hour north of the town. Edinburgh, Glasgow and Aberdeen are within 1.5 hours travelling distance by car with Dundee only 35 minutes away with all the amenities of a major city.
