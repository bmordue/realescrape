Detached House

5 Bedrooms

Nestled within mature, beautifully stocked gardens, this exceptional detached villa offers a rare blend of privacy, tranquillity and versatile family living. Built in 1995 and thoughtfully maintained, the property provides an impressive level of accommodation extending to five bedrooms, three bathrooms and three reception areas, making it ideally suited to growing families, multi-generational living or those seeking generous space to work and entertain from home. From the moment you arrive, the home exudes a warm and welcoming atmosphere. A bright entrance porch leads into an inviting reception hallway, setting the tone for the light-filled interiors beyond. At the heart of the home lies a superb dining kitchen, designed equally for everyday family life and social occasions. Featuring a central island, quality Neff ovens with Slide&Hide� doors, gas hob, dishwasher and wine fridge, this is a space where cooking, dining and conversation naturally come together. A practical utility room sits adjacent, offering additional storage, a pantry cupboard and a convenient WC. The kitchen flows seamlessly into a stunning garden room, where panoramic views of the grounds create a wonderful connection with the outdoors. French doors open directly onto the expansive decking, providing the perfect setting for al fresco dining, summer entertaining or simply enjoying the peaceful surroundings. For more formal occasions, the elegant dining room also benefits from French doors to the rear garden, while the generously proportioned lounge, complete with a charming box bay window, offers a relaxing retreat at the end of the day. The ground floor accommodation includes two spacious double bedrooms, including a principal suite with en-suite shower room, together with a contemporary family bathroom. The upper level provides exceptional flexibility, featuring a further sitting area and three additional bedrooms, creating an ideal space for children, teenagers or visiting guests. A substantial utility room on this floor offers exciting potential to be reconfigured as an additional bedroom, home office, studio or reception room, subject to requirements. A separate shower room completes the accommodation. Storage is excellent throughout, complemented by an integral garage with electric door, ideal for secure parking, hobbies or further storage. The grounds are undoubtedly one of the property's most remarkable features. The extensive gardens have been lovingly cultivated and showcase an extraordinary collection of mature plants and shrubs, creating a private oasis reminiscent of a specialist nursery. Large decking areas, a covered outdoor seating space, timber shed, wood store and substantial carport capable of accommodating a caravan/ small motorhome combined to offer a lifestyle that embraces outdoor living throughout the seasons. To the front, a generous driveway provides ample off-street parking for multiple vehicles. Beyond the boundaries of the property, residents enjoy the best of both worlds; a peaceful setting overlooking Brodie Park while remaining within easy reach of everything Paisley has to offer. The town combines historic character with modern convenience, providing an excellent range of shopping, dining, leisure and educational facilities. Commuters are particularly well served, with swift access to Glasgow city centre, Glasgow Airport and the M8 motorway network. For those who enjoy the outdoors, Brodie Park is quite literally on the doorstep, while the scenic Gleniffer Braes offer miles of walking and cycling routes with spectacular views across Renfrewshire and beyond. It is this combination of tranquillity, connectivity and community that continues to make this part of Paisley such a sought-after place to call home.
