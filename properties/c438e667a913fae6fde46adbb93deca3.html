Offers over £189,999

Semi-Detached House

3 Bedrooms

Welcome to No155: This spacious semi-detached villa with driveway for off-street parking is ideally located in a popular, central, residential street with excellent amenities and facilities nearby. The property blends contemporary with art-deco tradition and features. The hub of the lower-floor accommodation is the bright and airy lounge with triple bay window formation and open fireplace to the front of the house. At the back of the house you will be impressed with the modern, spacious and recently installed dining kitchen with centre-island, appliances, and glazed door and picture window bathing the room with natural light and opening into the enclosed back garden. Moving on, you will be impressed with the large utility area / boot-room with door to the back garden and the pristine, contemporary shower-room. The ground floor is completed with a versatile room which could be an additional public room or bedroom 3 depending on user preference. You will then climb the bright staircase to the upper level where you will once again be impressed with the 2 stylish, generous, double bedrooms with lots of floor-space and storage. Accommodation is completed with a sumptuous, contemporary, part-tiled bathroom featuring a roll-top bath. Outside, there is a mature, enclosed garden to the rear with a lawn and patio area which is ideal for dining alfresco and entertaining friends and family. There is a large shed which will remain with the property and to the front you will find an easily maintained front garden and driveway to the side for off-street parking.

This property lies in a very popular area close to the town centre in the well served and historic town of Dunfermline which is just 5 miles from Queensferry Crossing and just 17 miles from Edinburgh, making it a prime location and a perfect base for commuters. The house is ideally located with excellent amenities within very easy reach, including Queen Margaret Hopsital, schools, the local train station and extensive retail, entertainment and sports facilities. There are bus and rail services plus the Park and Ride facility close-by, as well as Fife Leisure Park with its own cinema, restaurants and large selection of retail outlets also a short distance away.
