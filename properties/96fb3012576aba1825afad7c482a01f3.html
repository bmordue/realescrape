1 Stichill Eastfield Farm Cottages is a spacious, stone-built two-bedroom end-terraced cottage, beautifully refurbished by the current owner to a high standard and perfectly positioned in a peaceful rural setting just a few minutes? drive from Kelso. Offering a warm and welcoming feel throughout, the property features a cosy lounge with wood burner, generous dining kitchen, two good-sized double bedrooms and a stylish shower room. Enjoying stunning open views from every window, it also benefits from planning permission to extend the ground floor to add a utility room, WC and rear door, if desired. With ample private parking for several vehicles and a lovely front garden, this is an ideal countryside retreat and viewing is essential in order to fully appreciate.
