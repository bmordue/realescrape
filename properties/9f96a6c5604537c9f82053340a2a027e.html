The Kingsknowe Area
Kingsknowe is a well-connected residential district located about 3 miles southwest of Edinburgh city centre. It offers convenient commuting options, including Kingsknowe Railway Station with a 15-minute service to Waverley Station and nearby bus routes. The nearby city bypass provides quick car access to Edinburgh Airport and major motorway networks.
Residents can enjoy several local parks like Kingsknowe and Redhall, golf courses, and outdoor activities along the Water of Leith Walkway, Colinton Dell, and the Union Canal towpath. Sports facilities include the Craiglockhart Sports Centre and Green's Health Club. Entertainment venues such as the Edinburgh Corn Exchange at Slateford are close by.
Shopping needs are well covered with local shops, plus larger supermarkets including Asda and Sainsbury's at Slateford and Longstone. The Gyle Shopping Centre and Hermiston Gait Retail Park are just a short drive away for more extensive retail services.
Local schools include Longstone Primary, Canal View Primary and Kingsknowe is also conveniently close to Heriot-Watt and Napier Universities.
