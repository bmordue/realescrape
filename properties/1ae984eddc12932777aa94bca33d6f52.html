13 Kaimes View,
Danderhall EH22 1QZ

Offers over £170,000

Terraced House

2 Bedrooms

Early viewing is highly recommended for this two bedroomed terraced house ideal for a first-time buyer The property opens to an entrance hall with stairs to the upper landing and a handy under-stair storage cupboard. The dual aspect living/dining room offers a generous space with gas fire set within a fireplace with marbled hearth. The modern kitchen offers wall and base units, electric double oven, gas hob, fridge/freezer and washing machine. Upstairs there are two spacious double bedrooms one to the front and another to the rear both with built in wardrobes. A tiled shower room with 3 piece suite and shower off the mains completes this property. The property further benefits from gardens to the front and rear. The front the garden is laid to lawn and to the rear the garden has been paved for ease of maintenance. Extras - To include the aforementioned white goods (no warranties to be given) carpets, curtains and blinds. Set some five miles southeast of the city centre, Danderhall promises a fantastic setting for young families and professionals keen to escape the hustle and bustle of the capital, without leaving the city limits. Danderhall is also the perfect location for medical and research professionals working at Edinburgh Royal Infirmary and Edinburgh Bio Quarter, or staff at Queen Margaret University. Residents are just a five minute drive from Fort Kinnaird Retail Park and a 10 minute drive from an ASDA superstore, so have no shortage of retail and leisure facilities at their disposal. Sport and fitness enthusiasts can choose from a gym, fitness classes and court/pitch hire at Jack Kane Sports Centre or luxury facilities at Bannatyne Health Club & Spa in neighbouring Newcraighall and David Lloyd at Shawfair. Nearby schooling can be found at both Primary and Secondary levels. Danderhall and the nearby Sherrifhall Park and Ride are served by fast and frequent bus links into Edinburgh City Centre and across Midlothian. Shawfair train station also operates regular rail services between Edinburgh and Tweedbank along the Borders Railway Line. For travel further afield, proximity to the A1 and Edinburgh City Bypass guarantees swift links to the M8/M9 motorway network and Edinburgh International Airport.
