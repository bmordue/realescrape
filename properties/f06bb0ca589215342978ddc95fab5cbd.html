Semi-Detached House

3 Bedrooms

Semi-detached family home requiring refurbishment with gardens and garage. The property has well proportioned, light filled rooms with UPVC double glazed windows, gas central heating and a wood burning stove in the lounge. The ground floor comprises a lounge, dining kitchen which has direct access to the rear garden and bathroom. Upstairs, there are three double bedrooms, each with built-in storage and one of which has an en-suite shower room. Outside, the property comprises an easily managed front garden, grass area at the side and grass/drying green at the rear.

The property was originally of non-traditional "Orlit" reinforced concrete construction which was extensively renovated, modernised and improved by the local authority in the early 1980's. Further details are provided in the Home Report.

The property is located within an established residential development in the south-west of Castle Douglas. It is convenient for all local facilities and amenities in the town. Castle Douglas is a popular market town with a good range of independent shops, supermarkets, primary and secondary schools, park with loch, churches, theatre, swimming pool and all other facilities commensurate with a town of its size.
