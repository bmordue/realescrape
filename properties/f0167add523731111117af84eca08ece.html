Offers over £240,000

End Terraced House

3 Bedrooms

Warners are delighted to present this immaculately presented three-bedroom end-terraced villa, quietly positioned within a modern cul-de-sac development on the south side of Dalkeith. Beautifully upgraded and offered to the market in true walk-in condition, the property combines stylish contemporary interiors with excellent family living space, ideal for professionals, couples and growing families. The accommodation is entered via a welcoming hallway with a convenient ground floor WC. The bright dual-aspect living room offers an excellent space for relaxing and entertaining, with patio doors opening directly onto the rear garden. The impressive kitchen/dining room has been upgraded with striking dark grey and white contemporary cabinetry, integrated appliances and generous worktop space. Upstairs, there are three well-proportioned bedrooms, including a spacious principal bedroom with integrated wardrobe storage and a modern en-suite shower room featuring a waterfall-style shower. A stylish family bathroom with modern three-piece suite completes the accommodation. Externally, the enclosed rear garden has been designed for low maintenance with artificial lawn and an attractive Indian sandstone patio, ideal for dining and entertaining. Further benefits include gas central heating, double glazing, a partially floored attic and an allocated parking space. Situated close to Dalkeith's excellent amenities, schooling and transport links, the property is ideally placed for commuting into Edinburgh and beyond. Early viewing is highly recommended. Factoring: Hacking & Paterson Appox. £80 P/Q. EPC: C.
