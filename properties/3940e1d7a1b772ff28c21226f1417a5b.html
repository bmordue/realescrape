Offers over £160,000

Terraced House

2 Bedrooms

No. 8 Merlinford Way is a stylish modern mid-terrace villa tucked away at the peaceful cul-de-sac end of the ever-popular North Deanpark development. Beautifully maintained and presented in fresh, neutral décor throughout, this welcoming home offers comfortable living across two levels - perfect for first-time buyers, downsizers, or anyone seeking a move-in-ready property. The accommodation comprises a welcoming entrance hall leading to a bright rear-facing lounge, complete with a full-length feature window and door opening directly onto the garden - ideal for relaxing or entertaining. The modern fitted kitchen includes an electric hob and oven, while upstairs you'll find two generous bedrooms, both with ample storage, including mirrored wardrobes in the main bedroom. A sleek contemporary shower room completes the interior. Externally, the enclosed rear garden is mainly laid to lawn, providing a lovely private outdoor space, while the front garden and allocated parking add further practicality. Location - Why Living in Renfrew is So Appealing Situated in the heart of , this home enjoys the best of both convenience and community. The town offers a great mix of local shops, cafés, supermarkets, and everyday amenities, all within easy reach. For larger retail and leisure options, the nearby provides extensive shopping, dining, cinema, and entertainment facilities. Renfrew is also highly regarded for its excellent transport connections - with quick access to the M8 motorway, frequent bus routes, and easy commuting to Glasgow and beyond. Families will appreciate the well-regarded local schooling, green spaces, and friendly neighbourhood feel that make the area such a popular place to call home.
