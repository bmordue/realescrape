Detached House

4 Bedrooms

Number Nineteen is a spacious detached villa of 129sq ft of internal accommodation. Well maintained and set in a low maintenance plot, this family home is set in one of Bridge of Weir's most sought-after addresses. The internal accommodation comprises of a broad entrance hallway with cloakroom and WC off, open tread stairwell, front facing and dual aspect lounge, separate sitting room, modern breakfast kitchen with ample wall & base units with integrated appliances that include oven, hob extractor hood and fridge freezer. There is also a door to the side elevation. Completing the ground floor is a separate utility room with plumbing. The open tread stairwell leads to the first floor where there are four double bedrooms and a contemporary house bathroom complete with bath and separate walk-in shower. The attic is partly floored and can be accessed by a folding loft ladder, providing extra storage. There are nice open aspects over the rooftops from the front elevation. The private gardens are a real feature. There are stone chips around the front and perimeter to the paths and driveway that leads to the detached double garage. The enclosed rear garden has been designed for low maintenance with again, decorative stone chippings to the top tier of a three tier, neat and practical outdoor space. This well maintained family home also benefits from gas central heating (new boiler December 2025), double glazing and an electric up and over garage door. Bridge of Weir is a residential village, which offers a range of local shops and facilities which will adequately cater for everyday needs and requirements. Bridge of Weir falls within the Gryffe High School catchment and has its own village Primary School. For the commuting client, Bridge of Weir is located within 3 miles from the bypass which links up with the M8 motorway for connection to the airport, Paisley, Braehead Shopping Centre and Glasgow city centre. Furthermore, the village offers two golf courses, fishing on the River Gryffe and a selection of village pubs.
