Castlehill, Eskdalemuir,
Langholm DG13 0PJ

Offers over £395,000

House

3 Bedrooms

Castlehill is a very desirable stone built three bedroom detached cottage set in 1.7 acres of land in idyllic rural surroundings boasting unspoilt views over the River Esk and countryside beyond. Originally built in the mid 19th century the property has been sympathetically modernised over the years to a high standard. The accommodation briefly comprises entrance hall, large living room, kitchen, utility room, 3 bedrooms, bathroom and sun room. Outside is a well maintained surrounding garden, ample parking space, a paddock, stable, large garage, studio and workshop. There is also Planning Consent for the erection of 3 holiday camping pods. Castlehill sits in the Heart of Castle o'er Forest and has many paths and trails perfect for the outdoor enthusiast including cycling, horse riding, fishing and walking. It also sits on the Prehistoric Trail and is surrounded by ancient Iron Age Forts, Stone Circles and Monuments making it an interesting location with a fascinating history and an abundance of wild life to go with it, the perfect Retreat. The cottage is located approximately 12 miles from the town of Langholm where most amenities can be found. It is also only 14 miles from the larger town of Lockerbie which gives access to both the motorway and rail network.
