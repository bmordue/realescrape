6 Dickson Avenue, Hillside,
Montrose DD10 9EJ

Fixed £249,000

Detached House

3 Bedrooms

This three-bedroom detached home is set within the popular residential area of Hillside. Ideally situated 
for a family with Rosemount primary school within walking distance, or those looking to downsize into 
village life. Set over two floors comprising of lounge, bedroom, dining room, shower room, kitchen and 
conservatory on the ground floor, with two bedrooms and shower room on the first floor. To the outside 
the driveway provides off street parking leading to the garage with utility area, the front and rear mature 
gardens are beautifully presented including fruit trees. The property benefits from oil central heating and 
double glazing.
