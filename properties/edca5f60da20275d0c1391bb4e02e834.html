Detached House

5 Bedrooms

Lindsays are pleased to bring to the market this eye-catching five bedroom detached red sandstone family home set within a generous plot. Perth Road is ideally located with ease of access to local amenities such as schools, shops, public transport and great road links making it perfect for commuting both north & south bound. Norham oozes character throughout and benefits from gas central heating, wonderful original features and excellent storage. Included in the sale are all floor coverings, light fittings and white goods. 

This versatile home is split over two floors and entering from the front of the property comprises: Vestibule & hallway, bright and spacious open plan living room/drawing room with beautiful bay window bring in lots of natural light. Lovely family room, a dining room that leads to the large, fitted kitchen with a breakfast bar along with ample counter & cupboard space. A useful utility room and cloakroom/WC and access to the integrated garage & useful storage room finish off the downstairs. Upstairs is the stunning primary bedroom, boasting lovely views over the garden from the bay window and benefitting from modern ensuite shower room. Three further very generously sized double bedroom along with the fifth bedroom that has been utilised as an office. Finishing off the inside of the property is the family bathroom with separate shower and bath. 

Externally, the property lies on a generous, well maintained & fully enclosed garden ground. To the front is the driveway leading to the garage, with the garden itself mainly laid in lawn with mature plants, shrubs and bushes. To the rear of the property the south facing landscaped garden consist of a larger lawn area, a variety of fruit trees a lovely patio seating area with a summer house, potting shed included in the sale and also benefitting from a useful outhouse. 

These types of properties do not come along and early viewing is highly recommended to fully appreciate this grand family home. 


Area

Blairgowrie is a picturesque market town nestled on the banks of the River Ericht.

The bustling town boasts a good array of local specialist shops, cafes, restaurants, hotels, schools and many leisure pursuits including the fabulous Rosemount golf club, fishing, shooting and hill walking which includes the wonderful Cateran Trail. The popular ski resort of Glenshee lies just half an hour north of the town. Edinburgh, Glasgow and Aberdeen are within 1.5hours travelling distance by car with Dundee only 35 minutes away with all the amenities of a major city.
