Abbey Cottage, Abbey Lane,
Dumfries DG2 0DQ

Offers over £270,000

Detached Bungalow

3 Bedrooms

We are pleased to present this detached bungalow to the market for sale. The property is located in a quiet residential area with open areas to the front and side offering countryside walks close to the river. Lincluden Collegiate Church, or Lincluden Abbey, is situated nearby. Robert Burns visited and was inspired to write some of his songs here.
The town of Dumfries is a market town and former burgh near the mouth of the River Nith and sits on the A75 road between Gretna and Stranraer offering easy commuting access to the M74 to both north and south by road and with regular, direct, rail links to Edinburgh, Glasgow and Carlisle. The town has local shops and amenities including highly regarded primary and secondary schools..

The property is well maintained with recently fitted new roof and wall insulation and benefits from double glazing and gas central heating with new boiler.

Enter the property into the hall with the front bedroom on the left and the living room on the right. The living room has a Dowling multi fuel stove and electric blinds. This room is open plan into the kitchen area which is fitted with base units and has a fully shelved pantry and breakfast bar. Proceeding onto the hallway, there is a bedroom facing the side of the property. A door into the porch leads to outside and to the garden. Next along there is a convenient cloak cupboard, then the bathroom with shower enclosure and then an office / single bedroom. The attic is floored and insulated and there are roof vents in the roof for ventilation. Accessible from outside is the boiler room and a laundry room with sink and plumbing for washing machine.

The garden extends to approx. 1/2 acre with outbuildings and is laid in lawn. There is an large insulated workshop with power and lighting. The poly tunnel is included in the sale. There is a car port beside a lockable barn and the open fronted storage bays. The ride on lawnmower is also included in the sale. The septic tank is at the bottom of the garden. Returning to the dwellinghouse there are various garden areas with flowers, shrubs and vegetables and a seating area. Ample off road parking areas in the garden.
Viewing is highly recommended.
