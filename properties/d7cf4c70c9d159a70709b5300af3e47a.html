Detached House

5 Bedrooms

Beautifully nestled on a corner plot within a peaceful, family-friendly estate, this stunning five-bedroom, three-bathroom detached home offers the perfect blend of modern living and comfort. Immaculately presented throughout and upgraded to a great standard, it's ready for you to move in and enjoy.

The ground floor features a downstairs WC off the hallway, leading to a bright and spacious bay windowed lounge. The natural flow of the space leads through to a formal dining room, making it ideal for entertaining. The modern kitchen/diner is simply stunning, benefiting from a beautiful finish throughout, along with a large breakfast bar, which offers the perfect spot to stop and enjoy a coffee. The kitchen leads through to a well-equipped utility room, which will easily handle the laundry needs for the whole family. Off the kitchen, there's a beautifully bright and welcoming Sun-room, offering a great place for the family to relax in the warmth of the sunshine all year round. The ground floor accommodation is completed by the fifth bedroom, which, depending on requirements, could offer the perfect space for working from home or as a quiet space for the kids to study.

Upstairs, there are four generously sized bedrooms, complemented by a beautifully designed bathroom and two luxurious en-suites to the master bedroom and a Jack and Jill en-suite to bedrooms 3 and 4. The master bedroom also benefits from a beautiful dressing room.

Step outside to enjoy the stunning, low-maintenance, cool landscaped garden, a peaceful oasis for relaxing or entertaining. The property also features a detached garage and driveway, providing ample parking and storage space.

This stunning home offers everything a growing family could ever wish for. Contact us today to arrange your viewing!
