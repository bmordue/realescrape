Offers over £149,000

End Terraced House

2 Bedrooms

We are delighted to bring to market a lovely 2 bedroom end terrace in the popular Mayfield area of Dalkeith. This property would make a perfect family home or suit first time buyer. The property has fresh decor throughout and is in 'move in' condition. Entering the property into a light entrance hallway, the lounge is located to the left and is well presented with a feature wall complimenting the room and there is also access to the kitchen. The kitchen, also accessed from the hall, includes modern wall and base units, black laminate worktops and tiled splashback. Included within the sale are the integrated oven, hob, extractor and free-standing washing machine. There is also space for a free-standing fridge freezer. A separate cupboard in the kitchen hosts the meters and gives storage options, and the rear door to garden is also within this room. A large window giving plentiful natural light finishes the room. The hallway has under stair storage, is carpeted which continues up the staircase and on the upper landing. The upper landing has its own window, hosts a cupboard containing the combi boiler and gives access to all other rooms and the loft space. Bedroom 1 to the front of the property is very bright with natural light accessed from 2 windows and includes built in storage and carpeted flooring. Bedroom 2 to the rear is also a good-sized double room with window overlooking the rear garden. The bathroom includes a bath with mains shower over and screen, sink and wc with new flooring added and also has it's own window. The property is fully double glazed, benefits from external wall insulation and a combi boiler to ensure it's energy efficiency. Externally there is a large garden to front and side with a mixture of chipped areas and lawn. There is potential to incorporate a driveway to the side for off road parking subject to the usual planning requirements. At the rear there is an enclosed garden with separate patio area and shed. No warranties are given on the appliances Home Report Value - £155,000 Location Mayfield lies to the south of Dalkeith with a good range of of shopping facilities in the vicinity, mainly small specialist shops serving the local community. Further shopping, banking and postal services can be found at in Dalkeith or Newtongrange, both locations being easily accessible. Neighbouring Newbattle offers a golf course, bowling club and leisure centre. Schooling ranging from nursery through to secondary is within the vicinity. Regular bus services operate to Edinburgh city centre, with the nearby A7 linking to the city by-pass ensuring easy access to other outlying districts, Edinburgh Airport and the main motorway networks. Newtongrange's railway station is only a short drive away.
