63 Hays Walk,
Haddington EH41 3EH

Offers over £210,000

Terraced House

2 Bedrooms

Brought to market in a move-in condition with immaculate, contemporary interiors (and being sold with no onward chain), this mid-terraced house forms part of a modern development in Haddington and offers two bedrooms, a south-facing living/dining room, a stylish kitchen, a bathroom, and a separate WC. Externally, the house is accompanied by a sunny garden and benefits from access to unrestricted parking bays. A hallway welcomes you into the home, offering built-in storage and an immaculate two-piece WC and immediately setting the tone for the pristine interiors to follow with crisp-white décor and glossy wood-styled flooring. On your right, you step into a kitchen, where contemporary gloss-white and grey wall and base cabinets, spacious worktops, and metro-tiled splashbacks. Appliances comprising an oven, hob (paired with an eye-catching splashback), extractor fan, fridge/freezer, dishwasher, and washing machine are neatly integrated, complementing the sleek, modern finish. Continuing along the hall, you reach the reception room with space for lounge furniture and a dining area, if desired, and pared-back décor is enhanced by a stylish grey feature wall and the same flooring as the hall. The room is illuminated by south-facing French doors opening onto the garden. Upstairs, a landing leads to two well-proportioned double bedrooms, with the largest featuring neutral décor and a fitted carpet, and the other boasting chic, on-trend décor and a carpet for optimum comfort underfoot. The second bedroom is currently being utilised as a home office/sitting area. Finally, a stylish bathroom completes the accommodation on offer and comprises a bath with an overhead shower and a glazed screen, and a WC-suite. Gas central heating and double glazing ensure year-round comfort and efficiency. Externally, the home is accompanied by a south-facing, landscaped rear garden featuring a patio, a lawn, a large shed, and gated rear access to the parking area, where unrestricted bays can be found. Extras: All fitted floor coverings, window coverings, light fittings, integrated kitchen appliances, hall shoe storage, coat hanger, and garden shed will be included in the sale.
