22 Moray Park, Moray Street,
Doune FK16 6DJ

Offers over £135,000

First Floor Flat

3 Bedrooms

Tastefully presented and spacious, three-bedroom, first-floor, dual-aspect flat, with an allocated garage. Forming part of an established development, positioned on a quiet side street, in the historic and picturesque village of Doune, Perthshire.

Comprises an entrance hallway, living/dining room, kitchen, three double bedrooms, and a family bathroom.

Highlights include an impressive public room with a box bay window, a fitted kitchen and a generous bathroom suite. In addition, there is contemporary flooring throughout, double glazing, electric heating, and good storage.

Set adjacent to the open space for Moray Park, the development also provides a secure entry system and well-maintained communal garden grounds.

A welcoming entrance hallway provides access to all rooms throughout the property and benefits from useful built-in storage. The spacious living and dining room is a bright and comfortable space, enhanced by wood-effect flooring, two separate central light fittings and a wall-mounted TV point. A box bay window to the front aspect allows for excellent natural light and provides an ideal area for dining furniture or a seating nook, making this room well-suited for both relaxing and entertaining. Set to the front of the property, the fitted kitchen features tiled-effect flooring, stone-effect worktops and a tiled splashback surround, along with a stainless-steel sink and drainer. Appliances include a washing machine and a cooker with an electric hob and canopy extractor above. Well-planned cabinetry offers ample cupboard and worktop space for everyday storage and food preparation.

Positioned to the rear, the principal bedroom is a well-proportioned double room with wood-effect flooring, a central light fitting and space for additional freestanding furniture. Two further bedrooms are also located within the property, both offering flexibility for use as bedrooms, a home office or guest accommodation. Bedroom two benefits from a built-in wardrobe with sliding doors, providing excellent storage, while the remaining bedroom also enjoys a comfortable layout with space for storage furniture. Completing the accommodation, the modern fitted three-piece bathroom features tiled flooring, a stone-effect splashback surround, contemporary spotlighting, a shower over the bath and a ladder-style heated radiator.
