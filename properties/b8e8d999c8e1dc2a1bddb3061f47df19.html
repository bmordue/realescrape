End Terraced House

3 Bedrooms

An exceptional and versatile three-bedroom end-terraced home, occupying a generous corner plot with a beautifully maintained, private garden, within the highly sought-after residential area of Dalgety Bay. Offering a flexible layout across two floors, this well-presented home will appeal to a wide range of buyers, including families, downsizers and those seeking multi-generational living. The ground floor comprises a generous 15-foot lounge, a separate dining room and a well-fitted kitchen. A spacious ground-floor bedroom with its own private en suite provides excellent flexibility, whether used as a principal bedroom, guest suite or for those preferring single-level living. A useful store cupboard completes the ground floor. Upstairs, there are two further bedrooms served by a family bathroom, together with a landing cupboard providing additional practical storage. A standout feature of the property is the substantial detached outbuilding, extending to over 230 square feet, offering exceptional potential as a home office, studio, workshop, gym or extensive additional storage. Occupying a desirable corner plot, the property enjoys an exceptional, private garden laid to lawn, with direct access via French doors from the ground-floor bedroom, creating an ideal space for relaxing, entertaining or family life. Dalgety Bay is situated on the Forth Estuary approximately five miles to the southeast of Dunfermline. The well-established community provides a wide range of local shopping and recreational facilities including supermarkets, leisure centre, restaurants/ bars and primary schooling. Secondary schooling can be found in nearby Inverkeithing. Dalgety Bay is an ideal base for commuters with excellent public transport links to Edinburgh and close proximity to the A90, M90 motorway network. In addition the railway station provides regular links to Edinburgh and other neighbouring towns.EXTRAS INC.
