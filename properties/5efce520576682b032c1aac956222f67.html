Maindoor Flat

3 Bedrooms

20 JUNE 2024 AT 12 NOON An elegant main door dual level villa which benefits from private gardens to the front and rear, situated in a quiet street in the popular area of Pilrig. This beautiful property retains superb original features and offers the opportunity to create a stylish home in a sought-after location. In brief the accommodation comprises; inviting hallway with generous storage provisions and unique period features; modern fitted kitchen to the rear with wall and base units, solid wood worktop, modern tiling to splash areas and a rear door; off the kitchen is a formal dining room with a convenient Edinburgh Press and good size cupboard for more storage; to the front is a double bedroom with a corner bay window, high ceilings showcasing original cornice work and ceiling rose; the bathroom is also on the ground floor offering a white three piece suite, shower over the bath, storage for linen and a heated chrome towel rail; up the staircase which showcases a stunning stained glass window you will find the formal reception room with a striking period fireplace, a dual aspect including a similar corner bay window overlooking the leafy crescent, Furthermore the room benefits from cornice work and ceiling rose. Two further well-proportioned double bedrooms complete the accommodation. The property has been recently decorated and offers a true turnkey option for any discerning buyer. This property has been subject to virtual staging to show the effect of a makeover on the property. It should be noted that the property is currently empty as per the "before" images which have also been uploaded for perusal. To the rear is a secluded, private courtyard with a patio, ideal for outside entertaining or offering a peaceful retreat for a quiet morning coffee. Within the garden is a private outhouse with power and lighting which would make an ideal home office, studio or workshop. The street is residential permit parking for residents with a section of pay and display parking for visitors. The property is quietly positioned at the far end of the crescent, within the centre is a fantastic communal hub offering further green space for gatherings, or a child friendly area. Further green spaces are available at the nearby Pilrig Park for children and pets. Council Tax Band - E
