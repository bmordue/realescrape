Nestled amidst scenic countryside and peaceful woodlands, this stunning family home is located within the desirable village of Liff on the Western outskirts of Dundee. Situated just a few minutes from Ninewells Hospital and the A90 dual carriageway to Perth, the property combines the tranquillity of rural living with excellent access to nearby amenities and transport links.

Presented in immaculate condition throughout this stunning family home offers spacious, stylish accommodation across two levels and modern comforts including double glazing and gas central heating.

On the ground floor, the property enters via a welcoming entrance hallway providing access to a bright and spacious lounge with its feature bay window. The homes expansive kitchen sits to the rear and offers high quality fitted units, integrated appliances, ample space for a full-sized dining table and direct access to the south-facing rear garden. A cloakroom/WC completes the accommodation on this level.

Upstairs, the primary bedroom offers a spacious retreat with two fitted wardrobes and a contemporary en-suite shower room. There are a further two additional double bedrooms and a single room which is currently used as a home office, providing further flexibility. 

Externally, the front of the property includes off-street parking and a detached garage equipped. The south-facing rear garden is fully enclosed and enjoys sunlight throughout the day. Primarily laid to lawn it also features a sheltered patio area, ideal for outdoor relaxation.

Located within the historic grounds of the former Liff Hospital, the property benefits from its position in the exclusive West Green Development. Residents enjoy access to a children’s nursery, park, and tennis courts, all within the development. The village itself offers a primary school, while the nearby towns of Invergowrie and Dundee, only two miles away, provide a wide range of amenities. Dundee, a major city, offers extensive facilities, and the A90 ensures easy travel to Perth, Aberdeen, Glasgow, and Edinburgh.
