This well presented 3 bed, detached villa provides well-balanced family accommodation arranged over two levels. At ground floor level, a welcoming reception hallway with cloakroom and WC leads through to a comfortable & spacious lounge where patio doors allow masses of natural light to enter. Glazed split-pane doors open through to the dining room, while a further door provides access to the well-appointed modern kitchen. The dining room and kitchen are arranged in an attractive split-level, open-plan configuration, with patio doors from the dining area providing direct access to the enclosed rear garden. The upper floor comprises three comfortably proportioned bedrooms, a generous family bathroom and a contemporary en-suite shower room with feature glazed shower enclosure.

The property’s original garage has been professionally converted to form a highly versatile additional room, currently used as a home bar it could also be configured as a handy home office or act as additional living space depending on the owners preference. The property further benefits from gas central heating and double glazing throughout. Externally the front of the home offers private off street parking while the main garden sits to the rear offering a sheltered private retreat  ideal for entertaining, alfresco dining or relaxing when the weather permits.

McLean Street is positioned toward the edge of the city boundary and is accessed from Strathmartine Road, with the property enjoying a quiet end position within a private cul-de-sac. The location offers convenient access to a range of amenities and services in nearby Downfield, while excellent public transport links on the main arterial routes provide easy access to the city centre and onwards to other areas of the city.
