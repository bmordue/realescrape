Konda, Perth Road,
Crieff PH7 3EQ

Offers Over £525,000

Detached House

4 Bedrooms

Lindsays are delighted to offer for sale this modern build versatile four bedroom detached family home in the popular market town of Crieff. Located on the Perth Road the property is ideal for commuting and within walking distance of all local amenities. The property benefits from double glazing, under floor heating downstairs and gas central heating along with good storage throughout and a partly floored attic. Included in the sale are all floor and window coverings and light fittings. 

This two floor property is in move in condition throughout and the ground floor comprises: Vestibule, WC, hallway with storage, utility with door to back garden. Lounge with lovely wood burning stove and leads to the conservatory, dining room with patio doors leading to the decking. Fitted kitchen with integrated appliances including a wine fridge and a double bedroom with mirrored wardrobes and Jack & Jill ensuite. Upstairs the large landing can double as a sitting room and boasts lovely views and fantastic storage. Master bedroom with built in wardrobes and ensuite with separate bath and shower, guest bedroom with shower room ensuite and a further double bedroom. 

Externally to the front of the property is driveway providing parking for multiple cars with a car port and garage with electric door along with internal WC and a courtyard seating area. To the back of the property is a large, raised decking looking over the stone chip garden and beyond.
