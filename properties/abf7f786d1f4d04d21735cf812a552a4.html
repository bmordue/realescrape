Offers over £140,000

Ground Floor Flat

2 Bedrooms

Well-Presented Ground-Floor Flat with Private Gardens

Set in an elevated position above street level, this attractive ground-floor flat offers a fantastic opportunity for first-time buyers, downsizers, or anyone seeking a comfortable home with its own outdoor space. The property is move-in ready and enjoys a convenient location close to local supermarkets, schools, and handy bus links into the city centre.

Step inside via the secure, well-maintained communal hallway, where you'll find a bright and welcoming interior. The spacious living room is finished in light, neutral tones with laminate flooring and recessed down-lights, creating a relaxing space for everyday living. There's a useful storage recess and direct access to the separate kitchen.

The kitchen is fitted with modern maple-effect units and contrasting worktops, providing plenty of storage and workspace. Twin windows look out onto the secluded rear garden, and a side window brings in extra natural light. Appliances including a gas hob, electric oven, washing machine, and tall fridge-freezer are all included.

There are two double bedrooms, both with fitted, neutral-tone carpet. The main bedroom overlooks the private front garden, while the second bedroom enjoys a leafy view to the rear garden and offers access to a sheltered alcove-ideal for relaxing or drying clothes.

The modern shower room features a white suite, a mixer shower powered by the Worcester combi boiler, and is finished with easy-clean tiling and a mirrored storage cabinet. A window provides welcome natural light and ventilation.

The welcoming central hallway, accessed via a vestibule with two full-height storage cupboards, is bright and spacious enough for hall furniture.

Outside, the property boasts private, enclosed gardens to both the front and rear. The rear garden combines a paved patio with planting borders and backs onto mature trees, offering privacy and a pleasant outlook. The front garden, laid to lawn, enjoys an open aspect from its elevated position. Unrestricted on-street parking is available nearby.

With gas central heating, double glazing, and fresh decor throughout, this flat is ready for immediate occupation. It represents excellent value for those looking for a well-maintained, affordable home with its own outdoor space. Early viewing is recommended to appreciate all this property has to offer.
