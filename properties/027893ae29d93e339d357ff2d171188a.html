Second Floor Flat

2 Bedrooms

Forming part of an exclusive courtyard development, this beautiful two-bedroom second-floor flat has a prestigious location in Edinburgh's Old Town conservation area. It enjoys easy access to all the city centre offers and is within easy reach of iconic green spaces like Arthur's Seat and Calton Hill. Furthermore, the home benefits from modern interior design and quality finishings. It provides sociable open-plan living, a well-appointed kitchen, and an on-trend bathroom with a three-piece suite. It also has secure parking. Ideal for professionals, couples, and first-time buyers, this property is a fantastic city home in a highly sought-after setting. Factors: The development is maintained by James Gibb; at an approx. fee of £130 per month. This covers communal areas, maintaining the courtyard, car park and block buildings insurance. Extras: all fitted floor and window coverings, light fittings, integrated appliances (electric hob, oven, and fridge), an undercounter dishwasher, and a washer/dryer to be included in the sale.
