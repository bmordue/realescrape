Edinburgh EH1 2QL

Offers over £190,000

Top Floor Flat

1 Bedroom

McEwan Fraser Legal is delighted to present this exceptional one-bedroom top-floor flat, perfectly situated within the historic and atmospheric heart of Edinburgh's Old Town. Steeped in character and showcasing a wealth of period detail, this charming property offers a rare opportunity to acquire a home that blends timeless elegance with contemporary comfort. The living room is a captivating space, flooded with natural light and featuring a beautiful French balcony that opens to spectacular views across the Edinburgh skyline. The combination of generous proportions, unique architectural quirks, and traditional detailing creates a room that is both inviting and full of personality, a true highlight of the property. Adjoining the living area, the kitchen is thoughtfully designed to maximise space and practicality. It is well-appointed, offering an excellent range of fitted units and appliances, making it perfectly suited to modern urban living while maintaining the flat's distinctive charm. The bedroom provides a tranquil and cosy retreat, enhanced by elegant windows and a large built-in storage cupboard that ensures the space remains neat and uncluttered. The bathroom is tastefully tiled and features a bath with a shower over, combining style and function in equal measure. Residents benefit from a well-maintained communal entrance and access to a shared garden, offering a peaceful outdoor space, a rare privilege in such a central location. With its enviable position, abundance of character, and harmonious blend of traditional and modern features, Flat 11, 3 Forest Hill represents an outstanding opportunity to own a truly unique home in one of Edinburgh's most sought-after historic settings.
