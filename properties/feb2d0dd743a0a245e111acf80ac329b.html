Semi-Detached House

3 Bedrooms

Stunning three bed semi-detached house in true walk-in condition, boasting a well stocked and fully enclosed private garden. The house has a fabulous location, quietly tucked away in a pleasant residential street convenient for easy access to amenities and transport links. Viewing is highly recommended to appreciate this immaculate, beautifully presented family home. More than ample public living space is provided by way of a comfortable living room featuring an attractive cast iron mantelpiece finished with decorative tiles (please note the fire is not operational), from where patio doors lead through to a good sized conservatory looking out across the rear garden. To the front of the property is a multi purpose room, ideal as a double sized third bedroom, formal dining room or home office. Completing the accommodation on the ground floor is a high spec kitchen featuring sleek gloss units in a netural tone, and a fully tiled shower-room nicely fitted out with a modern white suite, including a large cubicle, mixer shower and wash basin set in a vanity unit. Upstairs you'll find two double bedrooms - the principal room has the benefit of a built-in wardrobe, plus access to a deep eaves storage cupboard with light. Cupboard space is also located off bedroom 2. At the front is a well kept private garden flanked by a driveway. A fence has been erected splitting the driveway, but this could easily be removed by a new owner to allow for further off-street parking space and vehicular access to the garage at the rear. Fully enclosed and child friendly garden ground to the rear showcases a lawn, plenty of greenery in borders featuring a range of mature growing stock and a low maintenance composite deck for outdoor relaxation. There's plenty of space to extend the accommodation if required, subject to receiving the usual consents from the Local Authority. Entrance hall with cloak cupboard, Living room with feature mantelpiece and patio doors to conservatory, Contemporary style fitted kitchen, Double bedroom 3/dining room/home office, Fully tiled shower-room with mixer shower, Upper landing, Double bedrooms 1 and 2, Gas central heating, Double glazing, Excellent cupboard storage space, Part floored attic with light, Private gardens front and rear, Driveway, Garage to rear.
