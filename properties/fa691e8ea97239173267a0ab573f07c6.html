Location
31 Dalmun Avenue is located in a popular, quiet residential area on the edge of Dalbeattie, close to Dalbeattie Forest. Dalbeattie offers primary and secondary schooling, shops, local food stores and a health centre. Walkers and mountain bikers are also well catered for in the region with the town woods just minutes away and the 7Stanes cycle tracks on hand in the Dalbeattie Forest, less than a mile away. The "Granite Town" of Dalbeattie is the gateway to The Solway Coast, and is 4 miles from the sailing village of Kippford, with the sandy beaches of Rockcliffe & Sandyhills just a few more minutes away.

Description
This three bedroom, semi-detached house benefits from double glazing and gas central heating. There are easily maintained garden grounds to the front and rear and a driveway with space for two vehicles. There is potential to extend the driveway further and to convert the large shed into a sizeable garage. The property would make an ideal family home.

Viewing is highly recommended to appreciate the accommodation on offer.
