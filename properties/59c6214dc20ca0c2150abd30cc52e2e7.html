41 Strathmore Avenue,
Ralston PA1 3EE

Offers over £350,000

Detached Bungalow

4 Bedrooms

This detached bungalow has a spacious and flexible layout that could work for families at all different ages and stages. There are four bedrooms and three public rooms including one bedroom on the ground floor. This has been a superb family home for many years and now offers the opportunity for a new owner to come in an put their own mark on the house. Strathmore Avenue is a desirable address in Ralston and this house sits in a lovely position and is only a short walk for Ralston Golf Club. There is a T shaped reception hallway which welcomes you to the house and accesses the lower apartments. As the property is double fronted there is a bay windowed lounge and sitting room to the front providing two useable and flexible public rooms. The third public room is the dining room which ash a window to the side of the building. The dining kitchen is at the rear with outlooks to the back garden and a side door. There is a selection of fitted storage units and space for appliances plus room for a table and chairs. The family bathroom is on the half landing and has a three piece suite. As mentioned, the property has four bedrooms over two levels. Bedroom 1 is on the ground floor and is a spacious double room with outlooks to the back garden. On the upper level are three further bedrooms, two with windows in the dormer to the front and one with a skylight window. The property has excellent views over Paisley and to the Campsie Hills in the distance. It further benefits from gas central heating and double glazing providing comfort year round. The plot that the property occupies is a generous size with well tended gardens to the front side and rear. The are mainly laid to lawn but also have planted borders. To the side of the house is the driveway which provides off street parking for several vehicle and leads to the detached garage. Ralston is a very popular area within Paisley and this property sits in a sought after address and is only a short walk from Ralston Golf Club. For families, there is well regarded local schooling including Ralston Primary School and Barshaw Park provides green space for outdoor pursuits. There are also good transport links via road, bus and rail from Hawkhead Train Station.
