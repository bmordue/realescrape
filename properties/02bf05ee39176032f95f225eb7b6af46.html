Set on a beautifully presented, substantial plot, this exceptional 3-bedroom, 3-reception room detached bungalow offers a rare opportunity for buyers to acquire a stunning family home in the highly sought-after and tranquil hamlet of Gauldry.

With its spacious and versatile accommodation, impressive surrounding garden grounds, private location, fully maintained swimming pool and solar panelling, it's safe to say that finding anything comparable on the market at this time will be a challenge.

The home’s accommodation is entirely on the ground level, spanning an impressive 267m². It comprises: a welcoming reception hallway and vestibule, a generous lounge, a 3-piece family bathroom, and a modern dining kitchen with in-built appliances. The semi-open plan family room sits adjacent to the kitchen, while double doors from the main hallway lead into a formal dining room, which in turn opens into the impressive sunroom. The sunroom, part of an extension added approximately twenty years ago, features patio doors that open onto the south-facing rear garden, offering an east-facing orientation that makes it an ideal spot to enjoy the tranquil setting year-round. A private office/4th bedroom is also accessible from the sunroom. 

The home’s three main bedrooms are all generously sized doubles. The primary suite includes a walk-in dressing area and an en suite shower room. However, the property’s standout feature is the indoor swimming pool, measuring 10.32m x 5.98m, located at the rear of the house. The pool area is complete with a plant room and a separate shower room, and it has been meticulously maintained, having undergone a full service just three weeks before coming to market. 

Externally, the property is accessed via a private cul-de-sac shared with just two other properties. The private garden grounds surround the home on all sides, with the entire plot measuring just under one acre. The beautifully maintained gardens offer expansive lawned areas, along with a mix of blooming shrubs and trees, creating a truly tranquil setting for relaxation or summer entertaining. A robotic lawnmower for managing the lawns is included in the sale. Parking for several vehicles is provided by the integral double garage with electric roller doors, as well as the private driveway and courtyard.

Gauldry offers the perfect blend of peaceful rural living with convenient access to modern amenities. With top-rated schools, local shops, cafes, and abundant green spaces, Gauldry and the surrounding villages provide a tranquil yet connected lifestyle, ideal for families and nature lovers alike.
