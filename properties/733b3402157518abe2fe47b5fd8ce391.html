28 Thornly Park Drive,
Paisley PA2 7RP

Offers over £575,000

Detached House

6 Bedrooms

This impressive, detached villa is located on Thornly Park Drive in the South of Paisley. Thornly Park is considered one of Paisley's most desirable areas and this property stands out as being a unique home in the area. The accommodation offered is formed over three levels and provides flexibility for families at all ages and stages with a selection of bedrooms, bathrooms and public rooms. The house and its ground have been upgraded and well maintained by the current owner providing a home that is ready to move into. The accommodation is currently used as a four public rooms, four bedrooms, a games room and a cinema room but could easily be six bedrooms if required. As the property has an elevated position the views to the front are fabulous looking over Paisley and to the hills in the distance. Accommodation Summary: Ground Floor Welcoming reception hallway with hardwood floor that extends to many of the ground floor apartments. Principle living room with a bay window to the front of the house and a beautiful fireplace which was imported from Ireland. Formal dining room which also enjoys a front facing bay window. This is an ideal space to entertain and is conveniently located adjacent to the kitchen.Great sized family rooms offering a cosy room for family evenings. Bright garden room on the rear of the house with windows allowing in lots of natural light. There are patio doors opening to the garden for convenient access. Exquisite modern kitchen installed in 2023 and providing plenty of space to whip up culinary delights and there is a breakfast bar for casual dining. The solid oak worktops compliment the units and there is a deep, Belfast style sink below the window. The range cooker will be a draw for cooks and other appliances include a wine fridge and integrated dishwasher. Utility room just off the kitchen with further storage units and space and plumbing for a washing machine and an American style fridge freezer. This is a handy space for coming in from the garden with direct access to the back of the house. First Floor Elegant main bedroom with double fitted wardrobes, a bay window and en suite shower room. The en suite was remodelled in 2020 and provides a luxurious space which is tiled and comprises a WC, wash hand basin and shower cubicle. Three further bedrooms on this level which all have ample fitted storage and en suite WCs with wash hand basins. Super stylish family bathroom (remodelled 2021) with a four piece suite including a shower cubicle, WC, sleek wash hand basin and impressive freestanding copper tub. For an added flourish there is an illuminated vanity mirror. Top Floor Enviable cinema room with a screen and projector. This is a great space to get together for movie nights but could also easily be a spacious bedroom. Games room which is currently set up with a pool table but again could be a generous bedroom with fitted wardrobes. The house has excellent storage space in the eaves and in the attic which is floored. Gas central heating and double glazing provide year round comfort. The property occupies a good plot with a South facing back garden. To the front there are lawns and mature plant beds whilst the rear garden offers low maintenance space with a seating area and parking facilities. The rear garden is accessed via a set of gates providing security and enclosing the garden making it perfect for kids or dogs. The double garage is at the foot of the garden and has an up an over door. There is also an outbuilding currently used as a gym but could easily be a home office, treatment room or a space to run another type of business.
