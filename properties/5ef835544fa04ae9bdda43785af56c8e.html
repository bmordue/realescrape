20 Marina Road,
Prestwick KA9 1QZ

Offers over £245,000

Garden Flat

2 Bedrooms

A wonderful opportunity to acquire a true "Seafront Home" on Prestwick's sweeping promenade. This desirable Traditional Flat occupies most appealing "Garden Level" within a distinctively styled 3-Storey Victorian Semi Villa.

Historically the property is believed to date back to circa 1890's, originally built as Semi Villas, subsequently converted to form individual flats. The building has been refurbished including a new roof whilst internally it benefits from a modern finish/specification.

Particular mention should be made that the Garden Flat retains private ownership of the entire lawned garden area directly in front of the property (together with the paved patio area adjacent to the side entrance) also noting the gated access (shared) on the front boundary wall opens onto the promenade allowing one to enjoy walking the seafront ...literally "a stone's throw" away.

The neatly presented accommodation which is contemporary in style is accessed via a garden level private main door to the side of No 20. Featuring 3 Main Apartments, comprising entrance hallway which opens onto the lounge, a secondary central hallway which provides access to the 2 double bedrooms which overlook the front garden (one of which could be utilised as an alternative main or 2nd public room) with sea/Isle of Arran views on the skyline just beyond the boundary wall (the view is more visible when one is sitting outdoors), the dining style kitchen and bathroom are both located to the rear.

The specification includes both gas central heating and double glazing EPC - C. Private car parking is available within the communal rear facing driveway (the access is easily missed, off Marina Road) - an allocated space to the left is provided adjacent to the property. On-street parking is also available. The property is self-factored with costs communally shared between residents (3).

Prestwick is very popular with its thriving town centre, excellent variety of amenities and its sought after coastal location. With Glasgow approx' 30 miles away Prestwick properties are particularly popular with Glasgow based buyers relocating (knowing the value of these style of homes in Glasgow - with no seafront views!) ...those commuting to Glasgow either by car (the A77 easily accessible from Prestwick) or by the popular train/bus service from Prestwick to Glasgow ...and of course local buyers seeking a true seafront home.

Graeme Lumsden, Director/Valuer of BLACK HAY ESTATE AGENTS comments...

"As I celebrate 40 Years in the property industry it is clear to me that every home is special in its own way to its owner/s ...however I confess to perhaps having a soft-spot for Traditional Homes as they illustrate through their longer history that we are merely custodians of the home that we own ...until the next person moves in ...however, what joy that home can bring to each & every owner.

Seafront Homes have an almost magical appeal as the seascape is one that can literally change by the minute versus a countryside view that changes gradually through the seasons.

Prestwick Promenade beckons one to stroll, exercise, relax, walk the sands, take in the views & sea air, to contemplate the joy of living by-the-sea ...and with No 20, you walk through your garden, open the gate ...and it is there for you to enjoy ...whenever you wish.

A wonderful opportunity awaits..."
