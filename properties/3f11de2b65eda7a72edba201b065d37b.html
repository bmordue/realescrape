HALL:
Spacious with both the guests w.c. and a lengthy storage cupboard off. To add to the storage, there is also the understairs space which sits below the wraparound staircase.

LIVINGROOM:
Rear facing with a feature (electric) fire centring the room and the sliding doors which, open out to the rear garden

KITCHEN:
Designed with a homely feel in mind, the kitchen is equipped with wall and base-mounted units, integrated 5-burner gas hob with extractor hood over. Additional freestanding white goods may be available with the sale. There is also the pantry cupboard which is currently set up to house the washing machine and the tumble dryer. An extended serving hatch between the kitchen and the living room adds to the family feel of the property and there is also the rear door.

LANDING:
Granting access to all rooms on this level along with the attic space (via the hatch). There is another lengthy storage cupboard and additional press.

ONE:
Found to the far end of the landing and faces to the rear of the property, looking out over the rear garden. This room comes with freestanding wardrobes.

TWO:
Further double bedroom again looking out towards the rear of the property with enough space for wardrobes, drawers etc.

THREE:
An ideal child's bedroom or could easily double as an office space and / or study.

BATHROOM:
Consists of modern suite, including a shower unit, WC and wash hand basin.

EXTERNAL:
The property features a private landscaped garden to the rear which holds a mixture of flower beds, shrubberies, seating areas, drying space connected by pathways. To the front, there is a shared gate and pathway which splits leading to our front door plus there is also a section of private garden, which is monoblocked in part along with the chipped section and there is an external tap. There is also an external storage area which is lockable.

LOCATION:
Livingston is a thriving town with some of the best designer and high street shops in Scotland with Livingston Designer Outlet, Almondvale Retail Parks and The Centre close by which includes Livingston Post Office and a choice of supermarkets.

The areas around Murieston Water and Campbridge Pond offer lovely walks and cycle paths - ideal for those seeking to escape the hustle and bustle. For the active type who prefers to exercise indoors, Xcite Livingston boasts a leisure pool and teaching pool, a gym, fitness classes, soft play area, and a cafe. Other nearby facilities include Livingston Football Club and Deer Park Country Golf Club.

Education is well catered for locally from nursery to secondary level, including Dedridge Primary School and James Young High School. Livingston is also well placed for commuting with road links via the M8 motorway network to Edinburgh and Glasgow both of which offer International Airports. There is also a good bus service. Livingston South railway station is also close-by.

Extras: All fitted floor coverings, light fittings, wardrobes in the master bedroom and integrated white goods are included in the sale. Some additional white goods may be included on request.
