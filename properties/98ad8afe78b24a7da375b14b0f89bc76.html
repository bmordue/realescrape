59 Forthill Road, Broughty
Ferry DD5 3DQ

Offers Over £330,000

Detached Bungalow

3 Bedrooms

Excellent opportunity to purchase this deceptively spacious, detached bungalow enjoying a prime location within walking distance of all central Broughty Ferry amenities including beach, shops, parks, excellent primary and secondary schooling and frequent commuter transport links by bus and rail, and just a 10 minute drive to central Dundee.  The subjects are presented to the market in absolute move-in condition, offering the very best of central Ferry living.

The accommodation, entered through a beautiful, bespoke stained glass door and set completely on the ground floor, spans to approximately 122 square meters. Internally comprising a hallway, large dining kitchen, utility, 3 double bedrooms, family bathroom with bath and separate shower and a wonderfully bright living room with wood burning stove and adjoining study/snug, the living spaces continue outside into the garden. The bi-fold doors open out onto a large well-maintained decking area, looking out over a substantial, private garden with a second gravelled seating space to capture the sun throughout the day. A partially floored loft accessed via pull down ladder offers significant storage space, with architectural plans and building warrant already drawn and approved for conversion into further living space if desired. A home for the future, the heating is provided by an air source heat pump and 14 solar panels (with 2 large batteries) provide free energy throughout the year, supporting the heating system and reducing energy costs from the grid. For electric car owners, an electric vehicle charging point is already fitted in the private driveway.
