Built in 1858 by James MacLaren, this impressive, detached stone-built villa situated in a sought-after position in Broughty Ferry with views over Orchar Park and the River Tay and beyond. The property has been well maintained by the current owner and retains many original period features including the ornate cornice and ceiling roses, fireplaces, window shutters, original maid’s bell system and original Victorian water pump and bakery. The accommodation is most versatile and would suit a large family seeking spacious accommodation with a high degree of privacy close to all amenities.

‘Bartley Lodge’ is accessed via a long sweeping driveway which meanders round to the side of the property and provides off-street parking for multiple vehicles. A covered pillared porch opens into the vestibule with original mosaic tiled floor, which in turn opens into the reception hall with dog-leg staircase leading to the main upper floor. There are three generous public rooms in this area of the house, two of which are south facing, enjoying views to the front over Orchar Park and the River Tay. The kitchen is fitted with original handmade Victorian units, integrated dishwasher and range style cooker.  A separate staircase leads to a cinema room. The rear hall has a lovely atrium feature and leads to the utility room, bakery, store, music room, tool store, bedroom and bathroom with freestanding bath and original Victorian water pump. The main front staircase has a large leaded window and leads to the five main bedrooms, two of which are south facing and enjoy the same stunning views as the lower floor, family bathroom and shower room. There is a useful kitchenette off Bedroom five which has sink units and plumbing for a washing machine and space for a dryer. Outside the property nestles in a large plot with areas of formal lawn and borders planted with mature shrubs and trees, a wooded area perfect for children to play, and places to enjoy the sunshine at any time of the day. There are also a garden room and a greenhouse. Benefits include gas central heating, hand laid ‘Ekki’ African hardwood flooring on the lower floor. Included in the sale are the fitted floor coverings, integrated kitchen appliances as detailed, range cooker, garden room and greenhouse.

This lovely property offers so much and the only way to fully appreciate the size and versatility of the accommodation is by internal viewing, which is highly recommended.
