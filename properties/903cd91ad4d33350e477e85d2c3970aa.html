The Cottage, Douglas
Square TD9 0QU

Offers Over £115,000

Terraced House

2 Bedrooms

Nestled in the heart of the picturesque Scottish Borders, this delightful two-storey, two bedroomed mid-terraced house offers a perfect blend of historical character and modern comfort. Originally constructed in 1790, the property has been thoughtfully updated over the years, creating a welcoming and practical living space of approximately 58 square meters. The property has a prime location in the centre of Newcastleton where all local amenities can be found. It is also in convenient proximity to Hawick and Carlisle (just a 30-minute drive) Ideal for couples or single professionals seeking a turnkey property, savvy investors looking for a buy-to-let opportunity or holiday home enthusiasts wanting a charming Scottish retreat This meticulously maintained property strikes the perfect balance between historic charm and contemporary living. Whether you're looking for a permanent residence or a strategic investment, "The Cottage" offers an exceptional opportunity to own a piece of Scottish border history with all the conveniences of modern living.
