24 Pitheavlis Crescent,
Perth PH2 0JX

Offers Over £220,000

Semi-Detached Bungalow

1 Bedroom

Lindsays are delighted to bring to the market this 1 Bedroom Semi-Detached Bungalow with potential for an attic conversion, subject to planning approval. Located in the desirable residential area of Craigie, the property benefits from gas central heating, double glazing, private driveway and well cared for gardens to the front and rear.

The accommodation comprises a hallway, dual aspect lounge/dining room with patio doors to the rear garden, fitted kitchen, double bedroom and shower room comprising quadrant enclosure, WC, wash hand basin and heated towel rail. Accessed from the hallway via a fixed ladder staircase are the attic rooms, one of which has windows and the other is a large storage space.  Other properties close by have successfully converted this space which would be subject to all the necessary planning consents. Externally there is a driveway with parking for several vehicles and gardens to front and rear. The rear garden is large with a patio, lawn with well stocked borders and apple trees. There is also a garden shed/store.   This lovely home would benefit from a degree of modernisation but would suit a variety of buyers so is sure to be popular. Early viewing is highly recommended to appreciate the location and potential on offer here.

Area

Pitheavlis Crescent is located in the highly desirable Craigie area of Perth, just off the Glasgow Road. The property is within walking distance of Perth City Centre where a variety of shops, restaurants and amenities are available. It also benefits from being within close proximity of Broxden Roundabout for travel links to other cities such as Glasgow, Edinburgh and Dundee. Local primary and secondary schools are also close by.
