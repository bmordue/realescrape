5 Cyprus Avenue,
Elderslie PA5 9NA

Offers over £360,000

Detached House

4 Bedrooms

This extended, detached bungalow has been extended and improved by the current owners and provides a superb family home with a flexible layout that could suit a range of buyers. The property can be used as a three or four bedroom depending whether you would like more public space. By adding the extension to the rear of the house the property offers an open plan living space that is perfect for socialising and opens to the back garden. Cyprus Avenue is a desirable address in Elderslie and is convenient for access to the amenities that both Johnstone and Elderslie have to offer. There is an excellent selections of local shops and businesses as well as a regular rail service from Johnstone Train Station to Glasgow City Centre, Paisley and Ayrshire. By Road, the A737 and M8 allows for swift access to the central belt and if you are looking for sunnier climates the Glasgow Airport is also only a short drive. Accommodation Summary: Open plan kitchen living and dining at the back of the house in the extension. The kitchen has modern, fitted units including a breakfast bar and space for appliances providing the perfect space for creating family meals. The current owners use the space for a dining table and chairs, relaxed seating area, pool table and it sweeps round to a fitted bar area. The bar is fitted out with a gantry, bar top and display areas.The principle living room is to the front of the house and has a bay window. This is an ideal space to receive guests but could also be easily used as another generous bedroom. There is a family bathroom on the ground floor which has a three piece suite.On the ground floor is bedroom one which is a generous size and looks to the front of the house. There are two further double bedrooms on the upper level which both have skylight windows and fitted wardrobes. On the upper level there is also a further sitting room providing another space for a potential playroom or for teenagers to have their own TV room. There is a second bathroom upstairs providing useful facilities for the upper bedrooms.The utility room has space for laundry appliances and is also upstairs. The property has gardens to the front side and rear which are primarily low maintenance. The back garden is enclosed and has a great deck area for enjoying the weather. It is easily access by two sets of French doors from the back of the house. The detached garage sits at the back of the house and has a side door and a window. The driveway leads down the side of the house to the garage and provides off street parking for several vehicles.
