27 Malachi Gait,
Kirkliston EH29 9FR

Offers over £495,000

Detached House

5 Bedrooms

Neilsons are delighted to present to the market this stunning, detached five bedroom villa, located in an established modern development in the popular village of Kirkliston. Close to local amenities and transport links, this beautiful family home has been extended and upgraded to offer well proportioned, flexible accommodation combined with high quality finishings, and viewing is highly recommended. In true move in condition throughout, you enter a welcoming entrance hall with understairs storage and a handy WC, and to your left is a bright and airy lounge. Moving to the rear there is a highly impressive and spacious kitchen/dining/family room providing a superb space for both relaxing and entertaining. There are glazed doors running the full length allowing the room to be flooded with natural light, electric rain sensitive skylights and a remote controlled gas fire. The stylish Leicht kitchen has a island and a range of wall and base units with Calcutta Gold worktops and splashbacks. There is also a separate utility room offering a useful space, further storage and a door to the garden. Finally on the ground floor is bedroom five, which has built in storage and would also make a good playroom or office/study. Moving upstairs the good size principal bedroom benefits from a walk in wardrobe, and a stylish en-suite shower room with an overhead rainfall shower and heated towel rail. There are three further double bedrooms all with built in wardrobes. Completing the accommodation on this level is the recently upgraded, partially tiled family bathroom, separate shower cubicle with overheard rainfall shower, and heated towel rail. There is also a partially floored attic accessed via a Ramsay ladder. A pebbled front garden with shrubs welcomes you to the property and to the rear is a fully enclosed, low maintenance garden with raised flower beds and paved with limestone slabs offering an ideal area for dining in the warmer months. A summerhouse offers an indoor space and has power, light, and an electric fire. A large driveway provides off street parking and on street parking is also available. The communal grounds around the development are factored by Speirs Gumley at a cost of £15 per month. There is also a charge of approximately £40 per quarter for the shared greenspace in the estate which is payable to Scottish Woodland. Council Tax Band - F
