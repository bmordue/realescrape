Semi-Detached House

3 Bedrooms

This three/four-bedroom semi-detached house is a spacious and rarely available family home, which has large rooms, ample private parking and expansive gardens. The south-facing residence also has a desirable location, close to idyllic walks, amenities, schools, and bus and rail links for swift commutes into the heart of Dundee. Whilst the property requires refurbishment, it remains an outstanding opportunity. In addition, it provides lots of scope to add significant value to the home. Stepping inside, you are greeted by a broad entrance hall with two built-in cupboards. On the left is a generously proportioned living room, which offers lots of floorspace for an assortment of furnishings. It also has a feature fireplace and dual-aspect windows to the north and south, ensuring a flood of light throughout the day. A large sitting room provides a second reception area, adding to the home's versatility. It features a south-facing bay window, a fireplace, and a shelved recess. Next door, the kitchen is fitted with cabinets and workspace, housing freestanding appliances. It comes with additional built-in storage and extends into a flexible storage area, which has two built-in cupboards, and connects to the garage and front garden. Upstairs, a bright landing leads to the three bedrooms, which are all bright and spacious. The principal bedroom has the largest proportions and charming wooden floorboards. Like bedroom two, it also benefits from a built-in wardrobe and dual-aspect windows for an airy ambience. Offering excellent versatility, the living room could also be utilised as a fourth bedroom, with the sitting area as the main reception space. The accommodation is completed by a two-piece bathroom, with half-height tiling, and a separate one-piece WC which is sat adjacent. Gas central heating and double glazing ensure year-round comfort. The property is enveloped by a wealth of garden space to the front, side, and rear. Perfect for families and for enjoying the summer months, these enclosed gardens incorporate sweeping lawns and mature planting. They capture lots of sun throughout the day too. In addition, there is a private driveway and integral garage, which has direct access to the covered storage area. Extras: to be sold as seen, including all fitted floor and window coverings, light fittings, and freestanding kitchen appliances. Please note, no warranties or guarantees shall be provided in relation to any of the services, moveables and/or appliances included in the price, as these items are to be left in a sold as seen condition. Dundee Scotland's fourth largest and notably sunniest city, Dundee lies on the east coast, on the Firth of Tay estuary, and was recently voted by the Sunday Times as one of the top twelve places to live in Britain. A historic port and now a UNESCO City of Design, Dundee is brimming with culture; boasting a regenerated waterfront with two nautical museums (RRS Discovery, Captain Scott's Antarctic expedition ship, and the 19th-century warship, HM Frigate Unicorn) and the architectural triumph of the newly opened V & A Dundee, Scotland's first design museum. Further museums, arts centres and theatres can be found across the city, along with a vibrant array of bars and restaurants. A wildlife centre, cinemas and excellent sports and leisure facilities are also on offer. Largely walk-able, Dundee provides a delightful and convenient shopping experience, hosting a wide selection of high-street retail outlets, shopping centres (one with a large public library) and supermarkets. The city provides education at all levels, with private and state institutions and one of the UK's leading universities. Major road and rail links connect Dundee to the rest of Scotland and further afield, whilst Dundee airport also offers flights to London City Airport
