Detached Bungalow

Set in a peaceful and sought-after cul-de-sac within the charming village of St Boswells, 11 St Cuthbert's Drive is an extremely spacious detached bungalow in a lovely location with fantastic open views to the rear. Positioned just a short stroll from the excellent local amenities within the village, the property is well presented throughout and offers flexible and versatile accommodation which comprises: Lounge, dining kitchen, utility room, conservatory, master bedroom with en-suite shower room, two further double bedrooms and a family bathroom. Externally, the property enjoys well-maintained gardens to the front, side and rear, garage and driveway. This is a fantastic forever home in a truly desirable setting and early viewing is essential to fully appreciate.
