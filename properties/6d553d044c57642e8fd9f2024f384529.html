Introducing an outstanding and highly versatile three/four-bedroom detached bungalow, which offers a wealth of space that is beautifully presented throughout to exceptionally high standards. The exclusive property features three washrooms, two reception rooms, and a statement dining kitchen, as well as generous private parking and mature gardens. Set on a peaceful cul-de-sac in Innerleithen, this home must be viewed to be truly appreciated.

Inside, a vestibule welcomes you in, flowing to a hall with built-in storage and a WC. Double doors lead into the living room, which enjoys a large footprint and a bay window for a light-filled ambience. This space is enhanced by elegant decor and plush carpeting, as well as an open fireplace for cosy evenings. Also neutrally decorated, a dining room provides a dedicated reception area for lively dinner parties. It has an engineered wood floor for ease of maintenance and can alternatively be used as a fourth double bedroom (if required). Meanwhile, the expansive dining kitchen has ample room for a table and chairs, as well as comfy seating. It has a high-specification design, arranged around a central island with a fitted breakfast bar. Stylish cabinets provide lots of storage, whilst luxurious granite worksurfaces complete the sophisticated aesthetic, along with the matching splashbacks. A gas range cooker, concealed extractors, an integrated fridge, and an integrated dishwasher are also included. A separate utility room provides a discreet setting for laundry. Located throughout the property, the three double bedrooms are all generously proportioned doubles with attractive neutral styling. The dual-aspect principal suite, with an oversized window framing delightful garden views, has the added advantage of a modern en-suite shower room. Like bedroom three, it also has a built-in wardrobe. The second bedroom showcases the versatility of the property, being arranged as a music and family room. Finishing the home is a spacious three-piece wet room. Gas central heating and double glazing ensure year-round comfort.

Outside, there is a monoblock garden and private driveway to the front, leading to an integral single garage which has convenient access to the utility room. To the rear, there is a large fully-enclosed garden that has a low-maintenance landscaped design and mature planting. It is the perfect setting for summer dining.

Extras: all fitted floor and window coverings, light fittings, a gas range cooker, integrated kitchen appliances, a washing machine, and a tumble dryer to be included in the sale. The bespoke kitchen table, built to match the built-in kitchen dresser, is available by separate negotiation.

Area
Innerleithen, Scottish Borders
Lying close to the banks of the River Tweed and nestled amongst rolling hills, the former mill and spa town of Innerleithen enjoys an idyllic Scottish Borders setting. The town is served by a local supermarket, a library, a health centre, a bank, a pharmacy, a post office and several independent shops including bakeries and a butcher. A selection of eateries is also on offer, whilst further amenities can be found a short drive away in the charming market town of Peebles. The town's scenic surroundings provide endless opportunities for outdoor pursuits such as fishing, walking and cycling, while nearby Glentress Forest Park boasts a treetop adventure course. Innerleithen also hosts its own park and a local history museum at St Ronan's Wells. Primary and nursery schooling is provided locally at St Ronan's Primary School, followed by secondary education at Peebles High School. Commuting to the capital takes approximately an hour by road, whilst Galashiels - located 20 minutes' drive from Innerleithen - provides fast and frequent rail connections as part of the recently re-opened Borders Railway.
