Semi-Detached House

The property is located in an attractive rural setting approximately 1.5 miles west of Castle Douglas and approximately � of a mile east of Rhonehouse. The property is semi-detached to a Category B listed barn building.

Castle Douglas is a popular market town with a good range of independent shops, primary and secondary schools, park with loch, churches, theatre, swimming pool and all other facilities commensurate with a town of its size.

Well presented, semi-detached property with adjoining Category B listed barn building overlooking a paddock which extends to just over an acre. The property enjoys well proportioned, light filled rooms, in good decorative order, spaciously laid out over two floors. It has UPVC double glazing and oil fired central heating.

On the ground floor, the UPVC double glazed entrance porch opens in to a spacious lounge which enjoys a double aspect with an open staircase to the upper floor. A light filled inner hallway gives access to a ground floor w.c. compartment and also the spacious dining kitchen which has windows to both the front and rear. Beyond this is a good sized utility room which has direct access to the front of the property. Upstairs, a spacious, light landing gives access to three double bedrooms and a family bathroom.

Outside, the driveway provides access to the house, adjoining barn and the field gate leading to the paddock. There is garden ground to the front and side of the property.
