Kirklands Cottage,
Winchburgh EH52 6PT

Offers over £449,000

House

3 Bedrooms

Set against the rolling backdrop of Winchburgh's peaceful countryside, Kirklands Cottage is the kind of home that charms you from the very first step. This beautifully extended stone-built cottage offers a rare blend of traditional character and generous, flexible living-all wrapped up in a single-storey layout that's as practical as it is inviting.

Step inside and you're welcomed by a long, elegant hallway that stretches across the front of the home, filled with natural light and warmed by soft wooden flooring underfoot. Immediately to your right, you'll find the formal dining room-an effortlessly stylish space with soft neutral tones, a feature fireplace, and room to comfortably host eight around the table. It leads seamlessly into the country-style kitchen, where green/beige kitchen cabinets, tiled splash-backs, and a mix of grey quartz and wooden countertops create a warm, welcoming vibe for home cooks and hungry guests alike. There is a very useful pantry off the dining room close to the kitchen.

Crossing back past the entrance to the other side of the home, you'll step into the main lounge. This vaulted haven features exposed beams, with wood-burning stove, and large windows framing views of the garden beyond. The decor blends soft greys, warm woods, and inviting textures-creating a room that's equal parts statement and sanctuary. Back in the hallway, continue along to discover the show-stopping family bathroom-a joyful blend of colour and charm. With its vibrant fuchsia feature wall, turquoise textured tiles, and a sleek freestanding bath, it's the kind of space that lifts your mood the moment you walk in.

From the sitting room, step into the elegant master suite-a tranquil, spacious bedroom finished in soft, restful tones with plush carpeting underfoot. It's complemented by a sleek en suite shower room, creating a private haven tucked away from the rest of the home. Just next door, a versatile bonus room currently styled as a dressing room adds even more flexibility-ideal for a home gym, office, or occasional bedroom. To the rear, a handy utility room provides a discreet and practical space for laundry and additional storage, keeping day-to-day living beautifully organised.

Bedroom 2 is a spacious, bright double bedroom with dual aspect windows and an original fireplace. Bedroom 3 is also a generous double bedroom with original fireplace, currently used as a TV room.

Outside, the charm continues with a generous garden featuring a mix of lawn, patio, and planting borders. A detached garage and private parking offer practicality, while the soft stone fa�ade and country views complete the postcard-perfect picture.

The location also makes it perfect for professionals and commuters alike, with excellent transport links placing Edinburgh and surrounding areas within easy reach.

Kirklands Cottage is more than a home-it's a warm, character-filled retreat where thoughtful design, countryside calm, and everyday convenience come together effortlessly.
