Detached House

4 Bedrooms

Immaculately presented and meticulously maintained larger DETACHED VILLA by John Lawrence circa 1975. This property has been extensively improved and offers excellent family accommodation set amidst extensive mature gardens.

Situated within the sought after Crookfur district the property enjoys excellent varied amenities nearby to include Parklands Country Club, Greenlaw retail park, The Avenue shopping centre and access to the motorway network, catchment of popular East Renfrewshire Schools at primary and secondary levels.

Double glazed and PVC front door with a mahogany veneer finish onto 23' reception hall with access to all ground floor apartments off and open tread staircase, deep walk-in storage cupboard, of note within the hall and a theme carried to other apartments is the mahogany hard wood finishes to door surrounds and skirtings. Impressive 20' lounge with aspects to front, focal point marble fireplace, separate formal dining room with aspects over the extensive carefully lait out rear garden, professionally designed and fully fitted (circa 2018) kitchen comprising extensive floor and wall mounted polished veneer fronted units with oak veneer work tops, stainless steel sockets and Bosch appliances to include double oven, hob, hood, microwave and dishwasher, ceiling lighted downlights with additional concealed lighting below wall mounted units, cloakroom/toilet comprising modern two piece suite.

First floor: four bedrooms (3 with built in fitted wardrobes) and modern fitted and fully tiled bathroom comprising four piece suite to include a separate shower cubicle, under floor heating, radiator, ceiling mounted downlights.

The specification includes gas central heating (Worcester boiler circa 2017) and double glazing to include front and rear doors, security alarm system (not tested). Extensive mature private gardens to both front and rear. Driveway to side providing off street parking with access to a double car garage.

Unquestionably one of the finest homes available within the district, viewing will not disappoint.
