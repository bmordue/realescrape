Gilson Gray are delighted to present to the market a spectacular luxury detached villa set in a highly sought-after location. The subject property has recently been fully renovated and finished to a very high standard, whilst maintaining all of its original charm and impressive period features.

The semi-rural village of Ardler offers immediate access to Blairgowrie, Alyth and Meigle. All three towns offer a wide range of local amenities including shops, leisure facilities and excellent transport links. Golf enthusiasts are also just a short distance from the prestigious championship golf course at Rosemount, which is considered one of the best courses in Scotland. Furthermore, the village is an ideal commuter location for accessing Dundee, Perth, Forfar and Kirriemuir.

The Old School enjoys spectacular south-facing countryside views and offers extensive accommodation which comprises: Bright lounge with double height vaulted ceiling and feature wood burning stove, contemporary kitchen / dining room with bi-folding doors, utility room, adaptable mezzanine level, master bedroom with en-suite, guest bedroom with en-suite and a further three well-proportioned bedrooms. The fantastic “Häcker” German kitchen includes an integrated extractor fan, micro oven, oven, steamer oven, full height fridge, full height freezer and a dishwasher.  Practical attributes include double glazing throughout and a gas fired central heating system which includes underfloor heating throughout the ground floor.

Externally the property enjoys beautifully maintained landscaped garden grounds which include artificial grass and composite decking. Vehicular needs are catered for with a gated driveway leading to parking for multiple vehicles and a detached garage with an electronically operated door. The gated driveway features an automated sliding gate with video entry system.

Internal viewing is essential to enable potential buyers to fully appreciate the extent and quality of accommodation within this magnificent family home.
