2 Easter Broomhouse Cottages,
Dunbar EH42 1RD

Offers over £220,000

Terraced House

2 Bedrooms

Beautifully presented and offering stunning uninterrupted views of open fields, the surrounding countryside, and Belhaven Bay, this two-bedroom mid-terrace cottage just minutes from Dunbar presents a rare opportunity. Boasting an elegant neutral décor, handsome wooden flooring, exposed beams, and an oak mantle the east-facing sitting room is cosy and inviting. Enhancing the space even further is a large picture window to the front that floods the room with natural light and offers a scenic rural aspect. Adjoining is a farmhouse-style dining kitchen with French doors opening to the west-facing rear garden allowing for a seamless indoor and outdoor connection. Cream wall and floor cabinetry are complemented by oak-effect worktops and integrated appliances including an induction hob, extractor hood, and oven. On this floor is a design-led shower room and a light and airy double bedroom with a serene westerly outlook over the rear garden and the surrounding landscape. Ascending to the first floor, the impressive principal double with eaves storage enjoys an abundance of light and breathtaking views along with access to a stylish en-suite shower room complete with a countertop washbasin, WC, and towel radiator. Externally, the rear garden with its spectacular west-facing aspect provides an enviable retreat, ideal for both entertaining and relaxation. There is parking to the front.
