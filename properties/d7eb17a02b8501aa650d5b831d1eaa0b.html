13 Belwood Crescent, Milton Bridge,
Penicuik EH26 0QL

Offers over £188,000

End Terraced House

2 Bedrooms

Light and tastefully presented, two-bedroom, end-terrace house, with generous front and rear gardens. Located in a quiet and leafy residential cul-de-sac, on the outskirts of Penicuik, to the south of Edinburgh.

Comprises an entrance hall, living room, dining/kitchen, a utility cupboard, two double bedrooms, a family bathroom and a ground-floor shower room.

Highlights include a modern fitted kitchen, fitted bathroom suites, well-proportioned rooms, and light contemporary decor throughout. In addition, there is double glazing, gas central heating, spot lighting, and superb storage, including a floored loft and an adjoining shed.

To the front is a lawn and shrubbery, with a large rear garden featuring a patio, lawn, established shrubbery, and access to the power store shed.

A welcoming entrance hall provides access to the ground-floor accommodation and the stairs leading to the upper hall. The bright and spacious living room enjoys a desirable dual-aspect outlook, allowing an abundance of natural light to flood the space. Beautifully presented with tasteful decor, carpeted flooring, and an attractive fireplace, the room offers generous space for both relaxing and dining. Positioned to the rear of the property, the well-proportioned kitchen is fitted with a range of stylish units complemented by wood-effect worktops and tiled splashbacks. Integrated appliances include a double oven, electric hob, and fridge, while a separate freestanding freezer is conveniently housed within a built-in cupboard. The kitchen also offers ample space for casual dining and provides direct access to the rear garden. A separate utility room offers additional storage and accommodates a freestanding washing machine and tumble dryer. Completing the ground floor is a contemporary shower room, fitted with a modern suite, tiled splash areas, bespoke shelving, and a ladder-style radiator.

On the upper floor, there are two generously sized bedrooms. The impressive main bedroom benefits from a dual-aspect outlook, creating a bright and airy atmosphere, while offering excellent proportions and ample space for freestanding furnishings. The room is finished with neutral decor, carpeted flooring, and two built-in storage cupboards. Bedroom two is similarly well presented with light decor and carpeted flooring, and further benefits from a built-in wardrobe. The accommodation is completed by a modern family bathroom, fitted with a contemporary three-piece suite and tiled splash areas.
