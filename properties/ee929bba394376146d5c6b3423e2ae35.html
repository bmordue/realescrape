Corrigall Black are excited to present to the market 'Kilmeny' a charming, detached bungalow commanding a prestigious position on Clyde Street, one of Kirn's most sought after addresses. This rarely available bungalow offers a blend of character features, wonderful views towards the River Clyde and the surrounding hills and an abundance of potential. The property is set within expansive private grounds, with two feature ponds and landscaped areas that include a mix of lawn and paving, offering a variety of fabulous outdoor space that is ideal for family living and those who enjoy al fresco entertaining. The driveway provides off-road parking for multiple vehicles and there is also the bonus of a garage located in the garden grounds. 'Kilmeny' is a truly special coastal home situated in an area renowned for its natural beauty and community spirit. The property is split over two levels and offers flexible living accommodation. Grand double doors open into a handy entrance vestibule which offers an ideal space to store outdoor shoes and clothing and a further door opens into the bright, welcoming hallway where access to all rooms on the ground floor can be gained. The bright lounge benefits from an attractive warming stove and feature natural wood mantle, the impressive box bay windows overlook the front garden grounds, creating a fantastic ambient space, ideal for relaxation. The comfortable sitting room is also a fabulous, flexible space for quiet evenings, social gatherings or if required could be utilized as a fourth bedroom. There are three double bedrooms, two on the upper floor and one on the lower floor where the family bathroom is also situated. The substantial dining room is a fantastic space for entertaining and leads directly through to the well-appointed kitchen, from there is access through to the rear entrance vestibule and to a useful WC.

Location

Nestled on the beautiful Cowal Peninsula on Scotland's West Coast, Kirn is a charming coastal location and it offers the feel of a traditional village. The locale is well known for its picturesque seafront line, with elegant Victorian villas and cottages that enjoy stunning views across the water and hills. With its friendly community, scenic setting, and excellent amenities, Kirn offers an enviable balance of seaside living and accessibility, appealing to families, retirees, and those seeking a tranquil retreat within easy reach of Glasgow. Located just a just a mile away is the bustling town of Dunoon and all it has to offer including GP surgeries, hospital, swimming pool, cinema, restaurants, cafes, and pubs. The town's Queens Hall has a library, gym and soft play area and is a fantastic live music venue with regular shows throughout the year. The Castle House Museum provides information about the historical town and is set in the wonderful Castle Gardens while the Burgh Hall is a renowned arts hub for the area and welcomes many exhibitions and performing arts to its outstanding venue. This area is perfect for outdoor enthusiasts who can enjoy paddle boarding, sailing, kayaking and sea swimming, while the backdrop of hills provides further activities, including hill running, mountain biking and rambling.

This special bungalow is rich in character and potential, offering purchasers the chance to create a bespoke family residence in one of Argyll's most picturesque areas and as such we highly recommend early viewing to avoid disappointment and to fully appreciate this rare opportunity presented in this wonderful family home.
