Paisley PA1 3RA

Offers over £185,000

Ground Floor Flat

3 Bedrooms

Set at the beginning of the ever-popular Greenlaw Avenue, this impressive ground floor flat offers spacious accommodation, period character and an enviable location close to the heart of Paisley. Combining traditional charm with excellent room proportions, this is an ideal home for first-time buyers seeking extra space, young professionals looking for convenient commuting links, or downsizers wanting comfortable ground floor living without compromising on accommodation. The property is accessed via a welcoming reception hallway which leads to a bright and spacious bay-windowed lounge, perfect for relaxing or entertaining. A generously sized dining kitchen provides ample space for everyday living and family meals, while three well-proportioned bedrooms offer flexibility for guest accommodation, home working or growing families. Completing the accommodation is a modern three-piece shower room. The flat retains many attractive traditional features and benefits from a private section of garden, providing valuable outdoor space to enjoy throughout the year. Living in Paisley Paisley continues to be one of the west of Scotland's most sought-after locations, offering an excellent balance of historic character, green open spaces and superb connectivity. Greenlaw Avenue enjoys a particularly convenient position, within easy reach of Paisley town centre's shops, cafés, restaurants and leisure facilities. Nearby Barshaw Park provides beautiful walks, sports facilities and family-friendly outdoor space, while Paisley Gilmour Street Station offers regular rail services to Glasgow city centre in around 10 minutes, making this an excellent choice for commuters. Glasgow Airport, the M8 motorway network and Braehead Shopping Centre are also easily accessible, adding to the appeal of this well-connected location.
