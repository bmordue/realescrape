Corrigall Black are delighted to present to the market 'Hillside', a unique detached property positioned in an elevated setting within the charming village of Kirn. This distinctive property boasts uninterrupted views over the River Clyde and to the hills beyond. Offering a rare combination of character, space and versatility, this magnificent family home is set within generous garden grounds to both the front and rear with a fantastic variety of outbuildings and the practical benefits of off-road parking and a private garage. One of the standout features of the property is the flexible layout, which includes a linked self-contained dwelling with its own front and back entrance, creating a substantial second home ideal for use as guest accommodation, an independent living area or potential holiday let. This fantastic property comprises entrance vestibule, spacious hallway, 4/5 bedrooms, two well equipped kitchens, utility area, lounge, sitting room, dining room/ 5th bedroom, bathroom, shower room and study. The property further benefits for spacious decked areas where you can sit and enjoy the fabulous elevated outdoor location with breath taking scenery.

Location

Lying approximately one mile from the town of Dunoon, Kirn is a beautiful village providing amenities including a variety of local shops, a bakers, golf course, bowling green and a primary and secondary school. In the main town of Dunoon further essentials can be found including GP surgeries, hospital, swimming pool, cinema, restaurants, cafes, and pubs. The town's Queens Hall has a library, gym and soft play area and is a fantastic live music venue with regular shows throughout the year. Also centrally located, the Castle House Museum provides information about the historical town and is set in the wonderful Castle Gardens while the Burgh Hall is a renowned arts hub for the area and welcomes many exhibitions and performing arts to this outstanding venue. This seaside area is perfect for outdoor enthusiasts who can enjoy paddle boarding, sailing, kayaking and sea swimming, while the backdrop of hills provides further activities including hill running, mountain biking and rambling. The area is accessible by road but there are also regular car/passenger ferries that run from Hunters Quay to Gourock and a passenger ferry from Dunoon to Gourock, where there is a train link to Glasgow.

Hillside offers an opportunity to acquire a distinctive home in a prime coastal location with stunning elevated views, extensive outdoor space and outstanding flexibility. Properties like this are rare to the market and as such early viewing is highly recommended to fully appreciate this attractive home and all it has to offer.
