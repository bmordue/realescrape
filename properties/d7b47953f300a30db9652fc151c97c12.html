Avant Homes, Plot 17 Thistle Meadows, Off Scott Glynn Walk,
Tranent EH33 2FX

Price £385,995

Detached House

4 Bedrooms

Immaculately presented and spacious, new four-bedroom family home, with gardens, driveway and a detached garage. A unique opportunity to acquire a desirable plot by Avant Homes, set adjacent to a shared green within a new development on the outskirts of Tranent, East Lothian.

Comprises an entrance hallway, lounge, dining/kitchen, utility room, snug/family room, four flexible bedrooms, an en-suite, a family bathroom and a ground floor WC.

Thistle Meadows forms part of an exclusive development and offers a versatile family home, ready to move into and beautifully finished with neutral decor, continuous contemporary flooring, quality carpeting, and a designer-integrated kitchen. In addition, there are stylish fitted bathroom suites, multiple TV and phone points, gas central heating, solar panels and double glazing.

Externally, there is a lawn and driveway to the front, an EV charger and a good-sized rear garden offering scope for development.

A bright and welcoming entrance hall sets the tone for this delightful home, providing access to all ground-floor rooms. The spacious lounge is finished with soft carpet underfoot and a large window that fills the room with natural light, creating a warm and inviting space to relax. To the rear, the open-plan kitchen and dining area is both stylish and practical, featuring wood-effect flooring, stone-effect worktops, a built-in storage cupboard, a central light fitting, and French patio doors. Integrated appliances include a fridge/freezer, dishwasher, oven, and electric hob with canopy above, making this the ideal hub for family life or entertaining guests. Adding further versatility to the ground floor is a cosy snug, perfect as a second sitting room, study, or playroom, along with a convenient WC.

Upstairs, the property boasts four generously proportioned, carpeted bedrooms. The principal bedroom is a true retreat, comfortably accommodating a king-size bed and additional furnishings, and benefits from a sleek en-suite shower room. Bedrooms two and three are excellent doubles, offering ample room for wardrobes, desks or children's furniture, while bedroom four provides a versatile single - ideal as a nursery, dressing room, or home office. All bedrooms are filled with natural light and finished to a high standard, making them welcoming and comfortable spaces for the whole family. A modern family bathroom completes the first floor, fitted with a three-piece suite, stone-effect flooring, a marble-effect splashback surround, and a shower over the bath.
