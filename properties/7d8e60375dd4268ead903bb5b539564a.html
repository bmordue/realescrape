10 Balwearie Gardens,
Kirkcaldy KY2 5LU

Offers over £360,000

Detached House

4 Bedrooms

Light-filled, extended and renovated 4 bed house, minutes from the Beveridge Park and close to the town centre, train station and world-famous Esplanade, has been the subject of a modernising, space-efficient renovation, designed to create a series of warm, welcoming spaces with a fantastic quality of soft light and a layout for comfortable family living with a clean interior finish.

This rarely available and prestigious property is located within one of the town's most sought after residential locations and will impress the most discerning viewer. Formed over 1 1/2 levels, you will enter this house through a front porch which leads to a split level hallway which first of all takes you into a very bright and airy lounge flooded with natural light from dual aspect windows. Moving on, you are going to reach the large, contemporary dining kitchen with family room area. The room boasts an array of nearly-new modern units and appliances, a large feature storage and breakfasting island and lots of space for dining and lounge furniture. The room is flooded with natural light from triple aspect windows and French doors open onto the enclosed, sunny and private back garden. This level also boasts a spacious double bedroom which overlooks the back garden and beside it you will be delighted with the modern, generous wet-wall shower-room with a large shower cubicle and vanity units. Into the hallway, and you will climb the staircase leading to the upper level, where you will be impressed with the bedroom accommodation, comprising a further 3 very good-sized double bedrooms and a wonderfully modernised family bathroom, featuring a free-standing roll-top bath, separate large shower cubicle with rainfall shower and wet-wall throughout.

Outside, you will be delighted with the beautifully landscaped and very private back garden which backs onto woodland and the Fife countryside beyond. This beautiful garden is an ideal escape for balmy summer evenings and ideal for family entertaining and alfresco dining. You will be impressed by the layout, which includes a patio area. To the front, you will find a generous chipped driveway with plenty of parking for multiple cars and a large double garage with concrete floor. There is also a mature, attractive front garden setting the house back from the street.

This lovely property is located within a charming, leafy residential street just a stroll from the Beveridge Park, Kirkcaldy Rugby Club, Golf Club and primary and secondary schools. Beveridge Gardens is an idyllic setting and well placed for lovely country walks and with the bonus of having the town centre just a short distance away where you will find an array of retail outlets, the Adam Smith Theatre, library, leisure centre, parks, beaches and fabulous riverside esplanade. This property is ideally located for easy access to the town's own bus and train stations and the A92 for commuting to Dundee, Edinburgh and beyond.
