Detached House

4 Bedrooms

Warners are delighted to present this beautifully presented four-bedroom detached villa, forming part of the highly sought-after development in Tranent. Enjoying a prime position within this modern residential setting, the property boasts a private driveway, an integral double garage, and thoughtfully landscaped gardens, making it an ideal choice for families seeking style, space, and comfort. The heart of the home is the impressive open-plan kitchen, dining, and family area, designed with modern living in mind. The kitchen offers sleek cabinetry, generous worktop space, and integrated appliances, with ample room for both dining and relaxing furniture. French doors open directly to the rear garden, creating a seamless flow between indoor and outdoor living, perfect for entertaining and summer gatherings. A separate utility room provides additional convenience, keeping laundry and household tasks neatly out of sight. An additional highlight of this home is the box-windowed dining room set to the rear, which enjoys views over the landscaped garden and offers an inviting space for more formal dining or family meals. To the front, a bright and generously proportioned lounge provides the perfect retreat for relaxing or entertaining guests. Completing the ground floor is a spacious welcoming entrance vestibule and hallway with storage and a handy downstairs WC. Upstairs, four double bedrooms offer superb flexibility, all benefiting from integrated storage to maximise storage space. The principal bedroom features a double wardrobe and a stylish en-suite shower room, while the remaining bedrooms are equally spacious and adaptable for use as guest rooms, home offices, or playrooms. The impressive family bathroom is fitted with a contemporary four-piece suite, including a bath and separate shower enclosure, adding a touch of luxury to everyday living. Externally, the property continues to impress. A mono-bloc driveway to the front provides parking for two vehicles and leads to the integral double garage, offering excellent secure storage and additional flexibility for use as a home gym or workshop if desired. To the rear, the fully enclosed landscaped garden combines a generous lawn with a spacious patio area, ideal for outdoor dining and entertaining, and also includes a practical garden shed.Situated within a desirable modern development, the property enjoys easy access to excellent local amenities, reputable schools, and superb transport links into Edinburgh and beyond. Offering stylish interiors, generous living space, and a convenient location, this outstanding villa represents a rare opportunity to secure a family home of exceptional quality.
