Upper Flat

2 Bedrooms

Corrigall Black are excited to present to the market this outstanding two bedroom, modern luxury apartment located in the prestigious and sought after Gerhallow development. With outstanding, uninterrupted panoramic views over the River Clyde and beyond from its elevated position, this stunning upper apartment comprises lounge with glazed door opening onto the fantastic balcony, dining kitchen with a further access door leading to the balcony, two double bedrooms, master ensuite and separate walk-in shower room. The property benefits from private parking, communal garden grounds and additional storage options. Apartments of this exceptional standard are rare, but coupling that with its exclusive location and just a short distance from the main town of Dunoon makes it extraordinary, and as such, early viewing is highly recommended.

Situation

Dunoon is a small town set in the stunning Cowal Peninsula of Argyll and is just over an hour's commute from the centre of Glasgow. Argyll Ferries run a half-hourly passenger service to Gourock connecting with trains to Glasgow Central. Western Ferries run a more frequent car service from nearby Hunter's Quay to McInroy's Point. The area offers primary and secondary schools, a leisure centre, an eighteen-hole golf course, two supermarkets, a variety of unique shops and restaurants and a cinema. There are numerous outdoor pursuits on offer locally, including mountain biking, sailing, quad biking and hill running, all available with a backdrop of outstanding scenery.

The main door to the building opens into an immaculately presented communal entrance area used by just four apartments. The property on offer is situated on the first floor.

Hallway

The apartment door opens into the welcoming hallway, from which there is access to all rooms in the property. The hall gives you the first glimpse of the quality of this apartment, which then continues throughout the rest of this immaculate home. The Karndean flooring that runs into the kitchen and dining area provides a warm, homely feel. A large storage cupboard houses the boiler and offers ample space for jackets and shoes, household equipment and much more.

Lounge

This spacious room has expansive dual aspect windows which offer the most outstanding panoramic views out to the River Clyde. The view will offer an ever-changing scene of colours and boats. From the lounge there is access to the balcony area which provides a wonderful space to sit and enjoy the outdoors in this special location. The bright lounge which is flooded with natural light creates a fantastic space to relax and it also benefits from a feature fire, adding to the ambience of the room.

Kitchen

The elegant and timeless kitchen comprises floor standing and wall mounted cupboards, integrated oven, gas hobs, microwave, extractor fan and has space for an American-style fridge freezer. A stainless-steel sink sits under a window which offers more stunning views out to the sea. The kitchen is open plan to the dining area creating a beautiful space that is ideal for meals and entertaining.

Dining Area

The area has a window to the side of the property that provides a breathtaking sea backdrop to enjoy when entertaining. The space also has a door to the balcony, ideal for sitting out taking in the amazing scenery while enjoying a morning coffee or post-dinner drinks. Cabinets which match the kitchen offer extra storage and there is ample room for a good sized dining table and chairs.

Bedroom 1

This double bedroom has a good amount of storage space provided by a large built-in cupboard. A window looks out over green land at the back of the property.

Master/Bedroom 2

The main double bedroom has dual-aspect windows flooding the room with natural light. One window faces out towards the sea, offering a delightful view to wake up to. Large built-in wardrobes provide a massive amount of storage space and the room also benefits from an en-suite shower room.

En-suite

The en-suite comprises wash hand basin, heated towel rail, walk-in shower and toilet. Built-in storage gives space for toiletries and there is an integrated mirror with lighting.

Shower Room

The main shower room has been designed as a modern wet room and comprises wash hand basin, walk-in shower, heated towel rail and toilet. Like the rest of the property the bathroom has a generous amount of storage space. There is a Velux window which keeps the room bright and fresh.

Garden

The apartment has a share of a communal garden which is mostly laid to lawn and an external storage area. The garden has beautiful bursts of colour from various plants, bushes and shrubs.
