Offers over £385,000

Detached Bungalow

4 Bedrooms

This beautiful, detached chalet bungalow is in superb condition and nestled in small group for only three houses off a private road. The property has excellent parking facilities at the front of the house in the courtyard and internally provides spacious and flexible accommodation with bedrooms on the upper and lower levels. There is an inviting reception hallway that brings you into the property and gives the first welcoming feeling of this home. Immediately on the left is a bright and spacious living room with a wood burning stove for cosy evenings with family and guests by the fire. The fabulous dining kitchen is adjacent to the living room and is a superb space for creating culinary delights and entertaining. There are French doors to the back garden from the dining area and the kitchen itself has a central island and plenty of fitted storage units. Appliances include a range cooker, dishwasher and space for an American style fridge freezer. Laundry goods are currently house in the extremely useful laundry room but there is also the potential for them to be plumbed into the kitchen. The family bathroom is on the ground floor and has a three piece suite with an over the bath shower. There are two double bedrooms on the ground level as well, but these could be additional public rooms if four bedrooms were not required. On the upper level there are two further bedrooms accessed from the bright landing. The principle bedroom is a great size and has a dressing room and en suite shower room with a skylight window. The property further benefits from gas central heating and double glazing. There is a detached garage at the side on the house with the laundry room attached at the rear. The front courtyard is a large, low maintenance space ideal for parking and turning space. To the rear there is an enclosed garden which is mainly laid to lawn but also has a patio which is ideal for alfresco dining.
