Detached House

5 Bedrooms

Striking, light-filled, 5/6 bed executive detached villa with double detached garage, 2 en-suites and dressing-room, nestled in a delightful and highly sought-after estate in NW Kirkcaldy.

This lovely family property is both spacious and pristine throughout, with lower floor accommodation comprising a bright and airy lounge with large bay window to front, and features a striking wall-mounted electric fire and double opening leading to the wonderfully contemporary dining kitchen with its array of modern units and appliances, lots of space for free-standing dining furniture, plus bi-fold doors leading to the beautifully presented, private back garden. On this level you will also find a second bright public room with dual aspect windows, including a bay window to the front, currently used as a family room with study area. This is a versatile room which could be used as a guest bedroom if preferred. Lower floor accommodation is completed with a utility room off the dining kitchen and a stunning WC / cloaks. Moving upstairs, you will be impressed with five good sized bedrooms including a master bedroom with French doors leading to a Juliette balcony, a dressing-room and beautiful en-suite shower-room. Two further double bedrooms with fitted wardrobes also boast a lovely shared Jack-And-Jill en-suite shower-room. There is a further beautifully presented double bedroom with fitted wardrobes and an additional single bedroom / study with more storage space. The accommodation is completed with a stunning, fully-tiled family bathroom. There is plenty of storage space throughout and the property has been exceptionally well maintained and upgraded throughout. Outside, you will find a good-sized driveway leading to the detached double garage and an easily maintained front garden. To the rear there is a very attractive, mature and sunny back garden with patio area. This lovely garden is an ideal spot for children to play, adult leisure time, alfresco dining and outdoor entertaining.

This lovely Estate in NW Kirkcaldy is ideally located within a very sought after residential development with good amenities close-by, including schools. It's also very close to Fife Central Retail Park and the A92 for easy commuting. The town centre is readily accessible, with its main line train station, theatre, library, leisure centre, beaches, parks, shops, restaurants and pubs.
