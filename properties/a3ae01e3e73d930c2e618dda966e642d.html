Moorhead House, King Street & Cowgate,
Dundee DD1 2JD

From £180,000

Ground / Basement Flat

2 Bedrooms

Thorntons are pleased to offer four newly created apartments in a beautiful, listed building 'Moorhead House' near Dundee City Centre. The conversion of the former offices is within a B-listed building to create ten flatted dwellings spread over three floors. The apartments are the last remaining units located on the lower ground floor level. Two of the flats 3 and 4 access points will be served by the existing external staircase at the northwest façade from King Street with the other two flats 1 and 2 being accessed via a gated access from South Cowgate.  This apartment has its own area of attractive courtyard garden to the front.

The building's conversion has created apartments that are finished to a high standard with newly fitted kitchens and bathrooms heating is provided by electric heating backed up by double-glazed period-style sash and case windows. Extras included are kitchen appliances and floor coverings throughout.

Moorhead House is located on the doorstep of Dundee City Centre and all amenities Dundee is very diverse with a mixture of culture, history and education. There are two excellent universities Dundee and Abertay, along with Ninewells Teaching Hospital. There are a great variety of bars, restaurants and amenities. Dundee benefits from excellent transport links with a mainline railway, airport and the A90 trunk road from Edinburgh to Aberdeen.
