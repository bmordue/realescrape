30 Park View,
Strathaven ML10 6NW

Offers over £110,000

Top Floor Flat

2 Bedrooms

Welcome to 30 Park View, a charming top floor flat situated in a three-storey block within a popular residential development in Strathaven, South Lanarkshire.

This delightful property offers a bright and welcoming lounge, perfect for relaxation and entertaining. It features two spacious double bedrooms, each equipped with built-in storage space, ensuring ample room for all your belongings. The well-appointed kitchen provides a functional space for cooking, while the bathroom offers comfort and convenience. The hallway boasts two additional storage cupboards, enhancing the overall practicality of the home. This property also features a large floored loft area, fit with lighting and a fold down ladder. Outside, you'll find a low maintenance communal patio area that overlooks the Powmillon Burn and the John Hastie playing fields of the picturesque Strathaven Park, providing a scenic backdrop.

This property is an ideal blend of comfort, convenience, and location, making it a perfect choice for first time buyers, or those downsizing.

Location and nearby amenities:
Explore the charming town center in the bustling town of Strathaven, which provides a variety of local amenities that cater for most day-to-day life with its array of shops, restaurants, and cafes. Strathaven also boasts sought-after schools, including Strathaven Academy, and a variety of Primary Schools. Venture into the surrounding countryside for scenic walks and outdoor adventures. With excellent transport links nearby, including bus routes and major roadways, commuting to nearby towns and cities is effortless.

Recreational facilities within the town include the prestigious Strathaven Golf Club, a Leisure Centre, Strathaven Rugby Club and the award winning Strathaven Park featuring a boating pond, tennis courts, football pitches, bowling and putting greens, children's play areas and a miniature railway.
