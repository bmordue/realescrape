Meadowbank EH8 7AB

Offers over £350,000

Maindoor Flat

3 Bedrooms

Welcome to this delightful three-bedroom main-door flat located on Wolseley Terrace in the highly desirable area of Meadowbank, Edinburgh. This property perfectly blends traditional elegance with modern comforts, offering a truly exceptional living experience. As you walk through the front door into the main hallway, you are immediately greeted by the inviting living room on your left. This large, light-filled space features high ceilings with intricate cornicing and a double-glazed bay window, creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere perfect for relaxing or entertaining. Continuing down the hall, you reach the heart of the home - a recently fitted, modern kitchen. This stylish and functional space is equipped with an induction hob, oven, ample countertop space, and sleek units. The kitchen is spacious enough to comfortably fit a dining table, making it ideal for family meals and gatherings. Off the kitchen, there is a sought-after utility room, providing additional convenience and storage. From here, a door leads out to the private rear garden. The principal bedroom is situated at the front of the property and benefits from an elegant cloakroom. Towards the rear of the flat, you'll find two smaller double bedrooms, one of which is currently outfitted as a home office, offering flexibility to suit your needs. Additionally, the property features a box room, which is currently utilised as a walk-in wardrobe, and a well-appointed main family bathroom with a three-piece suite. One of the standout features of this flat is the private rear garden-a highly sought-after amenity in this area. This sun-catching outdoor space boasts a charming summerhouse, providing the perfect retreat for relaxation or outdoor entertaining. This charming flat on Wolseley Terrace combines classic architectural details with modern upgrades, offering a comfortable and stylish home in a prime Edinburgh location.
