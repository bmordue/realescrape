Detached House

5 Bedrooms

This impeccably executed, large FIVE bed detached villa with garage is set over 3 floors and offers fabulous, spacious and versatile accommodation for the growing family. Set in the modern and ideally located Ostlers Way estate in the highly sought after NW area of Kirkcaldy, this is a lovely family home that boasts a large, bright and airy open plan social lounge / dining / kitchen area which overlooks and leads to the sunny, enclosed back garden. The contemporary dining kitchen features an array of high quality units with co-ordinating worktops and an array of integral appliances and plenty of space for free standing furniture. French doors open onto the back garden. The lower floor also benefits from a utility room and a handy WC / cloaks. Moving on to the first floor, you will be delighted with the 3 generous sized bedrooms, all with fitted wardrobes. The master bedroom on this level features a dressing area and a lovely en-suite shower room. There is also an attractive and good sized, pristine family bathroom with separate shower cubicle. On the top floor you will find an additional 2 bright and spacious double bedrooms (also with fitted wardrobes) and another immaculately presented shower room. There is plenty storage space throughout this house, plus an attic. Outside, the front garden is mono-blocked for ease of maintenance and additional parking and there is also a good sized garage. The sunny, enclosed back garden is laid mainly to lawn with patio area and there is a shed to the side. The garden is an ideal haven for outdoor entertaining and dining al fresco. This is a beautifully presented home which offers excellent accommodation for the growing family.

Located on the west side of the bustling Fife town of Kirkcaldy, this lovely family home is located in Ostlers Way and can be found less than half a mile from the A92, providing quick and easy access to north and south bound routes. It is also a stroll from the Fife Central Retail Park with an excellent selection of retail outlets. Kirkcaldy railway station is a short distance off with direct rail services to Edinburgh, Aberdeen and London. Edinburgh Airport is also within 20 miles by car. There are excellent amenities in Kirkcaldy, including the Fife Central Retail Park which is a short stroll from this house and the bustling town centre is a short distance off, with retail shops, cafes, restaurants, the esplanade, beach, parks, library, leisure centre and museum.
