Rose Cottage, Tynehead, Pathhead,
Midlothian EH37 5XS

Offers over £199,995

Detached Bungalow

2 Bedrooms

An excellent opportunity has arisen to purchase this charming, detached bungalow set on a large corner plot with extensive gardens grounds and off-street parking, enjoying a peaceful rural setting amongst rolling countryside, yet enjoying the convenient of the A68 and A7 nearby with local amenities available in neighbouring Pathhead. The property, which would now benefit from some modernisation/upgrading, offer excellent potential and will undoubtedly appeal to a wide variety of buyers, particularly those with a vision to upgrade or develop to their own particular specification, subject to the relevant permissions being obtained. The accommodation comprises; entrance hallway with hatch to the spacious attic. There is a twin windowed lounge with leafy backdrop and features a centrepiece stove, display recess with shelving and cupboard below. Located off the lounge is the kitchen/diner with triple windows to rear overlooking the gardens. There are ample wall and base units, worktops incorporating the sink unit and built-in storage cupboard. A sizeable and useful porch provides additional storage space, houses the boiler with windows to front and side providing good natural light. This is used as the main entrance by the current owner. There are two generous double bedrooms and a bathroom with white suite and electric shower. Further benefits include LPG heating. The property is set on an enviable corner plot with private off-street parking. It should be noted that an opportunity may be available for equine grazing in one of the adjacent fields (available by separate negotiation via the land owner). Council Tax Band - B
