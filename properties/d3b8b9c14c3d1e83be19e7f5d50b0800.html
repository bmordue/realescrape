Detached Bungalow

3 Bedrooms

Sumptuous 3 bed detached bungalow with integral double garage, stunning private landscaped garden with garden room and occupying a well-placed corner plot on the coveted Lochty Meadows Development.

This spacious and prestigious bungalow is in pristine condition throughout and will delight the most discerning viewer. The quality of the finishings in this wonderful house mean that you must view to fully apprciate all that is on offer with this splendid forever-home. The property has been upgraded to a very high specification, with CCTV; garden room with power; part floored and lined attic which is also fitted with raised timber joists which will allow conversion to living accommodation (subject to obtaining any necessary consents from Fife Council); solar panels; whole house integral vacuum system; zoned underfloor heating throughout; Karndean flooring throughout and wooden window shutters, plus remote control roller blinds. The house also boasts quality oak doors and fittings throughout. These excellent contemporary interventions combine to make this a modern, elegant and superbly comfortable home.

The hub of this lovely home is the exceptionally high quality dining kitchen, with an array of high quality units, Neff integrated appliances, granite worktops and a fabulous walk-in pantry cupboard. There is plenty of floor-space for free-standing dining furniture and French doors open onto the private, sunny garden. There is a good sized utility room off the kitchen with plenty space for service-room appliances and a door leading to the side garden, as well as another door to the large integral double garage. Moving on, you will again be impressed with the large, bright and airy formal lounge which is flooded with natural light from its triple bay window formation. Next you will move through to the three impressive double bedrooms, including a splendid master bedroom with fitted wardrobes and a luxury en-suite shower-room. The two further double bedrooms also offer lots of floorspace, with fitted wardrobes and bedroom three currently used as a home-office. The accommodation is completed with the contemporary tiled family bathroom which also boasts an overhead mains shower and glass screen. Externally, there is a private, sunny and pristine back garden with summer house, ornate landscaping, pergola and patio area, making it the ideal spot for alfresco dining and entertaining friends and family. There is also a lovely side garden area, plus an attractive and easily maintainted chipped front garden, as well as a large integral double garage and a generous driveway affording parking for multiple cars. As would be expected, there is full gas central heating and double glazing throughout.

This substantial property is nestled in a particularly accessible, central location for the commuter. Thornton is set between Glenrothes and Kirkcaldy and the village itself benefits from nursery and primary education facilities, whilst falling in the catchment area for Auchmuty High School in Glenrothes. There are private schools within commuting distance, including Dollar, Edinburgh, Dundee and St Andrews. The village itself boasts very good local amenities including shops and cafes, plus a golf course and public park. The property is a short drive from Fife Central Retail Park in Kirkcaldy as well as an excellent variety of specialist shopping centres, shops and retail parks in nearby Fife towns and slightly further afield in Perth, Dundee and Edinburgh. There is an extensive recreation facility a five minute drive away at the Glenrothes Michael Woods Sports Centre and another in Kirkcaldy town centre. The surrounding Fife countryside is ideal for outdoor pursuits including Cluny Clays Outdoor Activity Centre, the Lomond Hills Regional Park, Balbirnie Park in Markinch, Riverside Park, Glenrothes and Beveridge and Ravenscraig Parks in Kirkcaldy. For entertainment the Adam Smith Theatre is nearby in Kirkcaldy and The Rothes Centre is in Glenrothes, as well as a Museum in Kirkcaldy. A wide variety of local water sports is available in nearby East Neuk coastal towns and the historic village of Falkland, once home to Mary Queen of Scots, is just a fifteen minute drive away. There is an abundance of Golf Courses nearby including Balbirnie, Kirkcaldy, Leven and further afield towards St Andrews, home of golf. The property is well placed for transportation routes with very quick and easy access to the motorway system allowing major towns in Fife, Dundee, Edinburgh, Perth and beyond to be reached within a comfortable drive. There is a local extensive bus service across Fife and beyond. Thornton benefits from its own Railway Station, connecting it to Edinburgh, Dundee and Aberdeen. Edinburgh Airport is accessible by motorway and a mere thirty five minute drive away.
