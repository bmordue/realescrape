Perth PH2 6RD

Offers over £325,000

Detached Bungalow

3 Bedrooms

McEwan Fraser Legal is delighted to offer a part exchange, ask us for details.

This charming detached bungalow, dating back to approximately 1935 and thoughtfully extended around 2007, offers a spacious and flexible living space ideal for a range of buyers. Upon entry, a welcoming vestibule and hallway lead into the main accommodation, where a comfortable living room and a dedicated dining room provide versatile spaces for both relaxing and entertaining. The well-equipped, and modern, kitchen offers ample storage and workspace, while a beautifully fitted utility room adds further convenience. The home includes three well-proportioned bedrooms, the principle bedroom has built-in wardrobes and storage, and there is a modern shower room. A new walk-in shower is currently being installed for ease of access.

Outside, the property boasts a double car garage, featuring an electric up-and-over door as well as a PVC side door for easy access. Set under a pitched, slate-clad roof, the garage adds substantial practicality. With its blend of character and modern convenience, this lovely bungalow is ready to welcome its new owners. Externally, the gardens are a delight and there is an impressive driveway to the front and stunning new gates that add kerb appeal to this desirable home.
