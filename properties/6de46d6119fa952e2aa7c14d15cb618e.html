19a Townsend Crescent,
KIRKCALDY KY1 1DN

Offers in excess of £445,000

Semi-Detached House

5 Bedrooms

Prestigious and wonderfully elegant 5 bed semi-detached period property which has been the subject of sensitive modernisation, cleverly woven with the timeless original features including stunning staircases, ornate cornicing and awesome fireplaces. This spacious, impressive house is spread over 2 1/2 levels and benefits from its own garage, driveway and private, landscaped gardens. Many rooms in this unique property are blessed with partial sea-views and the house is ideally located for amenities and commuting.

You will be impressed with the generous accommodation on offer, including a bright and airy conservatory and a stunning lounge which is flooded with natural light from dual aspect windows including a bay window formation. This room features partial sea-views and enjoys a feature fireplace. The dining-room is also light-filled from 2 windows and features a further stunning ornate fireplace. You will be delighted with the space offered in the contemporary breakfasting kitchen which benefits from an array of quality units, granite worktops and a Belfast sink. This room has plenty of space for free-standing furniture and there is also a separate utility / laundry room. This charming family home features 5 versatile, large, light-filled double bedrooms which could easily be used for a range of different purposes depending on owner requirements, with each room providing lots of space, a variety of views and unique features. Bedrooms 1 and 4 also uniquely enjoy generous sitting / study areas. You will not fail to be impressed with the large, bright, contemporary bathroom with roll-top bath and separate shower cubicle. The room also features partial subway tiling. In addition, there is a charming lower-floor WC / cloaks.

Outside, the property is reached via a communal drive leading a private driveway with room for multiple cars to park, as well as a detached garage. This wonderful family home is completed with a sunny, mature, enclosed (walled and timber fence) landscaped garden which is an ideal spot for alfresco dining and family and friends entertaining, or simply a peaceful haven to relax in. There is also a private door access to this property in addition to the shared driveway access.

This property is well-located just a stroll from the town centre and main-line train station, schools and High Street amenities. The stunning Beveridge Park is also nearby. The town centre benefits from an array of retail outlets, cafes, bars and restaurants, plus the famous foreshore and esplanade overlooking the Firth of Forth, a modern leisure centre, library and the Adam Smith theatre. Kirkcaldy is an excellent location for commuting as it has its own train station, bus station and to the northwest of the town you will find excellent facilities at the Fife Central Retail Park and the A92 link road providing commuter access to Scotland's major cities. Edinburgh Airport is also a mere forty-five minutes away.
