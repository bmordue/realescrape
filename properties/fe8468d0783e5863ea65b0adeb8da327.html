Detached House

McEwan Fraser Legal is delighted to present this exceptional stone-built detached family home, set within the highly desirable area of Liberton. Offering generous and versatile accommodation, this impressive three/four-bedroom property perfectly blends traditional character with modern family living. The ground floor is centred around a beautiful bay window living room, filled with natural light and providing an elegant space for both relaxing and entertaining. A separate dining room offers the ideal setting for family meals and formal occasions. The family room offers a comfortable area designed for daily relaxation, socialising, and entertainment, while the adjoining conservatory creates a wonderful additional living space with direct access to the garden. The large kitchen is well-appointed with ample worktop and storage space, making it perfectly suited to everyday family life. A substantial utility room offers excellent practicality and is complemented by a convenient shower room and WC. The dedicated study provides an ideal home working environment. Upstairs, the property boasts four well-proportioned bedrooms, each offering flexibility for family living, guests or additional work/study space. The home retains a wealth of traditional charm, enhanced by sash and case windows, while benefiting from modern comforts including gas central heating and double glazing. Externally, the property enjoys a low-maintenance rear garden, ideal for relaxing or entertaining, and perfectly suited to busy family life. This outstanding home offers a rare opportunity to acquire a substantial and characterful property in a sought-after residential location, with excellent access to local amenities, schooling and transport links. Early viewing is highly recommended to fully appreciate the space, quality and lifestyle on offer.
