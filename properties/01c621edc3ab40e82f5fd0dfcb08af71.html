Elibank, Camserney
Farm PH15 2JG

Offers Over £525,000

Detached Bungalow

4 Bedrooms

Elibank is a spacious family home in a peaceful location, with easy access to the local town of Aberfeldy, enjoying fantastic views of the surrounding countryside and enviable sunsets.

The property is accessed through a gated driveway which has a single garage to the rear. The main entrance opens into a central hallway leading to the living accommodation to the left and the bedrooms to the right.

The large dining kitchen is fully fitted incorporating a breakfast bar with pale wood wall and floor units with rangemaster cooker. The considerable living room is complemented by a bay window looking out over the hills and benefits from a wood burning stove.

There are further reception rooms including a study, games room and TV snug. 

There are 4 double bedrooms, the master with en-suite shower room and all with built in wardrobes. There is also a considerable amount of storage throughout.

The garden is laid mainly to lawn and there is ample for parking for several cars.

LOCATION

The hamlet of Camserney lies on a burn of the same name at the eastern end of the Appin of Dull,3 miles (5 km) west of Aberfeldy. 

Aberfeldy is the geographical heart of Scotland situated on Scotland’s longest river, the River Tay and is home to the Birks Cinema and Dewar’s Distillery. You will also find a good selection of local shops, a golf course, community campus housing the library, swimming pool and sporting facilities as well as primary and secondary schools. The area is renowned for outdoor activities including white water rafting, abseiling, gorge walking, canyoning, and mountain biking.

DIRECTIONS

From Aberfeldy take the B846 towards Weem/Kinloch Rannoch. Continue on through Weem and after approximately a mile & a half, directly after the humpback bridge, turn right up the track signposted Camserney Farm.

Follow the track up to the top until it splits. Keep left and Elibank is the middle of the three houses.
