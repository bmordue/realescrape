2 Glenshalloch Road,
Dalbeattie DG5 4DB

Offers over £120,000

End Terraced House

2 Bedrooms

2 Glenshalloch Road is located in a popular residential area close to Dalbeattie town centre. Dalbeattie offers primary and secondary schooling, shops, local food stores and a health centre. Walkers and mountain bikers are also well catered for in the region with the town woods just minutes away and the 7Stanes cycle tracks on hand in the Dalbeattie Forest, less than a mile away. The "Granite Town" of Dalbeattie is the gateway to The Solway Coast, and is 4 miles from the sailing village of Kippford, and the sandy beaches of Rockcliffe & Sandyhills just a few more minutes away.

Well presented, two bedroom, end terraced house offering spacious accommodation. The property benefits from gas central heating and double glazing. The current owner has made a number of improvements in recent years including the installation of a new kitchen, new shower room, new fencing, new boiler and new internal doors. Externally, there are enclosed gardens to the front and rear and a driveway with space for one vehicle. This property would be ideal for those looking to downsize, a first time buyer, or as a buy-to-let proposition.

Viewing is highly recommended to appreciate the quality of the accommodation on offer.
