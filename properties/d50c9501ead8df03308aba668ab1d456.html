115 High Street,
Dalbeattie DG5 4BT

Offers over £120,000

Terraced House

2 Bedrooms

115 High Street is located a short walk away from Dalbeattie town centre. Dalbeattie offers primary and secondary schooling, shops, local food stores and a health centre. Walkers and mountain bikers are also well catered for in the region with the town woods just minutes away and the 7Stanes cycle tracks on hand in the Dalbeattie Forest, less than a mile away. The "Granite Town" of Dalbeattie is the gateway to The Solway Coast, and is 4 miles from the sailing village of Kippford, with the sandy beaches of Rockcliffe & Sandyhills just a few more minutes away.

Traditional granite, two bedroom, terraced house offering spacious accommodation. 115 High Street benefits from gas central heating and double glazing (except where specified). The owners have carried out a number of recent improvements to the property including the installation of a new boiler, new windows to the front, new external doors and some replastering and roofing work. There is a private garden to the rear and on street parking is available to the front. 115 High Street will require some modernisation but once completed, it would ideally suit a first time buyer or buy to let investor. The property has previously been utilised as a private rental property.

Viewing is highly recommended to appreciate the accommodation on offer.
