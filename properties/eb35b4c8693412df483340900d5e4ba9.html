Ground Floor Flat

2 Bedrooms

Jas Campbell & Co Ltd are delighted to market this two bedroomed ground floor flat which is conveniently located within this harbourside complex in Ardrossan. The coastal town of Ardrossan is located 30 miles from Glasgow. Ardrossan is a historic port town boasting sandy beaches with beautiful views over the Firth
of Clyde to the Isle of Arran and beyond. It lies on the north side of Irvine Bay between Irvine and Largs, on the North Ayrshire coast. As the gateway to the Isle of Arran, Ardrossan makes for a great stop when travelling to or from the island home to a ferry port with regular Cal Mac services to the Isle of Arran. For
sailing enthusiasts, the Clyde Marina offers a variety of facilities including 250 fully serviced berths. The marina is also home to Cecchini's Italian Bar, Bistro and Restaurant which is glass-fronted and offers wonderful views. The town's two beaches, Ardrossan South Beach and Ardrossan North Shore, are perfect
for coastal walks. South Beach is lined with attractive cafes and tearooms, and boasts grassy areas, perfect for seaside picnics.

This property has an allocated private parking space and plenty of visitor parking spaces, it also has secure door entry at the front and the rear. The common close areas are very well kept, clean and airy.

Accommodation Comprises - Entrance Hallway which has access to all rooms - Lounge to the right which has double internal doors leading to the spacious lounge diner with patio doors allowing in the natural light and boasting the fantastic views over the Clyde Marina and allowing access to the garden area - Kitchen is separated from the Lounge Diner by a wall with open access from each side. The Kitchen has been newly upgraded, it has a gas hob, electric oven and fridge freezer - The family bathroom has a shower over the bath shower and needs some slight upgrading - The Hallway has two storage cupboards, hosting the service meters - Bedroom One which is of double size has mirrored wardrobes and an ensuite. The ensuite is mirroring the family bathroom and consists of a 2 piece suite and shower enclosure - Bedroom Two has mirrored wardrobes and a small in-built cupboard.

This property would suit a number of buyers including first time buyers and landlords.
