Detached House

4 Bedrooms

Gebbie & Wilson are delighted to welcome to the market this beautiful, detached family home situated within Colinhill Grange. This modern and beautifully designed property offers everything you need for comfortable and luxurious family living.

Upon entering, you are greeted by a warm and welcoming hallway that sets the tone for the rest of the home. The ground floor comprises; a cozy and bright living room fit with an electric roller blind that is remote-controlled for ease, an open-plan kitchen and living room area, with patio doors that lead to the rear garden, perfect for entertaining or hosting gatherings, and an integral garage, which includes a utility room for added convenience. A WC is also located on this floor for guests and family use.

The first floor boasts a large master bedroom, an expansive retreat with a private en suite bathroom, providing a serene space to relax. There are three further double bedrooms, all spacious and versatile, perfect for family or guest use. The family bathroom is modern and stylish, equipped with all the necessities.

Additional features of this home include a partially floored attic with electricity, offering potential for additional storage or a creative space. The private garden with patio and low-maintenance lawn area is ideal for outdoor dining and entertainment in the warmer months. The shed provides extra storage for gardening tools or outdoor equipment.

This entire home boasts contemporary finishes and high-quality fixtures, ensuring a stylish and comfortable family living environment.

Location and nearby amenities:
Explore the charming town center in the bustling town of Strathaven, which provides a variety of local amenities that cater for most day-to-day life with its array of shops, restaurants, and cafes. Strathaven also boasts sought-after schools, including Strathaven Academy, and a variety of Primary Schools. Venture into the surrounding countryside for scenic walks and outdoor adventures. With excellent transport links nearby, including bus routes and major roadways, commuting to nearby towns and cities is effortless.

Recreational facilities within the town include the prestigious Strathaven Golf Club, a Leisure Centre, Strathaven Rugby Club and the award winning Strathaven Park featuring a boating pond, tennis courts, football pitches, bowling and putting greens, children's play areas and a miniature railway.
