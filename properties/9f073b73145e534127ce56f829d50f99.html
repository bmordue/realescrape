Tigh Na Mara, Cove, Cockburnspath, Scottish
Borders TD13 5XD

Offers over £225,000

Detached Bungalow

3 Bedrooms

Set in the picturesque coastal village of Cove, Tigh Na Mara (which means house by the sea) is a detached three-bedroom bungalow with sea views, requiring refurbishment but full of potential to be transformed into a fantastic coastal home. Surrounded by wraparound gardens and located just a short walk to Cove harbour, this property offers peace, privacy, and an exciting opportunity to create something truly special.

The current accommodation is generously proportioned and begins with a spacious hallway offering excellent built-in storage. To the front, the sitting room takes full advantage of the property's position with delightful views to the sea. To the rear, a large kitchen/dining area and a family room with doors to the rear garden. There are three well-sized double bedrooms and a family bathroom completing the layout.

Externally, Tigh Na Mara offers generous outside space, with approximately one quarter of an acre, and plenty of potential. A large driveway to the front provides ample off-street parking, while the gardens wrap around the property, offering both privacy and scope for landscaping or redesign. A collection of outbuildings sits within the grounds; although requiring attention, they could be restored or redeveloped (subject to consents), offering further flexibility.

The property has benefited from some modernisation, including the installation of a new air source heat pump and solar panels-valuable upgrades that enhance its efficiency and future potential.

Tigh Na Mara presents a rare opportunity to reimagine a coastal bungalow with sea views, wraparound gardens, and modern energy-efficient upgrades already in place. With imagination and vision, this charming property could be transformed into a superb home in one of the Scottish Borders' most sought-after seaside villages.

Location

Nestled along the rugged coastline of the Scottish Borders, Cove is a picturesque village that offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and tranquil living. Surrounded by dramatic cliffs and serene beaches, Cove is a haven for nature enthusiasts and those seeking a peaceful retreat. The village is steeped in history, with charming stone cottages and scenic walking trails that provide stunning views of the North Sea. The nearby Cove Harbour, a quaint and historic fishing port, adds to the area's charm, offering opportunities for coastal exploration and maritime activities. Cove is conveniently located within a short drive to the vibrant town of Dunbar, providing access to excellent amenities, shops, and dining options. The area is well-connected, with Edinburgh just an hour's drive away, making it an ideal location for commuters who wish to escape the hustle and bustle of city life. Embrace the serene lifestyle of Cove, where breathtaking landscapes and a close-knit community await. Discover the unique charm and tranquillity that make Cove a truly special place to call home.
