The Robin, Oswald Crescent, Southerness,
Dumfries DG2 8AZ

Offers over £210,000

Detached Bungalow

3 Bedrooms

The Robin enjoys a central location within the village and is only a short walk away from Southerness beach. Southerness is a popular coastal village located approximately 16 miles from Dumfries and 14 miles from Dalbeattie. The village is home to a championship golf course and offers several amenities including shops, hotel and a pub. The Southerness Lighthouse is the second oldest in Scotland.

Well presented, detached, three bedroom bungalow, benefitting from solar panels, double glazing and an air source heat pump central heating system. The Robin has a flexible layout which will suit a variety of purchasers; the master bedroom, with its en suite bathroom and external access, has the potential to be utilised as holiday let accommodation or annexe accommodation for an elderly relative or teenager. Outside, the level, sunny aspect garden grounds are laid mainly to lawn. A driveway with space for one vehicle completes the accommodation on offer. Further on street parking is available nearby. The property would suit retired buyers, families and holiday let investors alike.

Viewing is highly recommended to appreciate the accommodation on offer.
