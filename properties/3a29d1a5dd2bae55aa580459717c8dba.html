Abbey Lodge, Abbey Road, Newburgh,
Cupar KY14 6JW

Offers over £460,000

Detached House

5 Bedrooms

Introducing a three-reception room, five-bedroom, mid-century detached house, which offers substantial accommodation and an abundance of character, complete with original features, huge wraparound gardens, and extensive private parking.

Occupying a very generous plot, Abbey Lodge is a magnificent detached house that offers an abundance of space both inside and out. It boasts expansive rooms with high ceilings and a wealth of charming features. Plus, it enjoys light decor that complements the character of this rarely available family home. Highly versatile and practical, the property has three large reception areas, a sunroom, a dining kitchen, five double bedrooms, a four-piece bathroom, and two WCs. Add outstanding storage, private parking for multiple vehicles, and stunning wraparound gardens and you have a dream forever home for luxury lifestyles. With a desirable setting in Newburgh, the property also offers proximity to the surrounding countryside and lots of idyllic walking opportunities. It boasts an easy 20-minute commute to Perth and a 30-minute commute to Dundee, and is perfect for those seeking a relaxed pace of life within swift reach of major cities.

Entrance - Welcome to Abbey Lodge

Nestled in its private grounds, Abbey Lodge has a commanding presence, accentuated by its impressive size and regal architecture. The front door opens into a vestibule where artistic stained glass frames the doorway to the reception hall. It is a striking introduction, heightened by the hall's egg and dart cornice work and rich wood panelling. A WC adds practicality.

Reception rooms - Three expansive reception rooms

Abbey Lodge boasts three expansive reception rooms that enjoy original wooden roller shutters, as well as their own character and styling. The multi-aspect living room (fronted by a box bay window) is a wonderfully bright space. The cornicing brings additional charm, whilst a brick-framed open fireplace forms an eye-catching focal point. Meanwhile, the sitting room is the ideal space for socialising, thanks to double-glazed French doors extending out into the garden and an open fireplace for cosy evenings in. For special occasions, the formal dining room exudes a sense of grandeur with its wood detailing and evocative colour palette. It has a handsome fireplace and a bay window. It easily accommodates an 8-person table and chairs as well, and is perfect for family gatherings.

Kitchen - Dining kitchen with generous storage

The dining kitchen is as large as the reception rooms, providing lots of space for family meals and entertaining. It features a generous range of wood-toned cabinets set against cream worktops and complementary splashback tiles - a practical design that adds to the light and airy ambience. Complete with the integrated appliances, it has a sleek finish too (six-burner gas hob, slimline extractor, eye-level double oven, fridge, and dishwasher). The kitchen is supplemented by a spacious utility room, two store rooms, and a handy WC just off a side vestibule opening out to the garden.

Bedrooms - Five spacious bedrooms in soothing hues

A traditional staircase rises to the first floor, where the landing is bathed in light from beautiful stained-glass windows. There is storage here too, before connecting to the five double bedrooms. Each room is spacious, light-filled, and comfortable. The principal bedroom has the most impressive dimensions accommodating a wide range of furnishings, as well as a bay window and a press cupboard. Bedrooms two and three also have extra large proportions, whilst bedroom four is a cosy double and bedroom five has the versatility to be used as a study or home office, and has a private staircase to the dining kitchen.

Bathrooms - Convenient bathroom facilities

In addition to the two ground-floor WCs, there is a first-floor family bathroom which is tiled with light decor. This bright space has the advantage of a four-piece suite, featuring a toilet, a washbasin, a towel radiator, a shower cubicle, and a bath with a handheld shower.

The property has gas central heating and traditional sash-and-case windows throughout.

Garden & Parking - A green oasis for the entire family to enjoy

Abbey Lodge has a wealth of enclosed garden areas that wrap around the home, providing a space for the entire family to enjoy. Mature trees and shrubbery trace the outskirts of the gardens to ensure excellent privacy, whilst the generous lawns provide all the room you need for family gatherings and summer soirees. Adding vibrant colours to the scenic environment, there are also seasonal flowers and various fruit trees, including apple, pear, and plums. For added practicality, there are two garden sheds attached to the home and a versatile workshop/shed. In addition, there is a charming sunroom for admiring the garden whatever the weather. A substantial driveway provides private parking for several vehicles, alongside a detached single garage.
